# Wiki Dashboard

Live views into the vault — powered by Obsidian Bases (native `.base` files, no plugins needed).

---

## Content Index

All wiki pages (concepts, entities, misc), sorted alphabetically.

![[_meta/content-index.base]]

## Entities Tracker

Entity pages as a visual card gallery.

![[_meta/entities-tracker.base]]

---

## Concepts by Domain

Concepts grouped by tag/domain. Each tag is a group header.

![[_meta/concepts-by-domain.base]]

---

## Misc Ingestion Log

Articles and miscellaneous content, most recent first.

![[_meta/misc-ingestion.base]]

---

## Stale Pages

Pages with a computed "days stale" column — sort by it to find untouched content.

![[_meta/stale-pages.base]]

---

## Recent Activity

Most recently modified pages across all folders.

![[_meta/recent-activity.base]]

## HTML Artifact

A standalone interactive HTML dashboard is also available at `_meta/wiki-dashboard.html`. Open it in any browser — no Obsidian or plugins needed. It embeds all 426 pages as inline data with real-time search, folder/tag filters, and dark mode.
