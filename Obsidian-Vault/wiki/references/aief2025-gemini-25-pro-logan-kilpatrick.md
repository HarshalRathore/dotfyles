# Gemini 2.5 Pro Launch

**Speaker:** Logan Kilpatrick
**Event:** AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 (AIEF2025) — AI Education Summit
**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wE1ZCmCLP5g

## Summary

Logan Kilpatrick, developer relations at Google DeepMind, announced Gemini 2.5 Pro at AIEF2025. His talk covered three topics: the new model announcement, a recap of a year of Gemini progress, and a preview of what's coming across the model family, Gemini app, and developer platform.

## Key Points

### Gemini 2.5 Pro

- Described as "hopefully the final update" to the 2.5 Pro model line
- Comprehensive performance improvements across benchmarks (Soda on Aider, Soda on HLE, and others)
- Addresses developer feedback from previous versions
- Logan called 2.5 Pro "the turning point" for Gemini — both internally and in developer perception
- Available at ai.dev, in the Gemini app, and via Google Cloud
- Logan invited direct developer feedback via email

### Year of Gemini Progress

Logan contextualized 2.5 Pro within a broader year of Gemini advancement:

- **Research convergence:** DeepMind's research bets across science (AlphaFold, AlphaProof, AlphaGeometry), robotics, and custom models all upstream into the mainline Gemini models
- **50x inference growth:** Google processed 50x more AI inference through its servers year-over-year
- **Developer adoption:** Rapidly growing demand from the external developer ecosystem
- **Organizational evolution:** Two-step restructuring — first merging AI research teams into DeepMind (late 2023/early 2023), then bringing product teams into DeepMind (earlier this year)

### What's Coming

Logan previewed upcoming work across three areas:

1. **Model side** — Further model improvements, more small models and big models, VO (video generation) burning all TPUs, native audio capabilities, pursuit of an "omnimodal" model, video into mainline via diffusion experiments
2. **Gemini app** — Positioning as "this universal system" that unifies all of Google's products, proactivity as a key trend
3. **Developer platform** — Agentic by default — models becoming more systematic, absorbing scaffolding work

### VO (Video Out)

VO is described as "soda across a bunch of stuff" with massive demand — "burning all the TPUs down." It is available in the Gemini app. Early diffusion experiments showed "crazy levels of tokens per second" — a research exploration area, not mainline yet. ^[extracted]

### Agentic by Default

Logan described a fundamental shift: models are becoming more systematic themselves, doing more of the scaffolding work that previously lived in external frameworks. The reasoning step is where this is happening. ^[extracted]
