# Veo 3 for Developers — Paige Bailey, Google DeepMind

Paige Bailey, engine lead for the DevRel team at Google DeepMind, presented Google DeepMind's latest generative media models at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025. She spoke on behalf of the generative media team, crediting their work as the foundation for three model families: **Veo 3** (video + native audio), **Imagine 4** (static image generation), and **Lyria 2** (music generation).

## Key Announcements

- **Veo 3** — State-of-the-art video generation with native audio composition (tokens composed across modalities, not audio added as a post-processing tool). Comparable to Gemini's native audio output. Improved prompt adherence, stylistic consistency, and contextual consistency over Veo 2. Includes human-visible watermarks and SynthID watermarks.
- **Veo 2** — Pre-existing model with creative controls: reference-powered videos, outpainting, add/remove objects, character control/consistency, first-to-last frame interpolation, and precise camera movement control — all available via API and tools like Flow.
- **Imagine 4** — Static image generation preserving realism (humans, animals, objects), detail across diverse styles, and typography.
- **Lyria 2** — High-fidelity music generation with granular creative control over tones and styles. Built in collaboration with musicians including Jacob Collier and Toru Iuma.
- **Music AI Sandbox** — Visual product for music generation (compared to Ableton-like workflow).
- **Music Effects** — Compose beats via natural language, from Google Labs.
- **Lyria Real Time** — Real-time music collaboration with musicians.
- **Research lineage** — Built on years of research including GQN and WALT.
- **Responsible AI** — SynthID watermarks on all synthetic media; close collaboration with artists (Darren Aronofsky, Ross Lovegrove) and musicians.
