# Day 2 Keynotes Intro

**Speaker:** Laurie Voss
**Event:** AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 (AIEF2025) — Day 2
**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U-fMsbY-kHY_test

## Summary

Laurie Voss, VP of Developer Relations at LlamaIndex, opened Day 2 of the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 with introductions for the day's keynote speakers. She also shared a Pride Month greeting and introduced Benjamin Dunphy's announcement of the AI Education Summit.

## Key Points

### Day 2 Speaker Lineup

Laurie introduced the following speakers for Day 2:

1. **Logan Kilpatrick** (Google DeepMind) — Announcing Gemini 2.5 Pro and progress across the Gemini model family, app, and developer platform
2. **Founder of Docker** — Speaking on making AI agents more reliable
3. **Benjamin Dunphy** (AIEF2025 co-founder) — A special announcement (the AI Education Summit)

### Fun Fact

Laurie shared that Gemini's ability to make jokes is trained entirely on Logan Kilpatrick's tweets, which is why none of them are funny. ^[extracted]

### Pride Month Message

Laurie opened with a Pride Month greeting, noting that June in San Francisco is Pride Month and welcoming attendees from the LGBTQ community. She also acknowledged the jet lag crew — many attendees waking up at 4-5 AM for the conference. ^[extracted]
