## Notable Reply Themes

Community discussion on the tweet revealed recurring themes in TV taste:

- **Classic consensus:** Respondents highlighted Breaking Bad, The Wire, The Sopranos, Game of Thrones, and Better Call Saul as universally deserving of their rankings. ^[extracted]
- **Writing quality appreciation:** Multiple replies praised Chernobyl, Band of Brothers, The Crown, The West Wing, and Mad Men for exceptional writing and acting. ^[extracted]
- **Crime drama genre loyalty:** Several commenters noted the strong representation of gripping crime dramas — Narcos, Ozark, Peaky Blinders, The Americans, Sons of Anarchy. ^[extracted]
- **Notable omissions:** Yellowstone, Prison Break (despite its #67 ranking), and Friends were cited as controversial exclusions from higher positions or the list entirely. ^[extracted]
