# AI Consulting in Practice – NLW, Superintelligent, @AIDailyBrief

**Speaker:** NLW, CEO of Superintelligent and host of the AI Daily Brief podcast.
**Event:** AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 (AIEF2025)
**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ehQFj6VmuI8

## Summary

NLW presents findings from Superintelligent's market study of 2,500 self-reported AI use cases, combined with analysis of enterprise AI adoption trends from KPMG, McKinsey, and Deloitte surveys. Key findings include the rapid rise of production agents (11% → 42% in one year), the pilot-to-scale bottleneck (only 7% at full scale), and a pull-forward in ROI expectations despite measurement challenges.

## Key Topics Covered

### Enterprise AI Adoption Status
- Coding and software engineering saw a massive uptake surge
- Other departments now also thinking about communicating with code
- Agent adoption jumped from 11% (Q1) to 42% (Q3) in enterprises with $1B+ revenue (KPMG Pulse Survey)
- Only 7% of organizations claim to be fully at scale with AI and agents (McKinsey State of AI)
- 62% still experimenting or piloting
- Large organizations outpacing smaller ones in scaling

### AI Spend and ROI Trends
- KPMG Pulse Survey: intended AI spend rose from $114M to $130M (next 12 months)
- Deloitte: 90%+ of organizations plan to increase AI spend in next 12 months
- KPMG CEO Survey: ROI expectation pulled forward — 67% now expect ROI in 1-3 years (vs 20% in 2024)
- 78% say ROI will become a bigger consideration; 78% say traditional metrics are falling flat

### Superintelligent ROI Study
- 1,000+ organizations submitted ~3,500 use cases (first 2,500 analyzed)
- Eight impact categories: time savings, increased output, quality improvement, new capabilities, improved decision making, cost savings, increased revenue, risk reduction
- Leaders vs laggers bifurcation: leaders do more comprehensively and systematically, thinking about revenue growth and new product lines, not just productivity

### Human Side of Agents
- Shift from resistance to upskilling and enablement
- Sandbox experiments for human-agent interaction
- Moving past the pilot/experimental phase faster than expected
