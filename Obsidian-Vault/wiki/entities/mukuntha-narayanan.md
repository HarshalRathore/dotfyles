# Mukuntha Narayanan

Mukuntha Narayanan (also known as Khan) is a **machine learning engineer** on the **search relevance team at Pinterest**. He presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on what Pinterest learned from integrating LLMs into their search pipeline. ^[extracted]

## Talk: LLMs in Pinterest Search

Mukuntha presented alongside Han Wang, covering Pinterest's journey from a traditional relevance model to an LLM-powered search system. He introduced the search pipeline architecture, the cross-encoder teacher model, and the knowledge distillation approach for production scaling. ^[extracted]
