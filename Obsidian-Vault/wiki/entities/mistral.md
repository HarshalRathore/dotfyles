# Mistral AI

**Mistral AI** is a French AI lab whose **Mixtral MoE 7×2** (Mixture of Experts) model played a key role in closing the gap between open-weight and proprietary intelligence before OpenAI's O1 breakout in late 2024.

## Mixtral MoE 7×2

Per George Cameron at AIEF2025, Mixtral MoE 7×2 was one of the models that helped narrow the open-weight vs proprietary intelligence gap during the closing phase (between the GPT-4 era and the O1 breakout). ^[extracted]

The Mixture of Experts architecture allowed Mixtral to achieve competitive intelligence at lower inference cost, making it an important milestone in the open-weight trajectory.
