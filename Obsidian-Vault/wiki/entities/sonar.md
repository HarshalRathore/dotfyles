# Sonar

**Sonar** is a company founded by **Tariq Shawkat** that launched a product around **guided agent workflows** at the time of the AI Engineer World's Fair 2026. ^[extracted]

## Product

Sonar's product focuses on providing shared context between agents and humans to reduce the explanation burden. It implements the **ACDC (Guide-Verify-Solve)** framework for AI agent reliability. ^[extracted]
