# New Generation

New Generation is a company founded by [[entities/adam-behrens|Adam Behrens]] that obsesses over building the next generation of consumer and merchant interfaces for an agentic commerce future. ^[extracted]

## Mission

The company builds dynamic, real-time, generative interfaces and infrastructure to serve new agentic needs in buying and selling. Their vision is a transition from static websites with human browsing to agent-to-agent interactions between buyers and sellers through programmatic interfaces. ^[extracted]

## Partnerships

New Generation works with partners including [[entities/visa|Visa]], who is developing delegated authentication — allowing AI agents to use a user's actual credit card through the checkout flow rather than relying on virtual cards. ^[extracted]
