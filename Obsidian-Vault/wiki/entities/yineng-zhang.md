# Yineng Zhang

Yineng Zhang is a core maintainer of [[entities/sglang|SGLang]] and the inference lead at [[entities/baseten|Baseten]]. He has been involved with LMSys.org and previously worked at Baidu. He is an author of the FlashInfo papers on efficient attention mechanisms. ^[extracted] He co-presented the "Introduction to LLM serving with SGLang" workshop at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025. ^[extracted]
