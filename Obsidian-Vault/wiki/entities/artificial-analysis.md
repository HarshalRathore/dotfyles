# Artificial Analysis

**Artificial Analysis** is an independent AI benchmarking company co-founded by [[entities/george-cameron|George Cameron]]. They benchmark a broad spectrum of the AI landscape across multiple dimensions.

## Scope of Benchmarking

Artificial Analysis evaluates: ^[extracted]

- **Models** — intelligence across seven evaluations composited into the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index
- **API endpoints** — speed and cost of model access
- **Hardware** — AI accelerators and GPU performance
- **Modalities** — language, vision, speech, image generation, and video generation

They publish nearly all benchmarks for free at [[https://artificialanalysis.ai|artificialanalysis.ai]], covering over 150 different models across a range of metrics. ^[extracted]

## Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index

The Intelligence Index is a composite score derived from seven evaluations, weighted to produce a generalist perspective on model intelligence. It serves as the primary metric for comparing frontier AI models and is used as the baseline for measuring trade-offs across reasoning, cost, speed, and open-weight frontiers. ^[extracted]

## Enterprise Subscription

Beyond free public benchmarks, Artificial Analysis offers an enterprise subscription for organizations looking to bring AI into production efficiently and effectively. ^[extracted]
