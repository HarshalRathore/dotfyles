# DevDay

DevDay is a platform that has reimagined the technical hiring process for the AI era. It presents workplace simulations as an alternative to traditional coding interviews. ^[extracted]

## Hiring Process

DevDay's approach replaces contrived coding puzzles with **real-life workplace simulations** where candidates:

- Work alongside AI agents with distinct personalities (perfectionist, pragmatist, security expert, junior developer needing mentoring)
- Build features for the company's actual business domain
- Are evaluated on collaboration with AI, handling ambiguity, communicating technical decisions, mentoring others, and adapting to changing requirements ^[extracted]

## Positioning

DevDay positions itself as the solution for companies that cannot afford Google/Meta's brute-force hiring approach (interview 100, hire 5). For most companies, one bad hire costs $20k–$60k, and DevDay aims to reduce that risk through simulation-based assessment. ^[extracted]
