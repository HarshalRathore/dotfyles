# Lexica

An AI company focused on generative models, specifically image models. Founded by [[entities/sharif-shameem|Sharif Shameem]], Lexica operates at the intersection of generative AI and creative tools. Shameem's work at Lexica is informed by the demo-driven exploration methodology he advocates — building interactive experiences to discover what AI models can do.
