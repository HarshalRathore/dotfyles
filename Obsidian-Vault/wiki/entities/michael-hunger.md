# Michael Hunger

Michael Hunger is VP of Product Innovation at [[entities/neo4j|Neo4j]]. He co-authored (with [[entities/stephen-shin|Stephen Shin]]) the book *GraphRAG: The Definitive Guide* for O'Reilly. ^[extracted]

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, he co-presented "Practical GraphRAG: Making LLMs smarter with Knowledge Graphs" alongside [[entities/jesús-barrasa|Jesús Barrasa]] and Stephen Shin, covering the case for GraphRAG, its advantages over vector-only RAG, and the three-phase knowledge graph construction pipeline. ^[extracted]

## Key Contributions

- Co-author, *GraphRAG: The Definitive Guide* (O'Reilly) with Stephen Shin ^[extracted]
- Pioneered the GraphRAG.com pattern catalog — a collection of named patterns for building GraphRAG applications, each with example graphs, descriptions, and Cypher queries ^[inferred]
- Advocates for the "left brain / right brain" analogy: knowledge graphs as the logical, factual left brain complementing LLMs as the creative, extrapolative right brain ^[extracted]
