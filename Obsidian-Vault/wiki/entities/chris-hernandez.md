# Chris Hernandez

Chris Hernandez leads the speech analytics team at [[entities/chime|Chime]], a financial technology company. He has approximately 10 years of experience in customer experience (CX) and about 9 years in the machine learning space. ^[extracted]

## AI Engineer World's Fair 2025

Co-presented "The Build-Operate Divide: Bridging Product Vision and AI Operational Reality" with Jeremy of [[entities/freeplay|Freeplay]]. The talk focused on human-in-the-loop as a feedback engine, the evolution of QA/CX teams into model shapers for Gen AI, and the emerging [[concepts/ai-quality-lead]] role. ^[extracted]

## Key Contributions

- Argued that LLM hallucinations at scale are not just a technical problem but an operational reliability challenge ^[extracted]
- Framed human-in-the-loop not as a safeguard but as a "feedback engine" — each correction is training signal ^[extracted]
- Made the case that QA/CX teams are already equipped for Gen AI quality work because they "evaluate interactions at scale, spot edge cases, and define what good looks like" ^[extracted]
- Emphasized that "without HITL you're not scaling productivity, you're scaling risk" ^[extracted]
