# Alex Atallah

Co-founder of [[entities/openrouter|OpenRouter]], the multi-model inference marketplace. Started OpenRouter at the beginning of 2023 after being "bitten by the AI bug" when ChatGPT launched in late 2022. ^[extracted]

## Key Contributions

### Window AI
Before OpenRouter, Atallah launched **Window AI** in April 2023 — an open-source Chrome extension that let users choose their own language model and inject it into any web app. This was a prototype exploring: "what if the developer didn't even know which model a user wanted to use?" ^[extracted]

### OpenRouter Founding
Co-founded OpenRouter with **Lewis** (founder of the Plasma framework that Window AI was built on) in May 2023. Started as a "place to collect all the models in one spot and help people figure out what to do with them" and evolved into a full marketplace. ^[extracted]

### Multi-Model Thesis
Atallah's central bet: the inference market will **not** be winner-take-all. OpenRouter's data shows Google Gemini growing from ~2-3% to ~34-35% market share over 12 months, with Anthropic, OpenAI, and others all holding significant share. ^[extracted]
