# Open Home

Open Home is an AI-driven smart speaker platform that enables developers to build custom voice AI applications in any form factor. It was presented at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Key Facts

- **Developer ecosystem**: Over 10,000 developers building on the platform ^[extracted]
- **Hardware**: Offering 500 free dev kits at AIEF2025 ^[extracted]
- **Last dev kit batch**: Booked up within minutes of release ^[extracted]
- **Philosophy**: Natural conversation over command-based language ^[extracted]

## Product Vision

Open Home's core thesis is that smart speakers "still suck" — users can barely talk to them, there's no customization, no community. The platform enables developers to build:

- Custom smart speakers in any form factor (talking toys, AI robots, AI appliances) ^[extracted]
- Voice AI applications (games, personalities, home automation tools) ^[extracted]
- AI-driven conversational interfaces that feel natural rather than command-based ^[extracted]

## Developer Requirements

Developers want: open source, LLM-driven, and "fully jailbroken" — indicating a preference for unrestricted access to model capabilities ^[extracted].
