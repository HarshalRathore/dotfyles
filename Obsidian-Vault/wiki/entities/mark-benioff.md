# Mark Benioff

Mark Benioff (typically spelled Marc Benioff) is the CEO of Salesforce. ^[extracted]

## AI Hiring Announcement

In 2025, Benioff announced that Salesforce would no longer hire software engineers, citing a 30% productivity boost since replacing people with AI. Beth Glenfield at DevDay noted she'd "like to see the data on that" claim. ^[extracted]
