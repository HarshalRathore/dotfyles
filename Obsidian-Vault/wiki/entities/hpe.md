Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) is a partner with [[entities/nvidia|NVIDIA]] and [[entities/los-alamos-national-laboratory|Los Alamos National Laboratory]] to build the [[entities/venato-supercomputer|Venato]] supercomputer — a 2,500+ node system using NVIDIA Grace Hopper superchips designed to push the boundaries of [[concepts/agentic-science|agentic science]] at a national laboratory.

Venato's key capability is bringing frontier commercial models (including [[entities/openai|OpenAI]]'s models) onto LANL's classified networks, enabling AI agents to run against mission-specific data that cannot leave classified environments. HPE's role involves the infrastructure and hardware integration required to operate such a system in a classified environment.
