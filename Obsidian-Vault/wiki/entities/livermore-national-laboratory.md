Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory is a sister national laboratory to [[entities/los-alamos-national-laboratory|Los Alamos National Laboratory]], located "across the bay." The two labs collaborate on nuclear stockpile stewardship and inertial confinement fusion (ICF) research.

LANL demonstrated an AI agent capable of designing ICF fusion capsules for Livermore by reading relevant scientific papers, generating hypotheses, and producing optimized capsule designs — executed on LANL's high-performance computing assets with thermodynamic and hydrodynamic simulation tools.
