## Y Combinator

Y Combinator (YC) is a startup accelerator that has become the most prominent pathway for AI-era startups. [[entities/gumloop|Gumloop]] was part of the Winter 2024 batch, and founder [[entities/max-brodeur-urbas|Max Brodeur-Urbas]] raised a Series A round with only two team members, demonstrating the YC model of funding small, high-velocity teams.

YC has become a signal for AI-native companies that prioritize lean team structures, fast iteration, and exceptional individual hires over traditional scaling patterns.
- **AI company signal:** Over the past three years, YC-accepted companies describing themselves as "agentic" increased by 254%, reflecting massive investment and excitement in agentic AI. ^[extracted]
