# Matthias Loibl

Matthias Loibl is the Director of Product and Cloud at [[entities/polar-signals|Polar Signals]], based in Berlin, Germany. He is also a maintainer of the [[entities/prometheus-operator|Prometheus Operator]] and other open-source projects, including the open-source version of Polar Signals' profiling platform.

He has been working on CPU and memory profiling using Linux [[concepts/ebpf|eBPF]] for three to four years before launching a GPU profiling preview in early 2025. He presented on continuous profiling for GPUs at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.
