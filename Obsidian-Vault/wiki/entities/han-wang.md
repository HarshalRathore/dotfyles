# Han Wang

Han Wang is a **machine learning engineer** on the **search relevance team at Pinterest**. She presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 alongside Mukuntha Narayanan on Pinterest's LLM integration for search. ^[extracted]

## Talk: Knowledge Distillation for Search

Han covered the productionization side of Pinterest's LLM search system — specifically how they distilled a large cross-encoder teacher model into a scalable bi-encoder student model using semi-supervised learning on daily search logs. ^[extracted]
