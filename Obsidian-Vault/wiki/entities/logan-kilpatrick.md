# Logan Kilpatrick

Logan Kilpatrick is a developer relations engineer at [[entities/google-deepmind|Google DeepMind]] who works on developer-facing content and communication around Gemini models. He spoke at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 (AI Education Summit), delivering a three-part talk on Gemini 2.5 Pro, a year of Gemini progress, and the future ahead.

## Gemini 2.5 Pro Announcement

At AIEF2025, Logan announced Gemini 2.5 Pro as the latest update to the Gemini 2.5 Pro family, described as "hopefully the final update" to that model line. He characterized it as a comprehensive performance improvement across benchmarks, noting it closes gaps identified by developer feedback on previous versions. He called 2.5 Pro "the turning point" for Gemini — both internally at Google and in developer perception. ^[extracted]

Logan described his collaboration with DeepMind's research team as "super fun," noting that being "on the frontier with them" and bringing new models to the world is what he finds most exciting. He emphasized that they ship "lots of stuff" and are "very early in that journey." ^[extracted]

## Role at DeepMind

Logan describes his role as doing "developer stuff at DeepMind" — working on developer relations, platform communication, and community engagement around Google's AI models. He invited developer feedback directly via email, emphasizing DeepMind's openness to iterating on model performance based on ecosystem input. ^[extracted]

## Speaking Style and Presence

Logan's presentation style is casual and conversational, frequently using filler words ("like," "sort of," "I think") and self-corrections mid-sentence. He structures talks around three clear sections (announcements, recap, preview) and uses humor (joking about Twitter complaints, referencing Ben's intro). ^[extracted]
