# Stanford University

**Stanford University** is the academic home of a prominent research group studying AI's impact on software engineering productivity.

## AI Productivity Research Group

Led by [[entities/yegor-denisov-blanch|Yegor Denisov-Blanch]] (since 2022), the Stanford AI productivity research group has produced:

### 100k Devs Study
- Largest study on AI's impact on developer productivity
- 100,000+ engineers across 600+ companies
- Time-series + cross-sectional design using private Git repositories
- Automated productivity measurement model trained on expert panel evaluations

### Ghost Engineers Study
- Same research group
- Found ~10% of software engineers (in ~50k dataset) were "ghost engineers" — collecting paychecks but doing no work
- Amplified by [[entities/elon-musk|Elon Musk]]'s retweet
