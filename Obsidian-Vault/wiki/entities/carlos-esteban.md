# Carlos Esteban

Carlos Esteban is a Solutions Engineer at [[entities/braintrust|Braintrust]], the AI evaluation and observability platform. He co-presented the hands-on "[Evals Workshop] Mastering AI Evaluation: From Playground to Production" workshop at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 alongside his colleague Doug. ^[extracted]

## Background

Before joining Braintrust, Carlos worked at [[entities/red-hat|HashiCorp]] on infrastructure tooling (Terraform, Vault). ^[extracted] He brings a background in platform engineering to the AI evaluation space. ^[inferred]

## At the Workshop

Carlos presented the core Braintrust evaluation model at AIEF2025, covering: ^[extracted]

- The [[concepts/eval-ingredients-task-dataset-score|three ingredients of evals]]: task, dataset, score
- The [[concepts/eval-quality-matrix|eval quality matrix]] for diagnosing improvement priorities
- [[concepts/online-evals|Online vs. offline evals]] framework
- [[concepts/llm-judge-best-practices|LLM-as-judge design principles]] and best practices
- [[concepts/eval-playground-experiment-workflow|Playground vs. experiments workflow]]

He also co-presented the earlier talk "How to build world-class AI products" at the same conference alongside [[entities/sarah-sachs|Sarah Sachs]] of [[entities/notion|Notion]]. ^[extracted]
