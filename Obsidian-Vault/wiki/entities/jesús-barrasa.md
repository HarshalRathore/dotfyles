# Jesús Barrasa

Jesús Barrasa is the Field CTO for AI at [[entities/neo4j|Neo4j]], where he focuses on the intersection of knowledge graphs and large language models for building AI applications. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

At the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Barrasa presented "Why Your Agent's Brain Needs a Playbook: Practical Wins from Using Ontologies," demonstrating how ontologies serve as an implementation-agnostic schema that drives both graph construction and dynamic retriever behavior. He introduced the concept of [[concepts/ontology-driven-retrieval|ontology-driven retrieval]], where the ontology stored as data in the graph controls retriever behavior dynamically. ^[extracted]

## Core Contributions

- Advancing the [[concepts/graphrag|GraphRAG]] architecture with richer retrieval strategies combining vector search, graph contextualization, and structured queries
- Identifying the **schema divergence problem** across structured and unstructured data pipelines
- Promoting ontologies (W3C standards like OWL, RDF) as a shared, formal, implementation-agnostic schema for knowledge graph construction
- Demonstrating ontology-driven dynamic retrievers where the ontology as a data artifact controls retriever behavior ^[extracted]
