# Dylan Patel

**Dylan Patel** is the founder and lead analyst at [[entities/semi-analysis|SemiAnalysis]], covering the semiconductor industry, AI infrastructure, and the geopolitical dimensions of compute.

## SemiAnalysis

SemiAnalysis is a research and analysis firm focused on the semiconductor supply chain, AI hardware, and the intersection of technology policy with chip manufacturing. Patel's work is known for deep technical analysis of chip architectures and supply chain dynamics. ^[extracted]

## Key Topics Covered

- **Chinese semiconductor advancement** — Huawei's Ascend chips, SMIC's manufacturing capabilities, and the effectiveness (or ineffectiveness) of US export controls ^[extracted]
- **AI infrastructure scale** — data center power requirements, GPU cluster architectures, and the economics of training vs. inference infrastructure ^[extracted]
- **Geopolitics of compute** — how export controls, sanctions, and international deals reshape the global AI hardware landscape ^[extracted]
- **Middle East AI deals** — the Stargate project, G42's GPU access, and the blurring of inference/training infrastructure boundaries ^[extracted]
