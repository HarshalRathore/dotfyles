# Rajiv Shah

Rajiv Shah is the chief evangelist at **[[entities/contextual-ai|Contextual AI]]**, where he leads talks, workshops, and advocacy around AI and RAG. He previously spent several years at **[[entities/huggingface|Hugging Face]]**, where he helped people build RAG pipelines and question-answer systems using open-source components. ^[extracted]

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, he co-presented with Nina Lopatina on the thesis that RAG should be treated as a managed service — the same way organizations no longer train their own LLMs or build their own vector databases. ^[extracted] His presentation covered the evolution from simple RAG demos to production-scale systems, the Contextual platform's three-tier architecture (no-code, developer, modular), and the company's founding story rooted in Meta's RAG research. ^[extracted]

Rajiv has been in the AI space for an extended period and has a background in helping practitioners cobble together open-source RAG components — experience that informed Contextual's approach to building a unified, managed RAG platform. ^[extracted]
