# SemiAnalysis

**SemiAnalysis** is a research and analysis firm founded by [[entities/dylan-patel|Dylan Patel]] focused on the semiconductor industry, AI infrastructure, and the intersection of technology policy with chip manufacturing.

## Focus Areas

SemiAnalysis produces deep-dive analysis on:

- **Semiconductor supply chains** — from design (TSMC, Samsung) through packaging (HBM, Coasia) to end-use (AI training clusters, data centers) ^[extracted]
- **AI hardware architectures** — GPU cluster designs, optical interconnects, and the engineering tradeoffs between rack-scale and multi-rack deployments ^[extracted]
- **Geopolitics of compute** — how export controls, sanctions, and international agreements shape who can build AI infrastructure ^[extracted]

## Notable Analysis

Patel has been a prominent voice analyzing China's ability to circumvent US export controls on advanced semiconductor manufacturing, documenting how Huawei accesses TSMC-manufactured chips through intermediaries and how HBM memory reaches China through complex supply chain workarounds. ^[extracted]
