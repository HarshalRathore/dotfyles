# Arize

**Arize** is an AI observability company that created **OpenInference**, a higher-level SDK and instrumentation convention for [[concepts/mcp-observability|MCP]] observability.

## OpenInference

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, [[entities/alex-volkov|Alex Volkov]] praised [[concepts/open-inference|OpenInference]] as a "great start" in the space of higher-level SDKs and instrumentation conventions for [[concepts/mcp-observability|MCP]] observability. OpenInference provides standardized attributes and values for agent traces, tool calls, and LLM interactions — complementing the [[concepts/semantic-conventions|semantic conventions]] effort by the [[concepts/open-telemetry|OpenTelemetry]] team. ^[extracted]
