## Eureka Labs

Eureka Labs is Andre Karpathy's new educational platform, mentioned as his most recent venture during the AIEF2025 talk. It follows his career trajectory through Google, OpenAI, Tesla, xAI, and represents a shift toward education after years of building AI systems.

### Context

Karpathy is widely recognized as a YouTube educator who produces highly accessible talks explaining how AI and machine learning work for general audiences. Eureka Labs extends this educational mission into a dedicated platform, building on the reputation he established through his YouTube channel and his work at major AI companies.
