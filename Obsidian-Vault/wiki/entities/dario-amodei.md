# Dario Amodei

CEO of [[entities/anthropic|Anthropic]]. His observation that generative AI systems are "more grown than built" was cited by [[entities/maximillian-piras|Maximillian Piras]] at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as inspiration for the [[concepts/gardening-as-design-paradigm|gardening paradigm]] — the idea that future AI UX design will shift from procedural construction to setting constraints and goals for generative systems to grow within. ^[extracted]
