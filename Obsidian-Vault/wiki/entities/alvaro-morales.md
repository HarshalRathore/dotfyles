# Alvaro Morales

Co-founder and CEO of [[entities/orb|Orb]], a billing infrastructure platform purpose-built for AI and SaaS companies. Presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on monetization strategies for AI products. ^[extracted]

## Background

- Co-founder and CEO of Orb, which specializes in billing for AI and SaaS companies. ^[extracted]
- Orb partners with companies across the AI stack including [[entities/vercel|Vercel]] (V0), [[entities/replit|Replit]] (Replit Agent), and Perplexity API. ^[extracted]

## Key Ideas

- **Don't price on vibes** — AI pricing should be data-driven, using real usage data to simulate the impact of pricing changes before rolling them out. ^[extracted]
- **Three frameworks for AI pricing:** (1) Direct vs indirect monetization decision, (2) Value metric spectrum selection, (3) Continuous experimentation and simulation. ^[extracted]
- AI pricing is uniquely challenging because of rapid model cost changes, margin/cogs pressure, and customer demand for ROI clarity. ^[extracted]
