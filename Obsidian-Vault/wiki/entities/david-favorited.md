# David (Favorited)

David is the founder of Favorited, described as "the world's most engaging live app." He spoke at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Background

David previously built a social app with 250 million users generating $20 million per year ^[extracted].

## At Favorited

Favorited scaled from $1 to $100 million annualized revenue in six months — called "the fastest growing company of all time" by David ^[extracted]. The company is seeking "cracked engineers" to join the team ^[extracted].

## Identity Note

David (Favorited) is distinct from David Vorick (Glow Labs) and Diego Rodriguez (CREA) ^[extracted].
