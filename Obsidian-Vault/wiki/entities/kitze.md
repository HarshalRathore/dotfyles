# Kitze

Independent developer, entrepreneur, and Twitter influencer known for advocating **vibe engineering** as a disciplined evolution of vibe coding. ^[extracted] Speaker at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.

## Products

Kitze is a serial builder with ADHD, working on multiple projects simultaneously:

- **Sizzy** — A browser specifically made for developers, not a replacement browser for general browsing but a tool like Photoshop for front-end development. ^[extracted]
- **Zero to Ship** — A "life OS" combining medication tracking, habits, to-dos, and planner into a full-stack application. Currently on sale. ^[extracted]
- **Glynk** — Changelogs, roadmaps, and related product management features. Being revived after a period of dormancy. ^[extracted]

## Philosophy

Kitze is a prominent voice distinguishing **vibe engineering** from **vibe coding** — the practice of using AI agents to code continuously while maintaining active scrutiny of the output, rather than blindly accepting generated code. ^[extracted] He describes himself as a "vibe engineer" with over 50 years of experience (hyperbole), always suspicious of generated code because it reflects his own knowledge and limitations. ^[extracted]

He advocates for voice-to-code as a game-changer, using it to externalize his thinking process by narrating what he sees in the UI and then in the code to the agent. ^[extracted]

## Community Presence

Active on Twitter with a large following. Known for provocative takes on AI coding, frequent conference speaking, and self-deprecating humor. Describes his own conference talks as "50% tweets and 40% pain and 30% reason to remember the name." ^[extracted]
