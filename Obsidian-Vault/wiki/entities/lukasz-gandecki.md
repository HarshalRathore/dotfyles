## Łukasz Gądecki

Łukasz Gądecki is a Polish programmer who has been coding since childhood and is the founder of [[entities/thebrain-pro|TheBrain.pro]]. He spoke at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 about his project [[concepts/books-reimagined|Books Reimagined]], which uses AI to create immersive, multimodal reading experiences for classic literature.

He describes his development approach as "vibe coding" — using AI to rapidly prototype without upfront planning, iterating through waves of AI-generated code until the waste amplitude decreases, then shifting to traditional engineering practices like testing and refactoring. He is a strong advocate for hiding AI from end users and preserving human curation in creative products.

His project started from a personal need: while reading a book about Donald Trump's re-election, he built an AI companion to provide context on unfamiliar characters. This evolved into full multimodal book experiences, starting with Polish-language editions of "The Snow Queen" and expanding to English titles like "1984."
