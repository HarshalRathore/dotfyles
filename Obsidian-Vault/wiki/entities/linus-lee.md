# Linus Lee

An early voice in the debate about AI interaction paradigms. In May 2022 — months before ChatGPT launched — Lee argued that exposing the raw text completion interface is not the right long-term paradigm for AI applications. ^[extracted]

[[entities/maximillian-piras|Maximillian Piras]] cites Lee as the earliest reference point in his [[concepts/schrodingers-chat|Schrödinger's Chat]] timeline, noting that despite Lee's prescient critique, ChatGPT's launch shortly after demonstrated the escape velocity of the chat paradigm. The contradiction — a valid critique followed by undeniable adoption — is the essence of the chat paradox. ^[extracted]
