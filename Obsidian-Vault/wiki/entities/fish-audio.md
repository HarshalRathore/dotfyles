# Fish Audio

Fish Audio is an open-source voice AI company that created Soviet SVC and Fish Speech. It grew from $400K to $5.5M annualized revenue in just four months before being acquired by OpenAudio at a $100 million valuation.

## Key Facts

- **Open source roots**: "We began with open source and crush it" ^[extracted]
- **Prior models**: Soviet SVC and Fish Speech ^[extracted]
- **Growth**: $400K to $5.5M ARR in four months ^[extracted]
- **Valuation**: $100M ^[extracted]
- **TTS Arena**: Surpassed 11 Labs on TTS Arena ranking ^[extracted]
- **Availability**: Instantly available at Fish.audio ^[extracted]

## Technology

Fish Audio's work laid the foundation for OpenAudio's S1 model — the first instructable voice model that controls not just what to say but how to say it ^[extracted]. The company's open-source approach to voice synthesis (Soviet SVC, Fish Speech) established technical credibility before the acquisition ^[extracted].
