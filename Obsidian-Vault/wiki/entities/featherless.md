# Featherless

An AI company focused on inference infrastructure and non-transformer model architectures. Their work challenges the dominant paradigm of scaling up general-purpose transformer models, instead emphasizing reliability and efficiency. ^[extracted]

## Key Work

- **Featherless Action R1** — an action model achieving 65% on real eval (real-world web task benchmark), compared to ~45% for Claude and Gemini. ^[extracted]
- **72B non-transformer model** — built on the RWKV architecture, claimed to be 1000x cheaper in inference costs while performing comparably to transformer models of similar size. ^[extracted]
- **Model acceleration** — also applies their technology to accelerate existing transformer models. ^[extracted]

## Philosophy

Featherless advances the [[concepts/scaling-is-dead-thesis|scaling is dead thesis]] — the position that scaling model size alone will not solve AI reliability problems, and that architectural innovation and specialization are needed for production-quality performance. ^[extracted]
