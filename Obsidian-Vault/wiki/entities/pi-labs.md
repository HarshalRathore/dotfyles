# Pi Labs

**Pi Labs** is an AI company focused on building evaluation metrics and methodology for AI systems. Founded by Google alumni including David Karam, who spent over a decade at Google working on search system evaluation.

## Focus

Pi Labs applies the evaluation methodology developed at Google for stochastic search systems to the broader problem of building reliable AI systems. Their core belief is that evaluation methodology — not tooling — is the primary lever for improving AI system quality. ^[extracted]

## Key People

- **David Karam** — Practitioner in AI evaluation and metrics, co-presenter of "Building Metrics that actually work" at AIEF2025. Also presented "Layering every technique in RAG, one query at a time" at AIEF2025, introducing the quality engineering loop and complexity-adjusted impact framework for RAG improvement.
