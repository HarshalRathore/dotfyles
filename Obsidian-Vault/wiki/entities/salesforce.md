# Salesforce

Salesforce is an enterprise software company known for its customer relationship management (CRM) platform. It has been cited as a company undergoing significant AI-driven transformation. ^[extracted]

## AI-Driven Hiring Halt

In 2025, Mark Benioff announced that Salesforce would no longer hire software engineers for that year, citing a 30% productivity boost since replacing people with AI. This claim was noted by Beth Glenfield at DevDay as something she'd "like to see the data on." ^[extracted]
