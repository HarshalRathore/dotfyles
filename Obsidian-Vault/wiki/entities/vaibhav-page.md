# Vaibhav Page

Vaibhav Page (also referred to as Vibev) is a Principal Engineer at [[entities/blackrock|BlackRock]], working on the data platform teams. He presented alongside [[entities/infant-vasanth|Infant Vasanth]] at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on how BlackRock builds custom knowledge apps at scale. ^[extracted]

## Background

- Principal Engineer at BlackRock on the data teams ^[extracted]
- Focused on building the sandbox + [[concepts/enterprise-ai-app-factory|app factory framework]] that enables BlackRock's investment operations teams to rapidly build custom AI knowledge applications
- His expertise covers prompt engineering at scale, LLM strategy selection, and enterprise AI deployment in regulated financial environments

## Notable Work

- Co-architect of BlackRock's sandbox (playground for operators to build extraction templates) and app factory (cloud-native deployment operator) framework
- Worked on compressing AI app delivery timelines from 3-8 months to a couple of days by giving domain experts self-service tools
- Addressed the challenges of building AI apps in regulated environments including prompt bloat, LLM strategy mixing, and deployment infrastructure
