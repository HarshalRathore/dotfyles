# Stefania Druga

Stefania Druga is a former research scientist at Google and a three-time speaker at AI conferences. She was announced as a pioneer in the field of AI education and a featured speaker at the AI Education Summit, launched by [[entities/benjamin-dunphy|Benjamin Dunphy]] at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.

## AI Education Work

Stefania's previous talk at the AI Engineer World's Fair (the prior year) demonstrated a student learning to code by programming "the very thing that is teaching them to code" — a recursive self-improvement loop in educational AI. Benjamin Dunphy described being "blown away" by this demonstration, calling it the spark that ignited his imagination for the AI Education Summit direction. ^[extracted]
