Nikhil Abraham is the co-founder and CEO of [[entities/cloudchef|CloudChef]], a company building wheeled robots with two arms that cook in commercial kitchens. He presented CloudChef's work at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 (AIEF2025) on the topic of "Robots as Professional Chefs."

Abraham describes CloudChef as a "very small team" that is "growing super fast" and actively hiring in software, ML, and robotics. His contact email is nic@cloudchef.co.

His core thesis: the future of affordable, high-quality food lies in automating commercial kitchen labor with robots that can "act, sense, and reason, and behave in the real world like a chef." He advocates for wheeled two-arm robots over humanoids for this task, citing cost, reliability, and maintenance advantages.
