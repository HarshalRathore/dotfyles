# Aurel Zion

**Aurel Zion** is a mobile software engineer with 14 years of experience who presented on "10x, reimagining the mobile dev workflow" at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2026. ^[extracted]

## Key Points

Aurel described the shift from traditional IDE-based development to **chat-style engineering** using tools like Claude Code, Codex, and Cursor. The key insight is that **iteration is the fundamental friction** in software workflows, and AI agents can unlock 10×–20× productivity by collapsing the iteration loop. ^[extracted]
