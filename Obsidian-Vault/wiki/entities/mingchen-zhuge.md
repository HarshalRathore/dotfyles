# Mingchen Zhuge

Mingchen Zhuge is an AI researcher at Meta, co-author of the **Agent-as-a-Judge** paper (Oct 2024) that extends LLM-as-judge by using agentic judge models with intermediate feedback for evaluation. ^[extracted]

## Research Contributions

- **Agent-as-a-Judge: Evaluate Agents with Agents** (arXiv 2410.10934) — Introduces DevAI benchmark (55 tasks, 365 hierarchical requirements) and demonstrates that agentic judges match human evaluation reliability while outperforming flat LLM-as-judge ^[extracted]
