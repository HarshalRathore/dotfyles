# Telemetrak

Telemetrak is a company focused on building trust in critical AI inference systems for enterprise environments. The company presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 through its representatives [[entities/sahil-yadav|Sahil Yadav]] and [[entities/hariharan-ganesan|Hariharan Ganesan]]. ^[extracted]

- Trustworthy AI inference for mission-critical applications ^[extracted]
- X-TOPS framework — extending MLOPS with built-in trust and human oversight ^[extracted]

- IoT and industrial AI applications ([[entities/sahil-yadav|Yadav]] has 10+ years deploying AI in health monitoring, industrial IoT, and telecom network automation) ^[extracted]
