# Jmo

Jmo is the founder of [[entities/confidence-security|Confidence Security]], a company building privacy-preserving remote compute infrastructure for non-Apple platforms. He holds a PhD in data science and biomedical informatics, and has founded two exited companies — one in AI/data, and one in cybersecurity/infrastructure. He is based at South Park Commons. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Jmo presented "The Unofficial Guide to Apple's Private Cloud Compute" on the CONFSEC track. The talk independently analyzed [[concepts/private-cloud-compute|Apple's PCC]] architecture — distilling the five privacy requirements, six technical components, and mapping Apple's components to equivalents available in the broader ecosystem (TPMs, Sigstore/Sigsum, confidential VMs, open source). ^[extracted]
