# Hamed (LinkedIn AI)

Hamed (also referenced as Hamid) is an AI engineer at LinkedIn who presented the 360Brew project at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 alongside Maziar.

## 360Brew Presentation

Hamed co-presented the 360Brew talk, covering the motivation behind building a single LLM-based foundation model for all of LinkedIn's personalization and ranking tasks. He introduced the brewing pipeline (model development) and the serving architecture (model deployment). ^[extracted]
