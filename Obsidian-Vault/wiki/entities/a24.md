# A24

A24 is an independent film and media company known for its innovative marketing campaigns. In 2024, A24 partnered with File.ai (FAL) for an interactive generative media campaign promoting their film *Civil War*.^[extracted]

## Civil War Campaign

A24's *Civil War* film — set in an imaginary civil war in the United States — featured a live-marketing campaign where users could upload selfies that were transformed into toy soldiers via generative media. These personalized toy soldiers were then displayed in Times Square, creating an interactive, personalized experience that merged physical and digital marketing.^[extracted]

This campaign exemplifies the kind of hyper-personalized, interactive brand experiences that generative media enables — a concrete example of the theoretical predictions made about advertising's transformation.^[inferred]
