# Anish Agarwal

Anish Agarwal is the CEO and co-founder of [[entities/traversal-ai|Traversal.ai]], a company building autonomous production troubleshooting systems. He is an AI researcher with deep expertise in [[concepts/causal-machine-learning|causal machine learning]].

## Background

Anish introduced himself as the CEO of Traversal.ai and noted that Matthew was the first person to join the company. He has spent years as a researcher in causal machine learning and related fields, publishing papers on cause-and-effect discovery from data.

## AIEF2025 Talk

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Anish delivered a talk titled "Production software keeps breaking and it will only get worse." He framed software engineering in three categories — system design, development, and production troubleshooting — and argued that AI coding tools are making development seamless while making troubleshooting exponentially harder due to context loss and increasing system complexity.

He presented Traversal's approach as a combination of causal machine learning, reasoning models, and agent swarms for out-of-sample autonomous troubleshooting.

## View on AI in Software Engineering

Anish holds a contrarian view on the trajectory of AI in software engineering:

- **The promise**: AI frees engineers to focus on creative system design work.
- **The reality**: Without solving troubleshooting, engineers will spend most of their time on on-call and QA.
- **The root cause**: AI-written code means humans have less context about system internals, while systems themselves grow more complex.
