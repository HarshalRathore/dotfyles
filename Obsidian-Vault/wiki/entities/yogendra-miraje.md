# Yogendra Miraje

**Yogendra Miraje** (goes by "Yogi") is an AI practitioner at [[entities/factset|FactSet]], a financial data and software company. He presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on building planning-based agentic workflows for enterprise environments.

## Key Ideas

Miraje's AIEF2025 talk focused on a practical architecture for planning agents that balances autonomy with control. His key contributions include the Blueprint Generator pattern, the workflow agent vs. agentic workflow distinction, and aspect-based evaluation strategies for enterprise agentic systems. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

**"How to Build Planning Agents without losing control"** — a practical guide to building agentic workflows in enterprise settings, featuring a four-node LangGraph architecture (Blueprint Generator → Planner → Executor → Joiner), tool design philosophy from the agent's perspective, and concrete examples from financial research workflows. ^[extracted]
