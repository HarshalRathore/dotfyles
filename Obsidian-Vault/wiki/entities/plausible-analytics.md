# Plausible Analytics

**Plausible Analytics** is a privacy-focused, lightweight web analytics platform.

## Capabilities

Plausible provides:
- Unique visitor counts
- Traffic source attribution
- Geographic breakdown by country
- Device type breakdown

## In Context

Hassan El Mghari uses Plausible for website analytics across his AI apps. A recurring surprise for him is that a significant portion of users access his apps on mobile devices, making mobile experience a critical design consideration. ^[extracted]
