# Alix Partners

Alix Partners is a global management consulting firm that distinguishes itself by "rolling up their sleeves and solving problems" rather than simply delivering PowerPoints. The firm employs a senior-led engagement model where experienced professionals with 15-20 years of domain expertise are deeply involved in day-to-day work delivery, as opposed to the junior-led model common in consulting that relies on leverage through directing many junior staff.

The company operates an internal generative AI platform built by a team of 20 engineers over two years, scaled to 50 deployments serving hundreds of users. This platform supports their consulting engagements across three phases: data ingestion/normalization, analysis/hypothesis generation, and recommendation/deliverable production.

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Alix Partners representatives Kevin Madura and Mo Bhasin presented on how AI is reshaping knowledge work and professional services, including real-world deployment use cases and lessons learned.
