# Brian Johnson

Brian Johnson is a speaker from Tavus who co-presented "Realtime Conversational Video with Pipecat and Tavus" at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 with Chad Bailey of Daily.^[extracted]

## Tavus Background

Brian comes from Tavus, which started as an AI research company focused on a rendering model.^[extracted] He explained how Tavus quickly realized the rendering model needed to operate in a real-time context to be useful — requiring speed, turn detection, response timing, signal processing, and orchestration.^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

Brian co-presented on building real-time conversational video systems, covering Tavus's conversational video interface — an end-to-end pipeline for having conversations with video replicas of anyone.^[extracted] He introduced Tavus's proprietary models (Sparrow Zero and Raven Zero) and explained how they are being integrated into the Pipecat ecosystem.^[extracted] Response times average around 600 milliseconds, though Brian noted these are sometimes intentionally slowed down depending on the models being used.^[extracted]

## Key Contributions

Brian demonstrated how Tavus offers a conversational video interface that goes beyond the traditional voice AI stack — adding video generation as a real-time output layer.^[extracted] He highlighted the partnership between Tavus and Pipecat, showing how Tavus models are being moved into Pipecat for broader accessibility.^[extracted]
