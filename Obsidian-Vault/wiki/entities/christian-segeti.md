# Christian Segeti

Christian Segeti is an AI researcher and the Chief Scientist at [[entities/morph|Morph]], announced at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025. He was formerly a co-founder at xAI, where he led the development of code reasoning capabilities for Grok 3. ^[extracted]

## Contributions

- **Grok 3 code reasoning** — Led code reasoning development at xAI ^[extracted]
- **Batch storm** — Invented the batch storm technique ^[extracted]
- **Adversarial examples** — Pioneered work on adversarial examples ^[extracted]

## Role at Morph

Segeti joined Morph as Chief Scientist to lead development of [[concepts/verified-superintelligence|Verified Superintelligence]], described by [[entities/jesse-han|Jesse Han]] as "perhaps the best person in the world for developing verified superintelligence." His work at the intersection of verification methods, symbolic reasoning, and reasoning in large language models over the past decade is positioned as foundational to Morph's [[entities/magi-1|Magi 1]] model. ^[extracted]
