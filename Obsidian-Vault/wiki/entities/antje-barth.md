# Antje Barth

AWS speaker at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 who presented on building AI agents at cloud scale. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk: Building Agents at Cloud Scale

Antje presented the largest integration of services, agentic capabilities, and LLMs that AWS knows of — the complete reimagining of Alexa through Alexa Plus. She demonstrated how over 600 million Alexa devices work with hundreds of specialized expert systems, tens of thousands of partner services, and devices. ^[extracted]

She also introduced Strands Agents, an open-source Python SDK for building AI agents, and highlighted Amazon Q Developer CLI as an example of rapid agentic service delivery (built in three weeks). ^[extracted]

## Key Themes

- **Model-driven agent design**: Let LLMs decide, plan, and reason; developers focus on what the agent should do, not how.
- **Scale at Amazon**: 1,000+ generative AI applications in development, transforming inventory forecasting, delivery optimization, customer shopping, and home interactions.
- **Speed of execution**: Emphasized that Amazon Q Developer CLI was built and shipped in just three weeks.
