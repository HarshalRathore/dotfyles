# Mark Andreessen

**Mark Andreessen** (often spelled Marc Andreessen) is a techno-optimist and venture capitalist associated with the **S-curve technology** framework discussed at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2026. ^[extracted]

## Techno-Optimism

Andreessen's techno-optimism — the belief that technology will continue to solve problems even as individual technologies reach their limits — aligns with the S-curve pattern: ^[extracted]

> All technology exponentials eventually flatten into S-curves, but humanity has always responded by developing new basic technologies to start the next exponential.
