# Michael Albada

Michael Albada is a Principal Applied Scientist at Microsoft working in the cybersecurity division. He has been at Microsoft for approximately two years and is one of the key contributors to [[entities/microsoft|Security Co-Pilot]] and the recently announced Security Co-Pilot Agents. ^[extracted]

## Background

Before Microsoft, Albada spent four years working on machine learning at [[entities/uber|Uber]], focusing on large-scale geospatial problems. Prior to that, he worked in startups. ^[extract0]

## O'Reilly Book

Albada is the author of a comprehensive book on building applications with AI agents, published by O'Reilly. The first seven chapters were available on early release at the time of his AIEF2025 talk, with the full print edition expected the following month. The book includes full code examples backing the concepts presented in his conference talks. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Albada presented "Building Applications with AI Agents," distilling insights from his upcoming O'Reilly book. His talk covered the promise and obstacles in agentic development, core components for building effective AI systems that reach production, common pitfalls, and lessons learned. ^[extracted]
