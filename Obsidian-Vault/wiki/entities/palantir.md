# Palantir

Palantir is an enterprise software company known for its data integration and analytics platforms (Goth, Foundry, Apollo).^[extracted] It has successfully scaled across multiple market segments using an organizational model that Olive adapts for consumer software.^[extracted]

## Organizational Model: The Four-Engineer Deployment

Palantir deploys small teams of four software engineers (called "Deltas") across different market segments or client engagements.^[extracted] Each Delta owns a product or engagement end-to-end — they are both builders and operators, living in the metrics and working directly with clients.^[extracted]

## Inspiration for Olive

Olive describes itself as "building the consumer version of Palantir" and adapts Palantir's model into a harvester/cultivator split:^[extracted]

- **Harvesters** map to Palantir Deltas — product engineers who own products end-to-end.
- **Cultivators** are Olive's innovation — AI software engineers building the agentic operating system, a role that doesn't have a direct Palantir analogue but extends the model into the AI-native era.^[inferred]
