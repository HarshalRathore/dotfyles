# NLW

NLW is the CEO of [[entities/superintelligent|Superintelligent]], an AI planning platform, and the host of the [[entities/ai-daily-brief|AI Daily Brief]], a daily news analysis podcast about AI. ^[extracted]

## Background

NLW brings two perspectives to AI consulting and enterprise adoption analysis:
- **Macro-level**: High-level analysis of AI news and trends through the AI Daily Brief podcast
- **Ground-level**: Direct engagement interviewing executives about what's happening inside their organizations through Superintelligent

## Notable Contributions

At AIEF2025, NLW presented findings from Superintelligent's market study collecting self-reported ROI data on ~3,500 AI use cases from 1,000+ organizations, analyzing the first 2,500. The study tracked eight impact categories and revealed patterns in enterprise AI adoption, the agent adoption surge, and the pilot-to-scale bottleneck. ^[extracted]
