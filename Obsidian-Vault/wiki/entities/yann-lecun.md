# Yann LeCun

**Yann LeCun** is an AI researcher at **Meta** and the creator of **JEPA** (Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture). His work was discussed at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2026. ^[extracted]

## JEPA

JEPA is a self-supervised learning architecture using a reflective optimization loop (collect feedback → LLM reflection → Pareto pool selection) to improve model prompts and harnesses without gradient-based training. Results include 7× AMD NPU improvement and 90× Databricks cost reduction. ^[extracted]
