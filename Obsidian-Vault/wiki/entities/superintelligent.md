# Superintelligent

Superintelligent is an AI planning platform founded and led by [[entities/nlw|NLW]]. ^[extracted]

## Focus

The company operates at the intersection of AI strategy and enterprise implementation, conducting extensive research into how organizations are actually using AI and what ROI they're achieving. Through their market study program, they collect self-reported data from organizations about their AI use cases, impact categories, and adoption patterns.

## Notable Research

In late 2025, Superintelligent launched a study collecting self-reported AI use cases from organizations. By the time of AIEF2025, they had received 1,000+ submissions representing ~3,500 use cases, with the first 2,500 analyzed and presented at the conference. The study tracked eight impact categories and revealed significant patterns in enterprise AI adoption, agent deployment, and ROI expectations. ^[extracted]
