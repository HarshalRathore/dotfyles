# Olive

Olive (originally named Aleve) is a New York City–based consumer software company founded by Sid Bendre and co-founders.^[extracted] The company builds a family of AI-powered consumer products and has demonstrated that a team of four can generate $6M in ARR profitably with over half a billion social media views.^[extracted]

## Products

### QuizBuddy (Quizzer AI)

Launched January 26, 2023, as an AI mobile app. A TikTok video went viral overnight, generating a million views that converted to 10,000 users in under 30 hours.^[extracted] The team initially used the beta Codex model (cycling through 10 accounts) before OpenAI sunset it for abuse.^[extracted]

### Unstuck AI

A study companion tool for students. Launched after the team doubled down on learnings from QuizBuddy.^[extracted] Reached 1 million users in under nine weeks and generated over a quarter billion views across social media in a single month.^[extracted] Reached #3 in the education charts, right under GoStudent and Duolingo.^[extracted]

### Stealth Product (Third Product)

Launched a few weeks before the AIEF2025 talk, this is Olive's first product outside the education domain.^[extracted] Built in three weeks using blueprints developed from prior products.^[extracted] Reached 1,000+ users and is already profitable.^[extracted]
