# Doug Guthrie

Doug Guthrie is a solutions engineer at [[entities/braintrust|Braintrust]], an end-to-end developer platform for building AI products. He joined Braintrust in mid-2025 (approximately three weeks before the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 where he presented).

## Background

Guthrie presents on AI evaluation topics, covering the practical mechanics of running evals from local development through production monitoring. His talk "Evals 101" at AIEF2025 covers the foundational concepts of evaluation: why evals matter, what they consist of (task, dataset, score), how to run them offline and online, and how to build a continuous improvement flywheel.

## Notable Contributions

- **"Evals 101"** at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 — A practical introduction to AI evaluation covering the full workflow from playground prototyping to production scoring and human-in-the-loop feedback
