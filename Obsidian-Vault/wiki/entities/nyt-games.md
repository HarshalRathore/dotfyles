# NYT Games

The games division of the [[entities/new-york-times|New York Times]], responsible for creating and maintaining the NYT's popular word and puzzle games. ^[extracted]

## Games

### Connections

[[concepts/connections-game|Connections]] is the second-most-played game on the NYT platform (after [[entities/wordle|Wordle]]), with hundreds of millions of plays within its first year. Launched in beta in June 2023 and officially in August 2023. ^[extracted]

### Wordle

The most-played game on the NYT platform. Acquired by the NYT in 2022. ^[extracted]

## Editorial

Puzzles are edited by Winnaloo, an editor at the NYT who works with the games development team. ^[extracted]

## Policy

All Connections puzzles, game mechanics, and programming are human-made. ^[extracted] This is stated as a permanent policy: "human-made now and forever." ^[extracted]
