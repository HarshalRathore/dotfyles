## Overview

Alicorp is an early-stage venture fund based in New York. Kenneth Auchenberg, former member of the early [[entities/vs-code|VS Code]] team at Microsoft and former head of developer platform at [[entities/stripe|Stripe]], is a partner at Alicorp.

The fund focuses on early-stage investments. Kenneth's product-builder lens from his time building developer platforms and AI tools informs his investment approach.
