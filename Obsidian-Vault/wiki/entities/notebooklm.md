## NotebookLM

NotebookLM is an AI-powered note-taking and document analysis tool developed by [[entities/google]]. The first version was internally codenamed "Tailwind" and was announced at Google I/O 2023.

The product was conceived by Raiza Martin, who served as its product manager. Her vision was to create a tool where users could "put a bunch of shit in it — docs, slides — and just chat with it and it does something for me that seems really valuable." She wanted to build it from the ground up rather than bolt features onto existing products.

The road to shipping NotebookLM was difficult. Martin recalls that "the amount of people that told me it was stupid was fascinatingly high" — including users, stakeholders, and teammates. She describes her personal clarity about the product as the energy source that sustained her through this skepticism.

NotebookLM exemplifies Martin's broader thesis: most products we use today feel "ugly" because they were built in a pre-AI era where interactions were constrained by what was technically possible rather than what would be ideal. NotebookLM was built from scratch with AI-native interactions at its core.
