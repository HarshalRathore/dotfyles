## Overview

**Jam** is a venture capital firm focused on early-stage investments. Dani Grant, a speaker at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, is an investor at Jam. ^[extracted]

## Investment Thesis

Jam is described as comfortable with pre-seed and seed-stage investments, including companies that are pre-revenue and sometimes pre-product. ^[extracted] The firm backs founders based on the team, the founder's vision, and their ability to recruit — rather than requiring demonstrated product-market fit at the time of investment. ^[inferred]

## Fund Stage Focus

Jam operates in the pre-seed and seed stages, which the speakers characterize as the most founder-friendly stages for raising capital without revenue or a finished product. ^[extracted] Later-stage and multi-stage funds tend to require ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) growth or ARR itself. ^[extracted]
