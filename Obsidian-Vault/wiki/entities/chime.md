# Chime

Chime is a financial technology company whose speech analytics team, led by [[entities/chris-hernandez|Chris Hernandez]], participated in the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025. Chris co-presented "The Build-Operate Divide" with Jeremy of [[entities/freeplay|Freeplay]], bringing perspective from operationalizing AI in a regulated financial context. ^[extracted]

## AI at Chime

Chris's speech analytics team at Chime works on evaluating AI interactions at scale — a domain that parallels the [[concepts/ai-ops-evolution]] described in the talk. His experience in CX and ML at a fintech company provides a real-world example of how QA/CX teams are evolving for Gen AI: from scorekeepers to model shapers who test prompts, tag outputs, and influence model behavior. ^[inferred]
