# Tom Shapland

Dr. Tom Shapland is the Director of AI at [[entities/livekit|LiveKit]], working on voice AI infrastructure and turn-taking technology. He presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on the voice AI interruption problem.

## Role at LiveKit

- Director of AI (joined approximately 4 weeks before his July 2025 talk) ^[extracted]
- Works on LiveKit's end-of-utterance detection model and voice AI pipeline ^[inferred]

## Research & Contributions

- Delivered a talk synthesizing linguistics research, human conversation analysis, and practical voice AI engineering
- Built a side-by-side demo comparing traditional VAD-based turn-taking vs semantic end-of-utterance detection
- Advocates for augmented VAD approaches over full duplex models for production voice AI ^[inferred]
