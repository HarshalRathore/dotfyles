# Apoorva Joshi

AI-focused Developer Advocate at [[entities/mongodb|MongoDB]], building workshops and educational content for AI developers. Prior to MongoDB, spent six years as a data scientist in the cybersecurity space.

## Notable Work

- Presented "Building Multimodal AI Agents From Scratch" at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 — a hands-on workshop on building multimodal AI agents using Python
- Authored a talk on AI agent memory systems (mentioned during the workshop)
- Builds educational workshops to help developers build AI applications

## Key Contributions

At the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, presented a practical framework for understanding and building multimodal agents, covering:
- The four components of AI agents (perception, planning, tools, memory)
- The [[concepts/react-pattern|ReAct]] (reasoning + acting) pattern for agent planning
- [[concepts/screenshot-based-multimodal-retrieval|Screenshot-based multimodal retrieval]] using VLM-based embedding models
- [[concepts/modality-gap|Modality gap]] — architectural limitation of CLIP-based models

## Team

Presented alongside team members Richmond, Rafa, Tebow, and Mikiko at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.
