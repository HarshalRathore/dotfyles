# Lou Bichard

Lou Bichard is a Field TTO at [[entities/gitpod|Gitpod]].

## Background

- Nearly four years at Gitpod building a platform for automated development environments.
- Prior background in software engineering and platform engineering.
- Six years total working on product infrastructure and architecture for secure environments, particularly in regulated industries.
- Spoke at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on agent fleet architectures for CISO-approved deployments.

## Key Contributions

- Led Gitpod's architectural evolution from multi-tenant SaaS through self-hosted and managed substrate models to a first-principles architecture for regulated environments.
- Architect of the platform now running Gitpod's AI agent fleet.
