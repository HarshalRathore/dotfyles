# Eugene Yan

Scientist at Amazon, presenting on the intersection of recommendation systems and large language models. Gave the keynote at the inaugural REXIS track at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025. Known for writing about applied machine learning, RecSys, and LLM integration patterns. ^[extracted]

*Note: This is a different person from [[entities/eugene|Eugene Chee]] (founder of Featherless AI).*

## Key Contributions

- Authored the REXIS keynote proposing three ideas for merging RecSys with LLMs: semantic IDs, data augmentation, and unified models. ^[extracted]
- Presented detailed industry case studies from Kuaishou, Indeed, Spotify, Netflix, and Etsy on LLM-enhanced recommendation systems. ^[extracted]
- Traced the evolution of language modeling in RecSys from Word2Vec (2013) through GRUs and transformers. ^[extracted]
- Introduced [[entities/yesu-feng|Yesu Feng]]'s Netflix Recommendation Foundation Model talk at AIEF2025, noting it as a highlight of the REXIS track. ^[extracted]
