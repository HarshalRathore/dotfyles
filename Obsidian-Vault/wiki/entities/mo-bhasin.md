# Mo Bhasin

Mo Bhasin is Director of AI Products at [[entities/alix-partners|Alix Partners]]. Prior to Alix Partners, he was a co-founder of an anomaly detection startup and a data scientist at Google. He and Kevin Madura co-lead the development of an internal generative AI platform at Alix Partners, which they have been building for two years with a team of 20 engineers, scaled to 50 deployments and hundreds of users.

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Bhasin presented on the enterprise AI productivity paradox — why 89% of CEOs plan to implement agentic AI (per Deloitte) but NBER reports no significant impact on earnings, BCG says three quarters of companies fail to scale Gen AI value, and S&P Global reports almost half are abandoning AI initiatives. He argued the distinction between employee productivity and enterprise productivity is the key to understanding this gap.

Bhasin demonstrated practical use cases including AI-powered categorization using structured outputs and tool calls (web queries for grounding), showing that accuracy gains from unsupervised learning require close partnerships with business teams and convert skeptics into champions.
