# Grant Lee

**Grant Lee** is the co-founder and CEO of [[entities/gamma|Gamma]], the AI-powered presentation platform positioned as "the anti-PowerPoint." ^[extracted]

## Background

Grant previously worked in consulting, which is where he encountered the pain point that led to Gamma: staring at blank slides and feeling there must be a better way to create presentations. ^[extracted]

## Leadership Philosophy

Grant speaks on organizational innovation — applying first-principles thinking not just to product building but to team building. ^[extracted] His core themes:

- **Rise of the generalist** — Hiring people who span multiple disciplines (e.g., Gamma's head of design who also codes and does user research)
- **Player-coach model** — Leaders who manage while still doing hands-on work, adapted from sports metaphors
- **Lean scaling** — Growing to 50M users on a team of only 30, roughly one-tenth the size a similar company would have needed a few years prior ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

Grant presented "Rethinking Team Building: how a 30-person Startup serves 50 Million Users" at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, sharing Gamma's organizational design lessons. ^[extracted]
