# Benjamin Dunphy

Benjamin Dunphy is the co-founder of the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, organized in partnership with SWIX. He is a speaker and organizer at the event who announced the launch of the AI Education Summit as a new initiative.

## AI Engineer World's Fair

Benjamin co-founded the AI Engineer World's Fair with SWIX, describing it as "one of the most rewarding experiences of [his] career." The conference brings together AI practitioners, researchers, and engineers to share knowledge about building with AI systems. ^[extracted]

## AI Education Summit

Benjamin announced the AI Education Summit as a new chapter for the AIEF community. The summit aims to address the gap between rapid AI advancement and the preparedness of children, parents, and educators to navigate AI ethically and effectively. The first event is a free online gathering featuring Stefania Druga (former Google research scientist) as a keynote speaker. ^[extracted]

## Personal Background

Benjamin describes his "oh shit moment" with AI as prompting ChatGPT to recursively decompose the universe into core principles and subclassifications — finding the responses both fascinating and wrong (e.g., classifying viruses as a subcategory of quarks). This moment led him to text his brother-in-law "AGI has been achieved." He also reflects on the parallel between computers evolving from cold corporate machines to personal companions, and how AI education must evolve similarly for future generations. ^[extracted]
