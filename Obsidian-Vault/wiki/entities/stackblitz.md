# StackBlitz

**StackBlitz** is a web development company founded by **Eric Simons** and his co-founder (a childhood friend they've been building websites with since age 13).

## History

The company operated for seven years before launching Bolt.new in October of the previous year. Before that, StackBlitz operated in the IDE space, where they had one main competitor that was acquired and "stripped for parts" two weeks before Bolt.new launched — a competitor that would have been meaningful in this space.

Prior to StackBlitz, Simons and his co-founder bootstrapped an earlier company from scratch, experiencing the full startup journey of being broke and living on couches.

## Philosophy and Structure

StackBlitz operates on a philosophy of small teams with high context per head:
- Fewer than 20 people at time of Bolt.new launch
- No dedicated support or success roles — leadership handled support tickets directly
- Low ego, high trust culture
- Seven years of working together before Bolt.new launch created deep alignment
- Core mantra: "small number of people with more context per head"

## Bolt.new Launch

Bolt.new was not conceived as a strategic pivot but as the company's "last effort" before potentially shutting down. It uses the same core technology StackBlitz had been building for seven years. The launch grew ARR from $0.7M to $20M in 60 days.
