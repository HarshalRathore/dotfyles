# Alibaba (AI / Qwen)

**Alibaba** is a Chinese technology company whose AI division produces the **Qwen** series of large language models. Per George Cameron's AIEF2025 talk, Alibaba is a leading force in the open-weight frontier.

## Open-Weight Leadership

Alibaba's Qwen 3 series ranks as the **second-leading open-weight model in reasoning** and leads in **non-reasoning open-weight** categories, according to Artificial Analysis benchmarks. ^[extracted]

This places Alibaba alongside DeepSeek as one of the two dominant Chinese labs driving the open-weight frontier forward. ^[extracted]

## Position in the Open-Weights Landscape

Within the open-weight ecosystem, Chinese labs collectively lead both reasoning and non-reasoning categories: ^[extracted]

- **DeepSeek** — leads both reasoning (R1) and non-reasoning open-weight
- **Alibaba/Qwen** — second in reasoning open-weight, leading in non-reasoning
- **Meta** — Llama family with NemoTron fine-tunes, competitive close
- **NVIDIA** — NemoTron fine-tunes of Llama, competitive close
