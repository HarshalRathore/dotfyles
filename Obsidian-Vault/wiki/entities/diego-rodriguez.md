# Diego Rodriguez

Diego Rodriguez is co-founder and CTO of CREA, an AI creative suite with 25 million users. He also founded Krea.ai, an AI company focused on generative media and perceptual evaluation. He spoke at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 in two contexts: the HF0 Residency showcase and a talk on perceptual evaluations for aesthetics.

## Background

- Previously founded a big data privacy tool, which he and his co-founder raised $50 million for before selling ^[extracted]
- Describes himself as building "an AI creative suite" focused on helping studios generate content at massive scale ^[extracted]
- Known for framing complex problems through storytelling — uses car/traffic, Tower of Babel, and Korea ad adoption as metaphors for AI challenges ^[extracted]

## At CREA

Rodriguez highlights a case where a studio went from sign-up to conversion to payment to broadcast in two days, demonstrating the velocity of AI-driven creative workflows ^[extracted].

## At Krea.ai

At Krea.ai, Rodriguez focuses on perceptual evaluation — developing evaluation methods that measure what humans actually perceive in AI-generated media rather than relying on proxy metrics like FID scores. His AIEF2025 talk argued that the AI field is spending too much effort on computationally convenient metrics and not enough on what humans actually experience ^[extracted].
