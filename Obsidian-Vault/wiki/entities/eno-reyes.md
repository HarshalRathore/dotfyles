## Eno Reyes

Eno Reyes is the founder of [[entities/factory|Factory]], an AI agent platform company building infrastructure for agent-native software development. He began working on LLMs about two and a half years ago around the time GPT-3.5 was released.

### Key Views

Eno advocates that software development is transitioning from the **era of human-driven development to agent-driven development**. He argues that the current zeitgeist of adding AI on top of 20-year-old human-oriented tools is insufficient — true power requires building platforms specifically for agent delegation.

He draws an analogy comparing agents to **climbing gear** and building production software to **scaling Mount Everest**: better tools make the climb more accessible, but expertise and proper leverage are still required.^[extracted]

### AIEF2025 Talk

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Eno presented "Ship Production Software in Minutes, Not Months," a deep dive into agent-native development and lessons learned helping enterprise organizations transition. The talk covered Factory's platform of "droids," the critical importance of context over model capability, and the vision of moving from executing to orchestrating systems that work on behalf of teams.^[extracted]
