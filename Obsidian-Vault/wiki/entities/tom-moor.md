# Tom Moor

Tom Moor leads the engineering team at [[entities/linear|Linear]], a product development tool that describes itself as "an operating system for engineering and product teams." ^[extracted]

## AI Journey at Linear

Moor spun up Linear's internal AI skunkworks team in early 2023, around the GPT-3 era. The initial team had no prior AI experience and learned by doing, focusing first on pragmatic, hidden value-adds rather than flashy AI features. ^[extracted]

Under his leadership, Linear's AI strategy evolved through two distinct phases:

**Phase 1 (2023):** Small, seamless features — similar issues via cosine similarity, natural language filters, Slack-to-issue conversion. Deliberately avoided shipping a Copilot-style feature because quality didn't meet their bar. ^[extracted]

**Phase 2 (2024+):** After foundation models improved (reasoning, multimodal, million-token context windows), Linear rebuilt their search infrastructure and launched more sophisticated features including product intelligence, customer feedback analysis, daily pulse summaries, and issue creation from video. ^[extracted]

His philosophy centers on "small, pragmatic value adds" that users don't even realize are AI-powered — contrasting with competitors who "slap AI in your face" with obvious AI branding. ^[extracted]

## Agent Coordination Vision

Moor frames agents as "infinitely scalable cloud-based teammates" and believes they should live in the same communication space as humans. Linear launched an agent platform to make their product development tool "pluggable" — enabling teams to integrate custom agents into their workflow. ^[extracted]
