# Jeff Huber

**Jeff Huber** is the co-founder and CEO of [[entities/chroma|Chroma]], an open-source AI-native vector database. He presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on how to empirically evaluate and improve retrieval systems using fast evals and synthetic query generation.

His core thesis: you can only manage what you measure. He advocates for fast evals — sets of query-document pairs that run in seconds for pennies — as the primary tool for systematic, deterministic improvement of retrieval pipelines. He demonstrated this approach on the [[entities/weights-and-biases|Weights & Biases]] chatbot, showing that synthetic queries can be generated to semantically align with real user queries.
