# Mitesh Patel

Mitesh Patel leads the Developer Advocate team at [[entities/nvidia|NVIDIA]]. His team creates technical workflows and notebooks for AI applications and releases them as open-source on GitHub for developers to build upon.

## Background

- Leads the NVIDIA Developer Advocate team
- Focuses on creating reusable technical workflows and Jupyter notebooks for AI applications
- Presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on HybridRAG ^[extracted]

## Notable Work

- Co-developed a HybridRAG system with NVIDIA partners combining knowledge graph and vector retrieval
- Worked on fine-tuning LLaMA models (LoRA) for improved triplet extraction quality
- Researched data cleaning techniques (regex) for better knowledge graph construction
- Advocate for developer programs at NVIDIA, releasing technical content regularly
