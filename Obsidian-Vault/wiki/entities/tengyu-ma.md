# Tengyu Ma

CEO and co-founder of [[entities/voyage-ai|Voyage AI]], which was acquired by [[entities/mongodb|MongoDB]]. Also teaches at Stanford University.

## Focus Areas

Tengyu's work centers on [[concepts/rag-pipeline-architecture|RAG]] and embedding models — the core of Voyage AI's product focus. At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, he presented a comprehensive view of the RAG landscape in 2025, arguing for RAG over fine-tuning and long-context for enterprise knowledge injection.

## AIEF2025 Talk

He delivered "RAG in 2025: State of the Art and the Road Forward," covering:
- Why RAG beats fine-tuning and long-context (library analogy)
- Embedding model progress and scaling laws
- Matricial learning and quantization-aware training for storage reduction
- Hybrid search, re-ranking, query decomposition, and document enrichment
