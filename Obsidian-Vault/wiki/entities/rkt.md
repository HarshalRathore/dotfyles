# rkt

## Basic Information
- Type: product
- Source: containerd-cri-etc_fb87d8

## Description
rkt (pronounced 'rocket') was an application container engine developed by [[entities/docker]]'s early competitor [[entities/cncf]] as a secure and standards-compliant alternative. It was designed with a core focus on security, composability, and conforming to open standards like the [[entities/open-container-initiative-oci]]. While rkt saw less widespread adoption than [[entities/docker]], it was a significant early player in the container runtime landscape and contributed importantly to the development of container standards and the ecosystem that led to projects like [[entities/containerd]]. The project explored different architectural models for container execution compared to Docker's monolithic daemon.
