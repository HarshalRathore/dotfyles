# Infant Vasanth

Infant Vasanth is a Director of Engineering at [[entities/blackrock|BlackRock]], working on the data platform teams. He presented alongside [[entities/vaibhav-page|Vaibhav Page]] at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on how BlackRock builds custom knowledge apps at scale. ^[extracted]

## Background

- Director of Engineering at BlackRock on the data teams ^[extracted]
- Focused on building the sandbox + [[concepts/enterprise-ai-app-factory|app factory framework]] for custom AI knowledge applications
- Expertise covers enterprise AI architecture in regulated financial environments with emphasis on human-in-the-loop design

## Notable Work

- Co-architect of BlackRock's sandbox and app factory for rapidly building custom knowledge applications
- Advocated for human-in-the-loop as a first-class design principle ("design for human in the loop first if you are in a highly regulated environment") ^[extracted]
- Key contribution: the insight that agentic systems do not yet work for complex domain-heavy use cases because the domain knowledge is "imbued in the human head" ^[extracted]
