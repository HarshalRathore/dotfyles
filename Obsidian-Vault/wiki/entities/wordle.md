# Wordle

The most-played game on the [[entities/nyt-games|NYT Games]] platform. ^[extracted]

## Overview

Wordle is a daily word-guessing puzzle game that became a cultural phenomenon before being acquired by the New York Times in 2022. It remains the top game by play volume on the NYT platform, ahead of [[concepts/connections-game|Connections]]. ^[extracted]

## Comparison to Connections

[[concepts/connections-game|Connections]] quickly became the second-most-played NYT game after Wordle, with hundreds of millions of plays within its first year (launched June 2023). ^[extracted]

## Key Source

- AIEF2025 talk by Shafik Quoraishee on Connections as an NLP benchmark mentions Wordle as the benchmark for play volume. ^[extracted]
