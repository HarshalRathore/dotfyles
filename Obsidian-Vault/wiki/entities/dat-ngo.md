# Dat Ngo

**Dat Ngo** is an AI architect at [[entities/arize-ai|Arize AI]], where he builds observability and evaluation infrastructure for LLM applications. He has been working in the observability and evals space since its inception. ^[extracted]

Arize AI is described as the largest AI evals player in the space, serving teams at companies like Reddit and Duolingo. Duolingo runs approximately 20 evals per trace, representing massive eval scale. ^[extracted]

Dat distinguishes himself as an engineer by trade rather than a developer advocate, with a strong passion for the AI community. Based in San Francisco for the past five years, he attends AI events broadly. ^[extracted]

At AIEF2025, he presented "Engineering Better Evals: Scalable LLM Evaluation Pipelines That Work," covering the full evals toolbox from LLM-as-judge through code-based heuristics, golden datasets, and the continuous tuning loop. ^[extracted]
