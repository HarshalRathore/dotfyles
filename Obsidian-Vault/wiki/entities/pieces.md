# Pieces

**Pieces** is a startup building artificial long-term memory for developers across the operating system. ^[extracted] Founded by Savu (CEO and co-founder), the company's vision centers on offline-first AI. ^[extracted]

## Journey to Foundry Local

Pieces' path to on-device AI: ^[extracted]

1. Late 2022: began exploring small language models running at the edge on all major platforms
2. Initially rolled their own Llama.cpp, then moved to O-Llama
3. Frustrations persisted around versioning, performance, and end-user experience
4. Partnered with Microsoft on [[entities/foundry-local|Foundry Local]] for an end-to-end solution

## Results with Foundry Local

After partnering with Microsoft: ^[extracted]

- Went from documentation access to production-ready build "in no time"
- Noticeable improvements in memory management
- Improved time-to-first-token
- Higher tokens-per-second throughput
