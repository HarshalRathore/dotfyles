# Dr. Jasper Zhang

Dr. Jasper Zhang is the CEO and co-founder of [[entities/hyperbolic|Hyperbolic]], an AI cloud platform building a global GPU marketplace. ^[extracted]

## Background

- **Education**: PhD in Mathematics from UC Berkeley, completed in 2 years — making him the fastest person in Berkeley history to finish a PhD. Won multiple gold medals. ^[extracted]
- **Career**: After his PhD, worked at Jane Street ("State of Securities") as a key strategist using AI and machine learning to predict markets. ^[extracted]
- **Focus**: Passionate about efficiency and cost reduction in compute — recognizing that GPU compute is one of the biggest costs for AI companies and startups. ^[extracted]

## Thesis

Jasper argues that the AI compute shortage should be solved through a GPU marketplace and aggregation layer rather than simply building more data centers. His core insight: GPU utilization is extremely low (80% idle for enterprises) and a fragmented market of 100+ GPU clouds exists alongside idle capacity in data centers. ^[extracted]
