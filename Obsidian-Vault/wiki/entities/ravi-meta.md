# Ravi Meta

Ravi Meta is a product leader who served as Chief Product Officer at [[entities/tinder|Tinder]] and held product leadership roles at Facebook, Microsoft, and TripAdvisor. ^[extracted]

## AI Strategy Contributions

Meta collaborated with [[entities/brian-balfour|Brian Balfour]] and [[entities/reforge|Reforge]] to create an AI strategy course that formed the foundation of the [[concepts/ai-lego-blocks-framework|AI Lego Blocks Framework]]. This course focused on the question of how to win in the AI era's intense competitive environment. ^[extracted]
