# AIEF2025

**AI Engineer World's Fair 2025** (AIEF2025) is a conference covering AI engineering, agent development, robotics, evaluation, generative media, and production AI systems.

## Talks

- [[references/aief2025-bolt-new-how-we-scaled-0-20m-arr-in-60-days-with-15-people-eric-simons-bolt|Bolt.new: How we scaled $0-20m ARR in 60 days, with 15 people — Eric Simons, Bolt]] — Startup scaling, small teams, product-market fit
- [[references/aief2025-does-ai-actually-boost-developer-productivity-yegor-denisov-blanch-stanford|Does AI Actually Boost Developer Productivity? (100k Devs Study) — Yegor Denisov-Blanch, Stanford]] — Largest study on AI's impact on developer productivity; 15-20% net boost; rework problem; critiques of commit-based metrics, greenfield experiments, and self-reported surveys
- [[references/aief2025-everything-is-ugly-so-go-build-something-that-isnt-raiza-martin-huxe|Everything is ugly, so go build something that isn't — Raiza Martin, Huxe (ex NotebookLM)]] — Product building in the AI era; multi-layer cake framework; personal clarity; consumer unrest; everything gets rebuilt
- [[references/aief2025-conquering-agent-chaos-rick-blalock-agentuity|Conquering Agent Chaos — Rick Blalock, Agentuity]] — Agent deployment challenges, observability, infrastructure patterns
- [[references/aief2025-why-your-product-needs-an-ai-product-manager-james-lowe-i-ai|Why your product needs an AI product manager, and why it should be you — James Lowe, i.AI]] — AI product management, evaluation frameworks, capability-first thinking
- [[references/aief2025-machines-of-buying-and-selling-grace-adam-behrens-new-generation|Machines of Buying and Selling Grace — Adam Behrens, New Generation]] — Agentic commerce evolution, two futures for AI-mediated transactions, three challenges (transactions, intent, product data), market design analogy to finance
- [[references/aief2025-knowledge-graphs-litigation-agents-tom-smoker-whyhow|Knowledge Graphs in Litigation Agents — Tom Smoker, WhyHow]] — Knowledge graphs and multi-agent systems for class action/mass tort case discovery; accuracy compounding in agent chains; graph-based state management
