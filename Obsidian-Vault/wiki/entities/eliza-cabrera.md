# Eliza Cabrera

Eliza Cabrera is a Principal AI Product Manager at [[entities/workday|Workday]]. She is currently building Workday's financial audit agent, and previously led go-to-market for Workday's policy agent and early access for the Workday Assistant (a copilot-like experience), as well as early Gen AI features. ^[extracted]

## AI Engineer World's Fair 2025

Eliza co-presented with [[entities/jeremy-silva|Jeremy Silva]] ([[entities/freeplay|Freeplay]]) at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on the topic "Build Dynamic Products, and Stop the AI Sideshow." She provided Workday's concrete example of the [[concepts/dynamic-products|crawl-walk-run framework]]:

- **Crawl phase:** AI-powered knowledge base content generation and one-click translations across 34 languages, built as embedded features in the existing editor UX. ^[extracted]
- **Walk phase:** The contextually aware Workday Assistant, a copilot that works across HCM and financials. ^[extracted]
- **Run phase:** The agent system of record — autonomous, proactive AI that listens to policy changes and notifies users. ^[extracted]

## Key Contributions

- Coined the term [[concepts/ai-sideshow]] with Jeremy Silva — the anti-pattern of treating AI as a bolt-on, non-integrated feature set. ^[extracted]
- Demonstrated the crawl-walk-run framework through Workday's real product evolution from KB content tools to autonomous agent system of record. ^[extracted]
- Advocated for "AI products that need not announce themselves as AI" — solving the customer problem better is the true measure of success. ^[extracted]
