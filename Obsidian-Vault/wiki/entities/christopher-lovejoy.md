# Christopher Lovejoy

Medical doctor turned AI engineer. Trained and worked as a clinical physician for eight years before transitioning to building AI systems that incorporate medical domain expertise. ^[extracted]

## Career

Spent seven years building AI systems with medical domain expertise at multiple startups:

- **Seracare** — health tech startup doing tech-enabled home care, recently hit $500M ARR ^[extracted]
- **Anterior** — currently works at this New York-based clinician-led company providing clinical reasoning tools to automate and accelerate health insurance and healthcare administration. Their AI system serves health insurance providers covering about 50 million lives in the U.S. ^[extracted]

## Philosophy

His core bet is that for vertical AI applications, the system built for incorporating domain insights is far more important than model sophistication or pipeline complexity. He argues that frontier models already reason well enough — the real bottleneck is giving the model the specific industry context for a particular customer and enabling rapid iteration with domain experts. ^[inferred]

He presented this framework at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 in a talk titled "Make your LLM app a Domain Expert: How to Build an Expert System." ^[extracted]
