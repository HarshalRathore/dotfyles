# Jan Čern

Jan Čern is the founder and CEO of [[entities/apify|Apify]], a marketplace of 5,000 AI-ready tools called Actors. He spoke at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on the rise of the agentic economy on the shoulders of MCP.

## Background

Apify originated in the web scraping industry and has evolved into a platform for AI agent tool distribution. Under Čern's leadership, Apify built an MCP server that allows AI agents to discover and call any of the 5,000 Actors on the marketplace. ^[extracted]

## Key Views

Čern argues that general intelligence will emerge through the interaction of multiple entities (agents) pursuing their own goals, not through ever-larger single models. He identifies MCP's **tool discovery** as the "killer feature" that enables agents to dynamically find and use tools at runtime. ^[extracted]

He identifies a critical gap: agents cannot yet independently find counterparts or purchase services. Until agents can discover and acquire tools on their own — without human developers facilitating access — we will not reach a higher level of agentic intelligence. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Čern presented "The Rise of the Agentic Economy on the Shoulders of MCP," arguing that:

1. Intelligence emerges from interaction of autonomous units (neurons, market participants, employees)
2. The same principle applies to computing systems — general intelligence emerges from agent-to-agent interaction
3. MCP provides the missing communication fabric
4. Agents still need human facilitation to access tools — this is the critical gap to solve
5. Central identity/payment providers (Coinbase X402, Stripe, Vertifier) are one solution path
