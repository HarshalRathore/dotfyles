# Freeplay

Freeplay is a company that "exists to help solve a lot of these operational problems and help companies ship great AI products." ^[extracted] Their product lead, Jeremy, co-presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 with [[entities/chris-hernandez|Chris Hernandez]] of [[entities/chime|Chime]] on the [[concepts/build-operate-divide]] — the gap between AI product vision and operational reality.

## Perspective from the Talk

Freeplay's experience working "across dozens of different enterprise teams" informed the key observations of the talk: ^[extracted]

- Companies build prototypes and ship V1 but hit a [[concepts/quality-chasm-in-ai]] going to V2
- Product quality is a direct function of iteration speed through the [[concepts/ai-iteration-loop]]
- The emerging [[concepts/ai-quality-lead]] role is seen at companies succeeding in Gen AI
- Human-in-the-loop is a "feedback engine," not just a safeguard ^[extracted]

## AI Sideshow Talk

Jeremy also co-presented a second talk at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 with [[entities/eliza-cabrera|Eliza Cabrera]] of [[entities/workday|Workday]] — "Build Dynamic Products, and Stop the AI Sideshow." This talk identified the [[concepts/ai-sideshow]] anti-pattern (centralized AI strategy running as a sidecar to core product strategy) and introduced the [[concepts/dynamic-products|crawl-walk-run framework]] for progressively integrating AI. ^[extracted]
