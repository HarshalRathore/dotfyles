# Google

Google (Alphabet Inc.) is a technology conglomerate and the parent company of [[entities/google-deepmind|Google DeepMind]]. It operates in search, cloud computing, AI research, and consumer applications.

## AI Research

Google DeepMind, formed from the merger of Google Brain and DeepMind, operates under Google/Alphabet and is responsible for the [[entities/gemini|Gemini]] model family. Google has processed a 50x increase in AI inference through its servers year-over-year. ^[extracted]

## Notable Alumni

[[entities/stefania-druma|Stefania Druga]] is a former research scientist at Google and a three-time AI conference speaker, featured at the AI Education Summit announced at AIEF2025. ^[extracted]

## Gemini 2.5 Pro

In March 2025, Google released Gemini 2.5 Pro, which [[entities/simon-willison|Simon Willison]] described as having a "pretty frickin' good pelican" on the SVG benchmark — the bicycle went "cyberpunk" but represented clear progress. ^[extracted]

- Cost: ~4.5 cents for the pelican SVG (very affordable)
- Part of the broader Gemini family developed by Google DeepMind

## AI Inference Scale

Google has processed a **50× year-over-year increase** in AI inference through its servers, according to Logan Kilpatrick at AIEF2025. This massive scale growth reflects the accelerating adoption of AI across Google's services. ^[extracted]
