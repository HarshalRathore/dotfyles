## Rick Blalock

Rick Blalock is the founder of [[entities/agentuity|Agentuity]], a platform for deploying and managing AI agents. He is based in South Florida and previously built qualitative research agent systems, which exposed him firsthand to the deployment challenges that plague agent developers.

Blalock presented "Conquering Agent Chaos" at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, where he described the common pain points of deploying agents — serverless timeouts, statefulness mismatches, and gateway wiring issues — based on conversations with students and professors at the University of Florida and his own experience building agent systems on serverless infrastructure.

He is a strong advocate for framework-agnostic agent deployment, noting that Agentuity internally runs 50+ agents using multiple frameworks (CrewAI, LangChain, Pydantic, Vercel AI SDK) that communicate via internal networking.
