# Sprout Social

Sprout Social is a public SaaS company providing a unified platform for social media management, engagement, analytics, and customer care. The company is in the process of an AI-first transformation, evolving from sprinkling AI features into its product toward rethinking how it plans, builds, and delivers value through an AI lens. ^[extracted]

## AI Transformation Approach

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Sprout Social shared a candid, real-time account of their AI transformation journey. Their framework organizes the transformation across three dimensions: ^[extracted]

1. **Strategy** — evolving from AI-enhanced to AI-first, reimagining what experiences are possible rather than just adding intelligence to existing workflows
2. **Ways of Working** — shifting from deterministic roadmaps to ambiguity-embracing discovery, treating process as a product, and moving from speed to smart velocity
3. **People** — investing in T-shaped talent, building AI fluency across the entire organization, and developing pathfinders who can navigate uncertainty

## Key Practices

- **Ritualized discovery** — building time into planning cycles for experimentation, hackathons, and learning in visible, actionable formats ^[extracted]
- **MVPs for learning** — shipping to validate direction, not just to launch quickly ^[extracted]
- **Process as product** — evaluating every process against outcomes; cutting or retooling what doesn't create clarity or unblock teams ^[extracted]
- **Smart velocity** — moving fast with purpose, not speed for its own sake ^[extracted]
