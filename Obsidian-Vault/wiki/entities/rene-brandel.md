# Rene Brandel

Rene Brandel is the CEO and co-founder of [[entities/casco|Casco]], a [[entities/yc|YC-backed]] company focused on [[concepts/red-teaming|red-teaming]] AI agents and applications.

## Background

Rene previously worked at [[entities/aws|AWS]] on AI agents. Ten years before founding Casco, he built Voice2Code — a system where he could speak commands and it would generate blog posts and websites — and won Europe's largest hackathon with it. ^[extracted]

## Career Transition

Two months before speaking at AIEF2025, Rene quit AWS and started Casco from a garage with his co-founder. They got into Y Combinator and produced the second highest upvoted launch post in YC history. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

At the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Rene presented "How we hacked YC Spring 2025 batch's AI agents," demonstrating that 7 out of 16 agents in the batch could be compromised within 30 minutes each. ^[extracted]
