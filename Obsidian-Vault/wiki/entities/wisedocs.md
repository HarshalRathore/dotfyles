# WiseDocs

WiseDocs is a **verticalized solutions / services company** focused on AI for document processing. It was cited by [[entities/denys-linkov|Denys Linkov]] as an example of a company in the middle of the technology spectrum — neither a pure technology company nor a tech-enabled company. ^[extracted]

## Company Type Classification

In Denys Linkov's framework from AIEF2025, companies fall on a spectrum:

1. **Technology companies** — technology IS the core value proposition (big tech, startups)
2. **Verticalized solutions / services companies** — domain-specific AI products (WiseDocs, Palantir)
3. **Tech-enabled companies** — core product is not technology but benefits from good tech (banks, retailers, SMBs)

WiseDocs sits in category 2: it provides domain-specific AI solutions rather than general-purpose technology. ^[extracted]

## Implications

As a verticalized solutions company, WiseDocs faces the "middle" challenge: either everything goes right or everything goes awfully poorly. This creates different hiring and technology decisions compared to pure technology companies or tech-enabled companies. ^[inferred]
