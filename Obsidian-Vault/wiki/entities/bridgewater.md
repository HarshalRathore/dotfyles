# Bridgewater

Bridgewater is a hedge fund and trading systems company where [[entities/adam-behrens|Adam Behrens]] previously worked on their trading system and market systems. ^[extracted]

## Relevance to Agentic Commerce

Behrens draws an analogy between financial market systems and agentic commerce market design. In finance, third-party institutions and market makers manage the differences between buyers and sellers who have complex, changing, and often conflicting preferences. Behrens argues agentic commerce needs to move to a similar model rather than naively trusting the information given to agents. ^[extracted]
