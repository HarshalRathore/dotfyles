# Rafal Willinski

Rafal Willinski is an engineer at [[entities/zapier|Zapier]] who presented on Zapier's evaluation lessons for AI agents at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, co-presenting with Vitor Balocco. ^[extracted]

## Key Contributions

At AIEF2025, Willinski articulated Zapier's two-year learning journey building AI agents for non-technical users, covering instrumentation practices, feedback mining (both explicit and implicit), LLM Ops tooling, and the eval testing pyramid. ^[extracted]
