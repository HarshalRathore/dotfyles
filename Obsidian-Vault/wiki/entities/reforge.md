# Reforge

Reforge is a company that helps product teams across the technology industry improve their craft through expert-led courses, community knowledge, and AI agents. Founded by [[entities/brian-balfour|Brian Balfour]], the company has been operating for approximately 10 years as of 2025.

## Scale and Reach

- Community of over 400 experts on the front lines of product development ^[extracted]
- Has helped over 100,000 professionals ^[extracted]
- Clients include major technology companies ^[extracted]

## Products

Reforge started with 40+ expert-led courses, including AI-focused courses, then began encoding knowledge into AI agents: ^[extracted]

- **Reforge Insights** — An AI product researcher agent that helps teams decode best practices
- **Compass** — An AI project manager that automates low-level, low-value product management tasks
- Two additional AI products planned for later 2025 ^[extracted]

## AI Strategy

Reforge collaborated with [[entities/ravi-meta|Ravi Meta]], former CPO at Tinder, to create an AI strategy course that formed the basis of the [[concepts/ai-lego-blocks-framework|AI Lego Blocks Framework]] presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025. ^[extracted]
