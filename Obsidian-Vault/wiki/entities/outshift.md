# OutShift

OutShift is Cisco's internal incubation group focused on emerging technologies. Its charter is to evaluate emerging technologies and determine how they can accelerate the roadmaps of Cisco's traditional business units. ^[extracted]

## Focus Areas

OutShift concentrates on two primary technology domains:

1. **Artificial Intelligence** — applied AI to traditional networking and enterprise products
2. **Quantum Technology** — including quantum networking research

## Operating Model

OutShift follows a structured product incubation process:

1. **Customer interviews** — deep discovery of customer problems
2. **Prototyping** — rapid proof-of-concept development
3. **A/B testing** — empirical validation against control groups
4. **MVP delivery** — production-ready minimum viable product
5. **Graduation** — successful products transfer to Cisco's main business units

This model was demonstrated through the network knowledge graph project presented by [[entities/ola-mabadeje|Ola Mabadeje]] at AIEF2025, which originated from customer pain around change management failures and progressed through incubation to a production system. ^[extracted]
