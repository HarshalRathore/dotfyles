# Magi 1

Magi 1 is an upcoming AI model from [[entities/morph|Morph]], announced at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 by [[entities/jesse-han|Jesse Han]]. It is being "trained from the ground up" to use InfiniBranch for reasoning time branching and verified reasoning, positioned as an agent fully embodied inside a cloud that can "move at the speed of light." ^[extracted]

## Development

- Early checkpoints of Magi 1 powered the demos shown at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 ^[extracted]
- Development is led by [[entities/christian-segeti|Christian Segeti]], Chief Scientist at Morph and former xAI co-founder ^[extracted]
- Full release targeted for Q1 2026 ^[extracted]

## Capabilities (from talk)

The demos demonstrated early Magi 1 checkpoints performing: ^[extracted]

- [[concepts/reasoning-time-branching|Reasoning time branching]] — Branching computational environments during reasoning to explore multiple solution paths
- Parallel sub-agent delegation — Spawning agents to explore different approaches concurrently
- Verified reasoning — Using verification conditions to evaluate and select solutions
