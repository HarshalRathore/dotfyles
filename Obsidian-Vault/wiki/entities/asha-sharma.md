# Asha Sharma

Corporate Vice President leading [[entities/microsoft|Microsoft]]'s Core AI Platform. She has spent 15 years building machine learning products and now leads the platform empowering every developer to build AI agents. ^[extracted]

## AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 Keynote

Sharma delivered the keynote "Spark to System: Building the Open Agentic Web" at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, articulating [[concepts/agentic-web|Microsoft's vision for the open agentic web]] — a world where [[concepts/agentic-architecture|agents]] interact with tools, models, and other agents across any cloud, company, or device. The talk introduced several patterns now central to Microsoft's AI platform:
- [[concepts/peer-programming]] — Evolution from pair to peer programming with AI as teammate
- [[concepts/agent-factory]] — From software factory to agent factory
- [[concepts/signal-loop-ai]] — Signal loop for fine-tuning on real interactions
- [[concepts/agentic-rag]] — Multi-shot iterative RAG
- [[concepts/local-first-ai-platform]] — Local as a core platform capability
