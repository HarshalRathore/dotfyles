# 11x

11x is a company building **digital workers** for go-to-market (GTM) organizations. They position their products as AI-powered replacements for traditional human GTM roles.

## Products

- **Alice** — An AI Sales Development Representative (SDR) that sources leads, sends personalized emails, and books meetings
- **Julian** — A voice agent for GTM conversations
- More workers are in development

## Technology Approach

11x builds its agents on a RAG-based knowledge architecture. Alice's knowledge base uses a five-step pipeline (parsing → chunking → storage → retrieval → visualization) with third-party vendors for parsing and a deep research agent framework for retrieval.
