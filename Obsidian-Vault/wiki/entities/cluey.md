# Cluey

Cluey is an AI cheating service for technical interviews. ^[extracted]

## Background

Founded by an ex-Columbia University student, Cluey has raised a $5.3M funding round and is heading toward $1M in annual recurring revenue. ^[extracted]

## Business

The company commercializes the use of AI coding assistants during technical interviews — essentially selling a service that helps candidates cheat on coding assessments using AI tools. Its existence is cited as evidence that AI cheating in interviews has become a mainstream, monetizable phenomenon. ^[extracted]
