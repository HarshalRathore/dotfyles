# Gorkem Yurtseven

Gorkem Yurtseven is a founder and key figure at File.ai (FAL), a company he describes as a "generative media platform." He has been working in the generative media space for several years and spoke at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on the state of generative media.

## Background

Yurtseven was working from home at the end of COVID when Sam Altman began tweeting DALL·E 2 images in January 2022 — an experience he describes as one of the biggest "WTF moments" of his life. He recognized OpenAI's lead at the time but also understood from prior AI waves that such leads tend to close quickly.^[extracted]

## Views on Generative Media

He draws a sharp distinction between the marginal cost of *creation* (which generative media is driving toward zero) and the marginal cost of *creativity* (which remains valuable). He predicts video generation will become 100–250× larger than image generation, citing compute intensity (20×), engagement (5×), and broader industry applicability.^[inferred]

## Company: File.ai (FAL)

File.ai operates as a generative media platform with an inference engine, partnering with both open-source and closed-source model providers. Yurtseven uses the company's revenue data as a proxy for the broader market, noting video model usage grew from near-zero to ~30% of revenue within six months.^[extracted]
