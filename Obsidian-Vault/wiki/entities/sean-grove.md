# Sean Grove

Alignment researcher at [[entities/openai|OpenAI]]. Presented "The New Code" at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, arguing that written specifications — not code — are the most valuable artifact in AI engineering.

## Key Contributions

- Articulated the [[concepts/specifications-as-code|Specifications as Code]] thesis: specifications are the new source code, and the scarce skill in the AI era is writing specifications that fully capture intent and values. ^[extracted]
- Part of the [[concepts/deliberative-alignment|Deliberative Alignment]] team at OpenAI, developing techniques to embed specifications into model weights. ^[extracted]
- Advocate for treating prompts as source code (rather than ephemeral communication), inverting the current vibe coding practice of keeping generated code and discarding prompts. ^[extracted]
- Proposed the "Integrated Thought Clarifier" vision for future IDEs — tools that detect ambiguity in specifications and ask authors to clarify. ^[extracted]
