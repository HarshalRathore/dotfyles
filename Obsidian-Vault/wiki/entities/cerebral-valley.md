# Cerebral Valley

A large AI community based in the Bay Area, described by the AI Engineer World's Fair organizers as "the largest AI community in the world" and "legends right here in the Bay Area for running hackathons." ^[extracted]

## Event Partnership

Cerebral Valley partnered with the AI Engineer World's Fair to organize the first official AI Engineer hackathon. From 500 applicants, 160 engineers were selected, building 46 projects over 48 hours. Three finalists were selected to present on the main stage. ^[extracted]
