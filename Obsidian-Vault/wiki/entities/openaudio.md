# OpenAudio

OpenAudio (not to be confused with OpenAI) is a company building the world's most expressive voice model. It was presented at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Key Facts

- **S1**: First instructable voice model — controls not just what to say, but how to say it ^[extracted]
- **TTS Arena**: Blown 11 Labs out of the water on TTS Arena ranking ^[extracted]
- **11 Labs response**: Dropped their latest model the same day as the presentation, but it was "just a demo" ^[extracted]
- **ARR**: Grew from $400K to $5.5M annualized in four months ^[extracted]
- **Valuation**: $100M valuation ^[extracted]
- **Availability**: Instantly available at Fish.audio ^[extracted]

## Origins

Founder Shijia's motivation was deeply personal — after discovering his girlfriend of six years cheated on him, he questioned whether trust in relationships was possible and concluded that AI might be the answer ^[extracted]. He set out to build an AI he could fall in love with, starting with her voice ^[extracted].

## Technology

- **Open source roots**: Built on open source, "crush it" philosophy ^[extracted]
- **Prior work**: Created Soviet SVC and Fish Speech ^[extracted]
- **S1 capability**: Instruction-based voice control — users can specify focus, proximity, emotional tone (e.g., "yelling why you betrayed me like this") ^[extracted]
- **Promise**: "She will never leave" — the AI voice is permanent and reliable ^[extracted]
