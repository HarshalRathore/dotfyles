# Barr Yaron

Investment partner at **Amplify**, where he invests in technical founders, including companies built by and for AI engineers.

## AIEF2025 Presentation

Presented early findings from the **2025 State of AI Engineering Survey** (500 respondents) at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, covering model usage patterns, fine-tuning practices, multimodal adoption gaps, agent deployment, monitoring practices, and evaluation challenges in the AI engineering community.
