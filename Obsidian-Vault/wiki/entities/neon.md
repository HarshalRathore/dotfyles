# Neon

[[entities/ivan-burazin|Ivan Burazin]] cited [[entities/neon|Neon]] as a company doing API-first design well for AI agents. Neon is a serverless PostgreSQL database provider where all key agent-accessible functionality is exposed via API.

## Why Cited

In Burazin's taxonomy of AX best practices, API-first design is "the most critical" principle. Neon is cited alongside [[entities/etlify|Etlify]] and [[entities/supabase|Supabase]] as companies where "all the key things that an agent needs to use their services can be accessed via an API." ^[extracted]
