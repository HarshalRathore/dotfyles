# Laurie Voss

Laurie Voss is the VP of Developer Relations at [[entities/llamaindex|LlamaIndex]]. She hosted Day 2 of the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, introducing keynotes including Logan Kilpatrick's Gemini 2.5 Pro announcement and Benjamin Dunphy's AI Education Summit reveal.

## Role at LlamaIndex

As VP of Developer Relations at LlamaIndex, Laurie works on community engagement, developer advocacy, and ecosystem growth for the LlamaIndex platform. She is also a co-founder of the AI Engineer World's Fair conference. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Day 2 Host

Laurie opened Day 2 of AIEF2025 with introductions for the day's keynotes, including:
- Logan Kilpatrick from Google DeepMind on Gemini 2.5 Pro
- The founder of Docker on making agents more reliable
- Benjamin Dunphy with a special announcement

She also shared a Pride Month message, noting that June in San Francisco is Pride Month and welcoming attendees from the LGBTQ community. ^[extracted]
