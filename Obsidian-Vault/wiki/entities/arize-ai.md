# Arize AI

**Arize AI** is described as the largest AI evals company in the space, specializing in observability and evaluation infrastructure for LLM-powered applications. ^[extracted]

## Clients

Arize works with top AI teams including:

- **Reddit** — AI-powered features at scale
- **Duolingo** — runs approximately 20 evals per trace, representing massive eval scale ^[extracted]

## Focus Areas

Arize's platform covers the full observability-to-evals pipeline:

1. **Observability** — traces, spans, analytics for LLM applications
2. **Evaluation** — structured evals across the full spectrum from code-based heuristics to LLM-as-judge
3. **Continuous monitoring** — real-time output monitoring and quality drift detection

The company's business model is built on seeing what everyone is building — giving them a unique vantage point on AI engineering patterns, pain points, and productionization strategies. ^[extracted]
