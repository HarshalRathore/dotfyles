# Jaspreet Singh

Jaspreet Singh is a senior staff engineer at [[entities/intuit|Intuit]], working on Gen AI for TurboTax. ^[extracted]

He presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on how Intuit uses LLMs to explain taxes to millions of taxpayers, covering their proprietary GenOS platform, evaluation strategies, model selection, and fine-tuning experiments at massive scale (44 million tax returns processed for tax year 2023). ^[extracted]
