**Brainbox AI** is a building operating system company that helps decarbonize the built environment. They manage HVAC systems across tens of thousands of buildings in the United States and Canada.

## AI Agent for Decarbonization

**[[entities/caylent|Caylent]]** built an AI agent for Brainbox AI to help optimize building operations for decarbonization. The agent manages HVAC systems to reduce greenhouse gas emissions while maintaining occupant comfort.

The solution was significant enough to be named among the **Times 100 best inventions of the year**. ^[extracted]

## Technology

- Building operating system for HVAC management
- AI agent for optimization and decarbonization
- Manages tens of thousands of buildings across North America

## Impact

- Drastically reduces greenhouse emissions from building HVAC systems
- Recognized by TIME magazine as one of the 100 best inventions of the year
