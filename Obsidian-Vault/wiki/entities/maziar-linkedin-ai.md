# Maziar (LinkedIn AI)

Maziar is an AI engineer at LinkedIn who co-presented the 360Brew project at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 alongside Hamed.

## 360Brew Presentation

Maziar covered the model building pipeline during the 360Brew presentation, explaining the brewing process from open-source base models through upcycling, continuous pre-training, fine-tuning, instruction fine-tuning, and alignment to produce Brew XL (150B parameters), then distillation down to a 3B production model. ^[extracted]
