# Julian

Julian is a voice agent built by [[entities/11x|11x]] as part of their digital worker platform for go-to-market organizations. He operates alongside **Alice**, the AI SDR, as a complementary voice-based GTM tool.

## Positioning

Julian represents 11x's expansion beyond text-based AI agents into voice. While Alice handles email outreach at scale, Julian is designed for voice conversations — likely including cold calls, follow-up calls, and voice-based lead engagement.
