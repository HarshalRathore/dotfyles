# Ben Stein

**Ben Stein** is a founder of [[entities/teammates|Teammates]], a platform for designing and managing an entire digital workforce of AI agents. He speaks and writes about product management for AI-native products. ^[extracted]

## Background

Ben wears his product manager hat when discussing Teammates' approach to building AI agents. He describes Teammates as building "two ticks up from" agents — focusing on the experience, interaction patterns, and identity of humans and computers working together. ^[extracted]

At Teammates, every customer gets to design their own teammates with custom avatars and personalities. Ben's own teammate, Stacy Hand, is an L3 engineer on his team who lives inside collaboration tools (Google Workspace, Slack), sends emails, and communicates in a "Gen Alpha" style. ^[extracted]

## Key Ideas

Ben's talk at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 ("Shipping Products When You Don't Know What They Can Do") introduced the concept of thinking in **affordances** rather than specific requirements when building AI products, and the idea that **behavior is emergent** — product people's job is to discover functionality by providing the right building blocks. ^[extracted]

He identified two core reasons why product management must transform for the AI era: (1) LLMs are inherently opaque — we don't know what the foundation can do, and (2) customer expectations are boundless — a free text input invites unlimited use cases. ^[extracted]
