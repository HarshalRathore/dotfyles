# Chin Keong Lam

Chin Keong Lam is the founder and CEO of [[entities/patho-ai|Patho AI]]. His company started with an NSF SBIR grant researching LLM-driven discovery applications, then expanded to building expert AI systems for enterprise clients. ^[extracted]

## Background

- Founder and CEO of Patho AI (est. ~2023)
- Received SBIR grant from the National Science Foundation for LLM-driven discovery research
- Background in IoT and Node.js (Node-RED / N8N workflow automation) ^[extracted]

## Work

- Developed the **Wisdom Graph** state machine model for expert AI systems that goes beyond RAG
- Advocates for **KAG (Knowledge Augmented Generation)** — using structured knowledge graphs with function-call-based knowledge cards for precise quantitative reasoning
- Builds multi-agent advisory systems for enterprise clients (competitive analysis, research and advisory roles) ^[extracted]

## Key Ideas

- KAG doesn't just retrieve — it understands. Structured knowledge graphs enable complex multi-hop reasoning that vector RAG cannot match. ^[extracted]
- Wisdom isn't static storage — it's an active, self-improving system powered by continuous feedback loops. ^[extracted]
- Recommends hybrid graph extraction: automated LLM extraction followed by expert pruning and taxonomy refinement. ^[extracted]
- Uses N8N for rapid prototyping of agent workflows before migrating to lighter frameworks. ^[extracted]
