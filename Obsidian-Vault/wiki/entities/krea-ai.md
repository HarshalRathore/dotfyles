# Krea.ai

Krea.ai is an AI company focused on generative media, multimedia, and multimodal generation. Founded by Diego Rodriguez, the company works at the intersection of generative AI and human perception — building tools that help creatives express themselves through AI-generated content.

## Focus

Krea.ai's work centers on perceptual evaluation: developing evaluation methods that measure what humans actually perceive in AI-generated media rather than relying on proxy metrics like FID scores. This represents a shift from computationally convenient evaluation to perception-aligned evaluation. ^[extracted]
