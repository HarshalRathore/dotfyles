# CoFrame

CoFrame is a marketing technology company that uses AI to personalize every customer experience on websites. It was presented at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Key Facts

- **OpenAI partnership**: The only marketing tech company partnered directly with OpenAI to date ^[extracted]
- **OpenAI team name**: OpenAI called their team "Cracked" ^[extracted]
- **Results**: Made $20M for Europe's largest travel company in a few weeks ^[extracted]
- **Results**: Increased click-through rate for India's largest company ($400B enterprise) in a few weeks ^[extracted]
- **Founder track record**: Co-founder Josh Brown previously scaled a company to over $2B ^[extracted]

## Product Philosophy

CoFrame's thesis is that "the internet is dead" because it's not adaptive, personal, or truly living. Websites are "one size fits all." CoFrame gives websites "a life of their own" by providing every single customer experience with its own AI growth team ^[extracted].
