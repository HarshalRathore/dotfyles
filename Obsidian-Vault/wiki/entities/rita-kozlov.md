# Rita Kozlov

Rita Kozlov is the VP of Product for Cloudflare's developer platform, overseeing Workers and Durable Objects. She spoke at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on "Building Agents (the hard parts!)."

## Role and Focus

Rita's work centers on making it as easy as possible for developers to bring ideas to life — from writing the first line of code to deploying to production and scaling to millions of users. Her domain spans Cloudflare's developer services including functions, storage, compute, and AI inference.

## AIEF2025 Talk: Building Agents (the hard parts!)

At the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Rita presented a four-component framework for building agents (client, AI reasoning, workflows, tools), discussed MCP as the emerging standard for exposing APIs to LLMs, and introduced Cloudflare's Agents SDK as a production-ready toolkit that abstracts away OAuth, transport, and state management via Durable Objects.
