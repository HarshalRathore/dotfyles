# Amanda Askell

Amanda Askell is an AI researcher at [[entities/anthropic|Anthropic]] known for leading the design of Claude's character and personality. Her approach — designing character as a first-class practice rather than controlling individual outputs — was cited by [[entities/craig-wattrus|Craig Wattrus]] of [[entities/flatfile|Flatfile]] as the inspiration for the [[concepts/character-coach-approach|character coach approach]] to AI agent design.

## Influence

Wattrus describes listening to Askell talk to Lex Fridman about building Claude's character as a turning point: he realized he had been treating agent behavior feedback like copy editing (controlling outputs, policing word choice) instead of coaching character. Askell's framing of character design as deliberate, systematic practice led him to build the "chat tuner" tool and shift his methodology. ^[extracted]
