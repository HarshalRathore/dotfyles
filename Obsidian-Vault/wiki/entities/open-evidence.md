# Open Evidence

Open Evidence is an AI platform that helps doctors stay current with medical research at the point of clinical decision-making. It requires clinician credentials (medical ID number) to access, ensuring appropriate use. ^[extracted]

## Traction

- Reaches 1/3 of US doctors weekly ^[extracted]
- Average user uses it daily ^[extracted]
- Clinician-only access control

Open Evidence illustrates the [[concepts/ai-leapfrog-effect|AI leapfrog effect]] in healthcare — a highly regulated, conservative industry adopting AI faster than anticipated. It also exemplifies the "[[concepts/cursor-for-x|Cursor for X]]" playbook applied to medical knowledge work. ^[inferred]
