# Stephen Chin

Stephen Chin is a speaker at [[entities/neo4j|Neo4j]] who presented at AIEF2025 alongside [[entities/andreas-kolleger|Andreas Kollegger]] on the "Top Ten Challenges to Reach AGI" talk, using sci-fi movie memes to frame key open questions in AGI development. ^[extracted]

He also presented on GraphRAG patterns alongside Michael Hunger and [[entities/jesús-barrasa|Jesús Barrasa]] at AIEF2025, introducing the GraphRAG Patterns Catalog and framing GraphRAG as a way to breathe new life into the AI ecosystem. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk: Top Ten Challenges to Reach AGI

Chin and Kollegger framed AGI challenges through eight sci-fi movie memes: Memento (prompt engineering amnesia), Skynet (autonomous system unintended consequences), The Matrix (agent simulation hypothesis), Monsters Inc. (emotions as bug vs feature), The Terminator (time travel), Star Wars (language/culture understanding), Star Trek (multi-agent hive mind assimilation), and Deep Thought (not knowing the right questions). ^[extracted]
