# Sid Bendre

Co-founder of Olive (formerly Aleve), a New York City–based consumer software company.^[extracted] Along with his co-founders, Sid scaled a portfolio of virally successful AI-powered products to $6M in ARR profitably, with over half a billion views across social media, using a team of just four people.^[extracted]

## Background

Sid and his co-founders launched Quizzer AI (later QuizBuddy) on January 26, 2023 — an AI mobile app that went viral on TikTok, generating a million views overnight and 10,000 users in under 30 hours.^[extracted] In the early days they cycled through 10 different accounts to access the beta Codex model, which was being used as a general-purpose conversation model before being sunset for abuse.^[extracted] OpenAI directly reached out to several of their accounts as top Codex users.^[extracted]

In fall 2023, the team moved to New York City and ran a "back to school" campaign of man-on-the-street videos at prestigious US colleges, hitting their first $1M in ARR and achieving profitability within nine months.^[extracted] A spring 2024 campaign reached #6 in the education charts alongside Duolingo, Photomath, and GoStudent.^[extracted]

## Olive / Aleve

Sid co-founded Olive (originally named Aleve), which builds a family of iconic consumer software products aimed at helping people live better, more fulfilling, and productive lives.^[extracted] The company operates with a team of four and has launched three products: QuizBuddy (education), Unstuck AI (study companion — reached 1M users in under 9 weeks with quarter-billion social views), and a third product launched in stealth outside the education domain that reached 1,000+ users in three weeks and is already profitable.^[extracted]

## Philosophy

Sid articulates "The New Lean Startup" — a playbook based on three pillars: operating principles (hiring, profit-first, KPI alignment, continuous refinement, super tools, compounding learnings), organizational structure (harvester/cultivator model inspired by Palantir), and AI tooling augmentation.^[extracted] He emphasizes that AI tools make a 10Xer into a 100Xer rather than filling gaps in weaker hires.^[extracted]
