# Sahil Yadav

Sahil Yadav is an AI practitioner who has spent 10 years deploying AI across health monitoring, industrial IoT, and telecom network automation. He is affiliated with [[entities/telemetrak|Telemetrak]]. At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, he co-presented with [[entities/hariharan-ganesan|Hariharan Ganesan]] on the topic of trustworthy AI inference in enterprise and mission-critical environments. ^[extracted]

## Background

- 10+ years deploying AI in high-stakes industries ^[extracted]
- Previously worked at [[entities/guardhat|Guardhat]], a worker safety company with an AI-driven IoT platform ^[extracted]
- His experience at Guardhat (particularly the GPS drift false-positive problem) informed the development of the X-TOPS framework ^[extracted]

## Key Contributions

- Articulated the gap between AI adoption (78%) and AI governance (11%) ^[extracted]
- Identified and described **silent AI failures** as a class of high-impact, hard-to-detect failures ^[extracted]
- Defined the [[concepts/ai-trust-pillars|three pillars of trustworthy AI]]: explainability, traceability, guardrails ^[extracted]
- Contributed to the [[concepts/x-tops|X-TOPS]] framework for operationalizing AI trust ^[extracted]
