# Mani Khanuja

AWS speaker and advocate focused on data strategy for generative AI applications. Presented "Data is Your Differentiator: Building Secure and Tailored AI Systems" at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.

## Core Philosophy

Mani's central thesis is that **data is the foundation of generative AI applications** — not the model, not the prompt, but the data. The data represents the company, brand, and organization, and generative AI requires special treatment of data based on application type and business requirements. ^[extracted]

## Key Contributions

- Framed three distinct AI application archetypes (customer-facing agent, employee chatbot, brand marketing) with different data requirements
- Mapped the complete data pipeline for AI agents: prompts, instructions, context, model data
- Positioned Amazon Bedrock as the comprehensive platform for responsible, data-driven generative AI development
- Emphasized that organizations can no longer afford data silos when building generative AI systems ^[extracted]
