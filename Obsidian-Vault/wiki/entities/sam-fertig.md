# Sam Fertig

Sam Fertig is a Principal Forward-Deployed Engineer at [[entities/windsurf|Windsurf]]. ^[extracted]

## Background

Sam works at Windsurf, where he engages in forward-deployed engineering — working directly with customers and the product to solve real-world problems. He speaks publicly about Windsurf's approach to AI coding, particularly around context philosophy and how Windsurf gets to know its users. ^[extracted]

He notably presented "The Eyes Are The (Context) Window to The Soul: How Windsurf Gets to Know You" at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, articulating Windsurf's two-pillar approach to context: what context to provide and how much context to include. ^[extracted]

## Personality Notes

Sam describes himself as someone who loves Slack emojis, sometimes wears turtlenecks, and has been known to exaggerate his title (he notes he's "just a deployed engineer for now," not principal). ^[extracted] These details were used as a framing device in his talk to illustrate how superficial observations gain meaning through context and framing. ^[inferred]
