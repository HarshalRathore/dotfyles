# Sean DuBois

Sean DuBois works on WebRTC at OpenAI. He is one of the people most responsible for making WebRTC accessible to everyone.^[extracted]

## Background

Before joining OpenAI, Sean worked on the Go implementation of WebRTC called Pion.^[extracted] At OpenAI, he works on the real-time API and 1-800 ChatGPT (the phone-callable version of ChatGPT).^[extracted]

## Views on Voice AI

Sean articulates a strong vision for voice as the next major computing interface:

- Voice will be the core building block of the next generation of user interfaces for the generative AI era^[extracted]
- Voice is "the next bicycle for the mind" — freeing computing from requiring hands^[extracted]
- We are in late 2007 equivalent for voice computing (like early iPhone era)^[extracted]
- People already spend hours talking to computers for coding, brainstorming, coaching, therapy^[extracted]

## Community Engagement

Sean is active on Discord, Twitter, and LinkedIn, and encourages builders at all skill levels to connect with him and the OpenAI team about voice AI and WebRTC projects.^[extracted]

## WebRTC Advocacy

Sean has worked with companies using WebRTC for critical applications including surgery over the internet and autonomous vehicles in the field. He describes WebRTC as "the standard language of the real-time world" and believes it enables building conversational intelligence on top of solved networking problems.^[extracted]

He emphasizes that WebRTC can be embedded in any app — websites, iOS, Android — and connections "just work" on almost any real-world network.^[extracted]
