# Swix

[[entities/ivan-burazin|Ivan Burazin]] mentioned [[entities/swix|Swix]] as someone who "coerced" him into giving the AIEF2025 talk on Agent Experience, arguing that "agent experience doesn't exist — it's just a wrap around developer experience." ^[extracted]

Burazin disagrees: if you're not thinking about how an agent can autonomously do its task, you are just porting developer experience to agents. True AX requires the autonomy dimension. ^[inferred]
