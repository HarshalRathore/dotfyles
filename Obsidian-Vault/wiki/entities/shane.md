# Shane

**Shane** is a member of **Google DeepMind's Omni team** who presented at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2026. ^[extracted]

## Contributions at WF2026

Shane discussed: ^[extracted]

1. **Model progression framework** — the typical evolution path of language models from pre-training through instruction tuning, reasoning capability, and test-time scaling
2. **Modality gap** — the disconnect between how machines perceive audio/video and how humans experience them
3. **Media model evaluation** — the challenge of evaluating multimodal models, noting that human judgment still plays a large role
