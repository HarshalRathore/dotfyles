# Nina Lopatina

Nina Lopatina is an NLP and language modeling engineer with approximately 7-8 years of experience in the field, spanning back to the BERT era when BERT itself was considered a large language model. ^[extracted] She joined **[[entities/contextual-ai|Contextual AI]]** and was in her fourth week at the company at the time of her AIEF2025 presentation. ^[extracted]

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, she co-presented with Rajiv Shah on building production-ready RAG agents using Contextual's platform. She led the hands-on demonstration portion, walking attendees through document ingestion, agent setup, and query execution against a RAG system built on Contextual's managed service architecture. ^[extracted]

Her presentation covered practical aspects of RAG implementation including quantitative reasoning over financial tables and handling spurious correlation data as a stress test for retrieval systems. ^[extracted]
