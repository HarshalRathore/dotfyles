# Ilan Bigio

Developer Experience team member at [[entities/openai|OpenAI]]. Focuses on early testing of new products and new directions for the OpenAI API.

## Role

As a member of OpenAI's Developer Experience team, Ilan works directly with companies helping them deploy AI use cases and applications. He does early testing on new products and API features, serving as a bridge between OpenAI's product development and external developers. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Ilan delivered the talk "RFT, DPO, SFT: Fine-tuning with OpenAI" as part of the Model Maxing series. The talk covered: ^[extracted]

- **Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT)** — direct example imitation for domain adaptation
- **Direct Preference Optimization (DPO)** — learning preference deltas between positive and negative outputs
- **Reinforcement Fine-tuning (RFT)** — chain-of-thought reasoning training with grader feedback
- **Live demos** — SFT on email classification, DPO on joke generation, RFT on email classification with reasoning
- **Case study** — GPT-3.5 → GPT-4 distillation for function-calling latency reduction
