# Favorited

Favorited is described as "the world's most engaging live app," scaling from $1 to $100 million annualized revenue in six months. It was presented at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Key Facts

- **Growth**: $1 to $100M ARR in six months — called "the fastest growing company of all time" by the founder ^[extracted]
- **Founder track record**: Previously built a social app with 250 million users generating $20 million per year ^[extracted]
- **Founder identity**: Called just "David" — distinct from David Vorick (Glow Labs) and Diego Rodriguez (CREA) ^[extracted]
- **Hiring**: Seeking "cracked engineers" to join the team ^[extracted]
