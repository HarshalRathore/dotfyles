# Hariharan Ganesan

Hariharan Ganesan co-presented with [[entities/sahil-yadav|Sahil Yadav]] at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 on a talk titled "Critical AI Inference Your CIO Can Trust." He is affiliated with [[entities/telemetrak|Telemetrak]]. ^[extracted]

## Key Contributions

- Articulated the [[concepts/ai-trust-pillars|three pillars of trustworthy AI]] — explainability, traceability, and adaptive guardrails — drawing analogies to safety principles in aircraft, energy grids, and banking ^[extracted]
- Described the X-TOPS lifecycle and its distinction from standard MLOPS ^[extracted]
- Introduced the concepts of **click-to-fix workflows** for structured human feedback ^[extracted]
- Presented the [[concepts/mttre|MT-T-R-E]] (Mean Time to Resolve Explainable Errors) and [[concepts/trust-adjusted-risk|Trust Adjusted Risk & Dollars]] metrics ^[extracted]

## Key Quotes

> "AI has to show its work. Every important decision shouldn't be a mystery." ^[extracted]

> "X-TOPS is creating a system where every AI decision has a clear why, a when, and a who attached to it." ^[extracted]

> "The challenge is adopting X-TOPS is like a journey. It's not a flip of a switch." ^[extracted]
