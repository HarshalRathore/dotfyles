# John Collison

John Collison is the co-founder of [[entities/stripe|Stripe]] and brother of [[entities/patrick-collison|Patrick Collison]]. ^[extracted]

During Stripe's early days, John played a critical role in the Wells Fargo payment backend integration. While [[entities/greg-brockman|Greg Brockman]] implemented everything, John tested from the top of the test script, constantly reporting what was broken. ^[extracted] Dara worked from the bottom of the test script upward. Together, the three completed in 24 hours what Wells Fargo said would take nine months. ^[extracted]
