# Answer AI

**Answer AI** is a company founded by [[entities/jeremy-howard|Jeremy Howard]]. It serves as a practical example of the small-team, generalist-first approach to AI company building. ^[extracted]

## Philosophy

Jeremy Howard's philosophy at Answer AI, which [[entities/vik-paruchuri|Vik Paruchuri]] studied while working with him, centers on: ^[extracted]

1. **Hire fewer than 15 generalists** — People who can work across the full stack and understand all aspects of the company
2. **Fill edges with AI and internal tooling** — Use AI to handle peripheral work rather than hiring specialists
3. **Use simple, boring technology** — No Kubernetes clusters for a three-person company; prefer straightforward solutions
4. **Build block libraries** — Invest in tools like fast HTML and Monster UI as reusable building blocks

This philosophy requires a high cultural bar: engineers who talk to customers, go-to-market people who actually build, high trust, and everyone focusing on customers. ^[extracted]
