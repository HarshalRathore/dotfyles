# PromptFu

PromptFu is an open-source safety evaluation tool from [[entities/red-hat|Red Hat]] for testing LLM behavior. It enables customizable safety and bias tests, with a range of examples available in its GitHub repository. ^[extracted]

## Usage

PromptFu supports a variety of test types beyond just safety-focused examples. At the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, [[entities/taylor-jordan-smith|Taylor Jordan Smith]] used it in the third hands-on activity of the LLM evals workshop, focusing on safety evaluation. ^[extracted]
