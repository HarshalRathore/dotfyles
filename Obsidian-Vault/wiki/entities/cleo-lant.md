# Cleo Lant

Cleo Lant is a PostHog engineer. She wrote the "stop AI slop" guide to AI evaluations with LLM-as-a-judge and co-authored (with [[entities/josh-snyder]]) the self-driving product improvement pipeline.

## Known for

- **AI evaluation methodology** — Guide to using LLM-as-a-Judge for eval templates, why manual review doesn't scale, five pre-built templates, and the AI product improvement loop
- **Self-driving product pipeline** — Co-author of the five-step automated pipeline that ingests signals, researches problems, and opens PRs automatically
