# Eric Simons

Co-founder of **Bolt.new** and **StackBlitz**. He and his co-founder (a childhood friend) have been building websites together since they were 13 years old — roughly 20 years of partnership.^{inferred}

## Career

Before StackBlitz/Bolt.new, Simons and his co-founder bootstrapped a previous company from the ground up, going through the experience of being broke and living on couches. This taught him "how far a dollar can stretch" and made the inefficiency of most startups in finding product-market-fit painfully obvious.

At StackBlitz, the company operated for seven years before launching Bolt.new in October of the previous year. At launch time, ARR was $0.7M and the team numbered fewer than 20 people. The company was nearly shutting down — Bolt.new was conceived as the last effort before potentially winding down, not as a strategic pivot.

## Philosophy

Simons articulates a company philosophy built on:
- **Small teams with high context per head** — people have agency, don't need permission, move fast
- **Low ego, high trust** — essential for surviving extreme startup chaos
- **Obsessed with making the user successful** — the north star metric
- **Grit and resilience** — the foundation underneath operational chaos
- **Lower burn rate = more shots on goal** — runway is the most important resource in early-stage startups
