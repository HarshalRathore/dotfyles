# Amazon AGI

Amazon AGI is Amazon's experimental artificial general intelligence lab, based in San Francisco. Described by [[entities/danielle-perszyk|Danielle Perszyk]] as "the experimental new Amazon AGI SF lab." ^[extracted]

## Research Focus

Based on publicly available information from the AIEF2025 talk: ^[inferred]

- **Computer-use agents** — Building agents that interact with visual UIs via pixels, using the browser as a shared environment
- **[[entities/nova-act|Nova Act]]** — The lab's agent SDK combining vision-trained models with a Python developer interface
- **Human augmentation** — The lab's stated vision is building AI that makes humans smarter and gives them more agency, distinct from the standard AGI goal of building machines that are smarter than humans

## Team

The lab includes cognitive scientists like Danielle Perszyk who bring neuroscience and evolutionary psychology perspectives to AI development. ^[extracted]
