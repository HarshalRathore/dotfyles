# Scott Wu

Scott Wu is the founder and CEO of **Cognition**, the company behind **Devin**, the first widely-adopted AI software engineering agent.

## AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 Talk

Scott spoke at AIEF2025 presenting "Devin 2.0 and the Future of SWE," where he outlined:

- **Moore's Law for AI agents** — AI agent capability in code doubles every 70 days (faster than general LLM doubling of ~7 months). This translates to 16-64x improvement per year.
- **The tiered evolution** of agent capabilities from tab completion → repetitive migrations → isolated bug fixes → cross-file diagnosis.
- **Interface evolution** — The right way to interact with agents changes every 2-3 months as capabilities advance to new tiers.

## Previous Appearance

Scott spoke at AI Engineer World's Fair the year prior (AI Engineer 2024), about 12-18 months before this 2025 talk, covering the early days of Devin development.
