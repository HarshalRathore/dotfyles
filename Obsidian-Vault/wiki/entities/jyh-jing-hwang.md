# Jyh Jing Hwang

[[entities/jyh-jing-hwang|Jyh Jing Hwang]] is an engineer at [[entities/waymo|Waymo]] who presented the EMMA system at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.

## EMMA Presentation

At AIEF2025, Jyh Jing Hwang presented EMMA (Embodied Multimodal Model for Autonomous driving), a foundation-model-based autonomous driving system that leverages Google's Gemini to drive using only cameras without HD maps. The talk covered:

- History of autonomous driving research (1980s neural networks → end-to-end models in 2020)
- The gap between L2 research prototypes and L4 production systems
- EMMA's architecture: routing text + 360° camera video → future waypoints
- Chain-of-thought reasoning for explainability
- Scaling laws in autonomous driving
