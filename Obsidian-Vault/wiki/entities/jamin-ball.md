# Jamin Ball

Jamin Ball is a partner at [[entities/altimeter-capital|Altimeter Capital]], a venture capital firm. He is known for articulating the [[concepts/sequence-of-moats|sequence of moats]] concept about competitive advantage in the technology industry.

## Sequence of Moats

In a newsletter cited by [[entities/brian-balfour|Brian Balfour]] at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Ball wrote: ^[extracted]

> "The real moat is just a sequence of smaller moats stacked together. Each one buys time. What you do with that time, how fast you execute, how quickly you evolve, determines whether you stay ahead."

He noted the dramatic compression of competitive timelines: ^[extracted]

- **Historical moat duration**: 6 to 12 months
- **AI-era moat duration**: 2 to 3 weeks
