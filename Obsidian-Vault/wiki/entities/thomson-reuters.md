# Thomson Reuters

**Thomson Reuters** is a 100+ year old company serving legal, tax, compliance, audit, and risk industries. It is the highest employer of lawyers in the world.

## Scale and Market Position

- 97% of the top 100 US law firms are customers
- 99% of Fortune 100 corporate customers in the top 100 US CPA firms
- Over $3 billion in acquisitions over the last couple of years
- North of $200 million per year in capital on AI product development

## Domain Expertise Moat

- 4,500 domain experts
- 4,500+ proprietary content across legal, tax, compliance, audit, and risk
- 1.5+ terabytes of proprietary content served through software products

## AI Infrastructure

- Applied research lab with 200+ scientists and engineers working closely with development teams
- Heavy investment in AI product development ($200M+/year)

## AI Agent Strategy

Under CTO Joel Hron, Thomson Reuters shifted from building helpful AI assistance to productive agents that make judgments and decisions in high-stakes environments where the risks of being wrong are not acceptable. The company's approach involves decomposing legacy applications — 100+ years of highly tuned domain logic — into tools that agents can use, treating legacy systems as assets rather than baggage. ^[extracted]
