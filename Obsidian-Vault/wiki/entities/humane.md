Humane is an AI hardware company known for the AI Pin, a wearable AI device that operates without a screen. The company has built a close partnership with xAI's Grok chip team, which provides a pathway for other companies to explore Grok chips for AI inference workloads.

## Connection to Luma AI

Luma AI announced a significant partnership with Humane, which provides indirect access to Grok chips for Dream Machine inference evaluation. Keegan McCallum noted at AIEF2025 that Luma is "exploring some of these other chipsets through these partnerships" — with the Humane-Grok relationship being the most concrete example.

This partnership fits into Luma's broader hardware diversification strategy: moving beyond exclusive reliance on NVIDIA H100s toward a multi-vendor GPU fleet that includes AMD and Grok chips.
