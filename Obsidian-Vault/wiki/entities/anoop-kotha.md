# Anoop Kotha

Anoop Kotha is a Solution Architect at [[entities/openai|OpenAI]] on the US Solutions Architecture team. He co-presented "Building Effective Voice Agents" at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 alongside [[entities/toki-sherbakov|Toki Sherbakov]].

## Talk Contributions

In the talk, Kotha focused on practical patterns for building voice agents:
- **Delegation through tools** — using the [[concepts/openai-real-time-api|Real-time API]] as a frontline agent that delegates complex tasks to more capable models (O3, O4 mini)
- **[[concepts/voice-agent-prompting|Voice agent prompting]]** — controlling demeanor, tone, enthusiasm, and expressiveness
- **[[concepts/voice-agent-evals|Evaluation strategies]]** — observability, SME labeling, transcription-based evals, audio evals, and synthetic conversations
- **[[concepts/voice-agent-guardrails|Guardrails]]** — async execution and debounce patterns for real-time safety
- **Case studies** — [[entities/lemonade-insurance|Lemonade]] (early focus on evals and guardrails) and [[entities/tinder|Tinder]] (brand customization for Riz Chat)
