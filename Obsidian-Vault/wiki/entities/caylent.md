**Caylent** (formerly Kalen) is a software development and AI consulting company based in the United States. It is an AWS Partner of the Year winner and co-founded by **[[entities/randall-hunt|Randall Hunt]].**

The company describes itself as built by hiring "passionate autodidacts with a little bit of product ADHD" who jump between different technologies and build cool things for customers. They serve hundreds of customers at any given time, ranging from Fortune 500 companies to startups. ^[extracted]

## What They Build

Caylent's portfolio spans the full spectrum of AI application development:

- **Chatbots** — Conversational AI interfaces
- **Copilots** — AI-assisted development and productivity tools
- **AI Agents** — Autonomous agents for enterprise workflows
- **Custom AI applications** — Fine-tuned models and application layers on top of self-service productivity tools
- **Intelligent document processing** — Custom classifiers combined with generative AI for receipts, bills of lading, and logistics documents
- **Multimodal search** — Video indexing and semantic understanding using Nova Pro models and Titan V2 embeddings

## Notable Customers

- **Brainbox AI** — Building operating system for HVAC management; Caylent built a decarbonization agent
- **Simmons** — Water management and conservation AI
- **Nature Footage** — Multimodal video search and indexing
- **Sports media company** — Real-time sports footage processing with highlight detection
- **Large logistics management company** — Intelligent document processing for receipts and bills of lading

## Company Philosophy

Caylent's motto, according to Randall Hunt, is "we build cool stuff." The company positions itself as a builder's company rather than a consultancy — they don't just advise, they ship production software. ^[inferred]
