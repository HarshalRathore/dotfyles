# SMIC

**SMIC** (Semiconductor Manufacturing International Corporation) is China's leading semiconductor foundry, often described as "China's TSMC."

## Manufacturing Capability

SMIC has demonstrated capability in 7nm manufacturing for smartphone chips, which are smaller and easier to produce than AI accelerators. Key facts: ^[extracted]

- The first 7nm chip ever produced was an iPhone chip in 2020
- [[entities/nvidia|NVIDIA]] didn't release a 5nm GPU until 2022–2023 with the H100
- Smartphone chips achieve better yields than AI chips due to their smaller die size
- SMIC is expected to transition from 7nm smartphone chips to 7nm AI chips within the year

## Strategic Significance

SMIC's ability to produce 7nm AI chips at high volumes would significantly reduce China's reliance on [[entities/tsmc|TSMC]] for AI hardware, undermining the effectiveness of US export controls. ^[inferred]

- The assumption that China cannot produce advanced AI chips domestically is incorrect
- SMIC is expected to produce millions of AI chips per year once the transition is complete
- This would enable domestic Chinese AI labs like [[entities/deepseek|DeepSeek]] to scale training independently
