# Jerry Liu

Jerry Liu is the co-founder and CEO of [[entities/llamaindex|LlamaIndex]].

## Role at LlamaIndex

As CEO, Jerry leads LlamaIndex's strategy for building generative AI applications with focus on agents and RAG. He has been a vocal advocate for using LLMs and LVMs for document understanding, arguing that LLMs provide a more general layer of accuracy than traditional task-specific ML models for document parsing. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

Jerry presented "Building AI Agents that actually automate Knowledge Work" at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, where he introduced LlamaIndex's document toolbox concept, the document MCP server pattern, and announced new Excel normalization capabilities for their GenEI-native document parsing platform. See [[references/aief2025-building-ai-agents-jerry-liu-llamaindex]] for the full talk summary.
