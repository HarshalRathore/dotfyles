# G42

**G42** is a UAE-based AI company that is building one of the world's largest data center complexes as part of the Stargate project.

## Stargate GPU Deal

G42's deal (announced during Trump's visit to Saudi Arabia and UAE, excluding Israel) allows: ^[extracted]

- **500,000 GPUs per year** — G42 can purchase this many GPUs annually
- **20% retention** — G42 keeps 20% of purchased GPUs for its own use
- **80% US allocation** — The remaining 80% must go to US hyperscalers, cloud companies, and AI companies

This means G42 acts as a procurement channel for US AI companies to access compute in the Middle East while the UAE benefits from infrastructure investment. ^[inferred]

## 5-Gigawatt Data Center Campus

G42 is building a massive 5-gigawatt data center campus in the UAE: ^[extracted]

- The first phase shown in satellite photos is part of a gigawatt-scale complex
- For comparison: [[entities/xai|xAI]]'s data centers are ~200 megawatts; OpenAI and others are 200MW or less
- Stargate's first six phases total 1.2 gigawatts
- These are described as "absolutely ginormous" compared to existing AI data centers

## Microsoft Relationship

- [[entities/microsoft|Microsoft]] is G42's large customer and investor
- Microsoft is expected to be a major beneficiary of the GPU allocation
- G42 also has data center sites in Kentucky and New York where UAE matching investments are being spent

## OpenAI Connection

The deal is understood to include an unstated component: [[entities/openai|OpenAI]] will have a GPU cluster in the Middle East. Sam Altman's earlier statements about building GPU clusters in the Middle East are now understood to be part of this arrangement. ^[inferred]
