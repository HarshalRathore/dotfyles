# Quan Vuong

**Quan Vuong** (also referred to as "Kwon" in the talk) is co-founder of [[entities/physical-intelligence|Physical Intelligence]], an AI robotics company building Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for general-purpose robot control.

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Quan presented PI's mission: "to make a model that can control any robot to do any task." He emphasized that this capability requires multiple scientific breakthroughs and is not yet achievable, but the company is open-sourcing their research and being transparent about their approach.

## Key Contributions

- Co-founded Physical Intelligence with Jost Tobias Springberg
- Articulated the vision for general-purpose robot control via VLA models
- Emphasized the importance of open science in robotics
