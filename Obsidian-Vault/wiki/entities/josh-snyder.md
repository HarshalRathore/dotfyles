# Josh Snyder

Josh Snyder is a PostHog engineer and co-author (with [[entities/cleo-lant]]) of the self-driving product improvement pipeline. The pipeline ingests signals from PostHog's platform, groups them into problems, researches them with a Claude Agent SDK agent, decides actionability, and opens PRs automatically.

## Known for

- **Self-driving product improvement pipeline** — The five-step automated loop: ingest signals → group into problems → research → decide actionability → write fix and open PR
- **Signal ingestion architecture** — Normalizing trillions of events/month into a unified shape with a security classifier at the top to prevent prompt injection
