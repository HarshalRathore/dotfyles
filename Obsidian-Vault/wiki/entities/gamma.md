# Gamma

**Gamma** is an AI-powered presentation creation company. It is a customer of [[entities/datalab|Datalab]] and is cited by [[entities/vik-paruchuri|Vik Paruchuri]] as an example of a small team achieving very meaningful growth in ARR. ^[extracted]

## Leadership

Gamma is co-founded and led by [[entities/grant-lee|Grant Lee]] as CEO and co-founder. ^[extracted]

## Product Philosophy

Gamma positions itself as "the anti-PowerPoint," aiming to reimagine how people create and share content. ^[extracted] The company follows a **content-first** philosophy — making it dead simple to create and share content by focusing on the content itself rather than design mechanics like formatting boxes, aligning layouts, and resizing elements. ^[extracted]

## Scale

Gamma has grown to over **50 million users** while remaining a team of only **30 people**. ^[extracted] This scale-to-team ratio exemplifies the small-team-scaling thesis: AI-era startups can serve massive user bases without traditional headcount growth. ^[inferred]

## Long-term Vision

Beyond product creation, Gamma aims to build what it calls **"tools for imagination"** — helping people stretch and shape ideas in ways that are easier to share, with the broader goal of pushing innovation forward. ^[extracted]

## Relationship to Datalab

Vik Paruchuri previously built presentations for Gamma using their tool. Gamma is one of Datalab's customers in the AI startup segment, alongside tier-one AI labs, universities, and Fortune 500 companies. ^[extracted]

## Significance

Gamma is used as a concrete example in the small-AI-teams thesis: a small team building a meaningful product with significant revenue growth, parallel to Datalab's own trajectory. ^[inferred]
