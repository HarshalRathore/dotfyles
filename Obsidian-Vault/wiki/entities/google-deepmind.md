# Google DeepMind

**Google DeepMind** is an AI research lab at Google that was featured at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2026 with presentations on model progression and the modality gap. ^[extracted]

## Omni Team

DeepMind's **Omni team** (led by Benoit Schillings, VP of Research) presented on: ^[extracted]

1. **Model progression framework** — the typical evolution path of language models
2. **Modality gap** — the disconnect between machine perception of audio/video and human experience
3. [[concepts/vo3|**VO3**]] — the first joint audio-visual generation model
