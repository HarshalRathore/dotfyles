# Stephen Shin

Stephen Shin leads Developer Relations at [[entities/neo4j|Neo4j]]. He co-authored (with [[entities/michael-hunger|Michael Hunger]]) the book *GraphRAG: The Definitive Guide* for O'Reilly. ^[extracted]

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, he co-presented "Practical GraphRAG: Making LLMs smarter with Knowledge Graphs" alongside Michael Hunger and [[entities/jesús-barrasa|Jesús Barrasa]], introducing the GraphRAG patterns catalog and the three-phase knowledge graph construction methodology. ^[extracted]
