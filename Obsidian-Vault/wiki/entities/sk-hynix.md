# SK Hynix

**SK Hynix** is a South Korean memory chip manufacturer and one of the world's leading suppliers of HBM (High Bandwidth Memory), the critical memory technology used in AI accelerators.

## HBM Market Position

SK Hynix and [[entities/samsung|Samsung]] are the two primary HBM manufacturers. HBM is essential for AI chip performance because it provides the high-bandwidth memory needed to feed data to GPU/accelerator cores during training and inference. ^[extracted]
