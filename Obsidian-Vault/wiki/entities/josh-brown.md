# Josh Brown

Josh Brown is the founder of CoFrame, an AI website personalization platform. He spoke at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Background

- Previously founded a company that scaled to over $2 billion in a couple of years ^[extracted]
- Started tinkering with AI code generation and created one of the top autonomous coding agents on GitHub (number one on GitHub for a week) ^[extracted]
- Recognized the opportunity to build something bigger than coding agents ^[extracted]

## At CoFrame

CoFrame gives every customer experience its own AI growth team, making websites adaptive and personalized rather than one-size-fits-all. The company is the only marketing tech firm directly partnered with OpenAI ^[extracted].
