# Alex Duffy

Alex Duffy is Lead of Training Consulting at [[entities/every-to|Every.to]]. He describes himself as "very into education and AI" and considers benchmarks an "underrated way to educate." ^[extracted]

## AI Diplomacy

Duffy created **AI Diplomacy**, a benchmark game where multiple LLMs (including Gemini 2.5 Pro, O3, and Opus) play the board game Diplomacy — a zero-luck strategy game where progress depends entirely on inter-model messaging, negotiation, alliance formation, and betrayal. The game demonstrates measurable **model personality** differences in strategic behavior. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

At the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Duffy presented "Benchmarks Are Memes: How What We Measure Shapes AI—and Us," arguing that benchmarks function as Dawkinsian memes that shape the most powerful tool ever created. ^[extracted]
