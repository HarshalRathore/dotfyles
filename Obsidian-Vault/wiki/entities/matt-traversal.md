# Matt (Traversal.ai co-founder)

Matt is a co-founder of [[entities/traversal-ai|Traversal.ai]] and was the first engineer to join the company after Anish Agarwal. He has a background in high-frequency trading and was motivated to found Traversal after taking a causal AI class.

## Background

Matt spent most of his career in high-frequency trading, where he wanted to focus on writing beautiful code but found himself spending most of his time debugging production incidents. He quit that role because he was "sick of this."

Later, he took a class on causal AI taught by a professor who mentioned that causal AI could be used to automate incident remediation workflows. This inspired Matt to approach the professor about a research project, and he learned the professor had already started a company to solve this problem — Traversal.ai.

## AIEF2025 Talk

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Matt presented the DigitalOcean case study, demonstrating how Traversal reduced MTTR by ~40% through autonomous incident investigation using agent swarms and causal ML.
