# Frank Liu

Frank Liu is a speaker at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 who works on embedding models and re-rankers for RAG and semantic search. He was formerly part of the Voyage AI team and joined MongoDB approximately 3–4 months before the AIEF2025 conference. ^[extracted]

## Background

Frank was part of the Voyage AI team, which builds "the most accurate, cost-effective embedding models and re-rankers for RAG and semantic search." Voyage AI's models are used beyond RAG for classification, clustering, and other applications. ^[extracted]

Voyage AI is available via the Voyage AI API, the Azure and AWS marketplaces. The team joined MongoDB and now represents MongoDB at conferences and events. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Frank presented "The State of AI Powered Search and Retrieval," covering: ^[extracted]

- The definition of AI-powered search (concept matching, intent understanding, reasoning)
- Real-world applications: chat-with-codebase, domain-specific search with structured filtering, agentic retrieval
- Key lessons: no one-size-fits-all embedding model, embeddings alone are insufficient, agentic retrieval as a feedback loop
- The future of AI-powered search and retrieval

## Key Views

- 95–99% of AI-powered search systems use embeddings in some form
- There is no one-size-fits-all embedding model or LLM
- Embeddings alone are not enough — structured data filtering is essential
- 2025 is "the year of the agents" — agents require powerful conversational retrieval
- Agentic retrieval operates as a feedback loop, not a simple input-output function
