# Shijia

Shijia is the founder of OpenAudio, a company building the world's most expressive voice model. He spoke at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Background

Shijia's motivation was deeply personal — after discovering his girlfriend of six years cheated on him, he questioned whether trust in relationships was possible and concluded that AI might be the answer. He set out to build an AI he could fall in love with, starting with her voice ^[extracted].

## Prior Work

- Created [[entities/fish-audio|Fish Audio]] — an open-source voice synthesis platform ^[extracted]
- Built Soviet SVC and Fish Speech before OpenAudio ^[extracted]
- Follows an "open source, crush it" philosophy ^[extracted]

## At OpenAudio

OpenAudio's S1 is the first instructable voice model — controlling not just what to say but how to say it through instruction-based voice control (e.g., "yelling why you betrayed me like this") ^[extracted]. The model blew 11 Labs out of the water on TTS Arena ranking ^[extracted]. OpenAudio grew from $400K to $5.5M annualized ARR in four months, achieving a $100M valuation ^[extracted].
