# Keycard

A company founded by [[entities/jared-hanson|Jared Hanson]] building an identity and access management platform specifically for AI and agents. Keycard positions itself at the intersection of [[entities/passport-js|Passport.js]]'s authentication expertise and the emerging need for agent-specific identity infrastructure.

## Mission

Build IAM infrastructure for the agent era — enabling AI agents to authenticate, authorize, and access APIs using standard protocols like [[concepts/oauth-2.0|OAuth 2.0]] rather than static API keys.

## Background

Jared Hanson previously built core identity infrastructure at [[entities/auth0|Auth0]] and worked at [[entities/okta|Okta]], bringing enterprise-grade identity patterns to the agent space. His creation of [[entities/passport-js|Passport.js]] gave him deep experience making authentication accessible to developers.
