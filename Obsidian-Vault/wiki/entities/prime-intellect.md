# Prime Intellect

**Prime Intellect** is an AI company affiliated with [[entities/will-brown|Will Brown]], who presented on training agentic reasoners via reinforcement learning at AIEF2025. ^[extracted]

The company's work focuses on making RL-based model training accessible beyond the big labs — bridging the gap between the complex RL training pipelines used by OpenAI and DeepSeek and what startups and individual researchers can realistically accomplish. ^[inferred]
