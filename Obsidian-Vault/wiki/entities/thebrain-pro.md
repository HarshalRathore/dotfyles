## TheBrain.pro

TheBrain.pro is a company founded by [[entities/lukasz-gandecki|Łukasz Gądecki]] that builds [[concepts/books-reimagined|Books Reimagined]] — AI-powered, multimodal reading experiences for classic literature. The project combines text, AI-generated graphics, AI-generated music, and interactive voice Q&A to transform reading into something closer to watching a movie.

At AIEF2025, Gądecki announced that TheBrain.pro was open-sourcing its book player, enabling anyone to create Netflix-style interactive book experiences. The underlying technology uses LLMs for scene analysis, character detection, mood detection, music generation, and structured XML metadata extraction.

The company's philosophy centers on hiding AI from the end user — AI handles the initial draft and heavy lifting, but human curation is essential for quality control over music, graphics, and character avatars.
