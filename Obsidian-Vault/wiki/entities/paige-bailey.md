# Paige Bailey

Paige Bailey is the engine lead for the Developer Relations (DevRel) team at Google DeepMind. At the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, she presented on behalf of the generative media team, delivering a talk covering Google DeepMind's latest generative media models: Veo 3, Imagine 4, and Lyria 2.

She has expressed a strong perspective on creative AI, noting that music AI should be built "in collaboration with the creative industry, not outside it," and drew parallels between the forces that make music work and the forces that make life work. ^[inferred]

## At AIEF2025

Delivered the talk "Veo 3 for Developers," covering:
- Veo 3's native multimodal audio-video generation
- Veo 2's creative control features (reference-powered videos, outpainting, character consistency)
- Imagine 4's image generation capabilities
- Lyria 2's music generation and collaboration with musicians
