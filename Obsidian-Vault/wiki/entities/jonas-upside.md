# Jonas (Upside)

Jonas is an engineer and co-founder of Upside, a forensic revenue attribution platform. He spoke at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Background

- Dropped out of high school at age 15 ^[extracted]
- Moved across the country to California to join a startup called Branch ^[extracted]
- At Branch: built a search engine used by over 100 million people daily ^[extracted]
- Left Branch last year with one of Branch's founders to start Upside ^[extracted]

## At Upside

Upside uses LLMs to transform messy enterprise data into structured maps of customer interactions, building a "data command center" for sales and marketing teams ^[extracted]. The company has been in stealth for about a year and recently attracted significant customer interest ^[extracted].
