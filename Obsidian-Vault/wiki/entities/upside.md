# Upside

Upside is a forensic revenue attribution and intelligence platform that uses LLMs to transform messy enterprise data into structured maps of customer interactions. It was presented at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Key Facts

- **Stage**: Quietly building for about a year, just started public conversations a couple weeks ago ^[extracted]
- **Traction**: LinkedIn post about the product "blew up" with demo requests and a "who's who" of named companies lining up as customers ^[extracted]
- **Team**: Co-founded by Jonas (engineer, ex-Branch) and a co-founder of Branch ^[extracted]
- **Hiring**: Looking for engineers interested in knowledge graphs, data analytics agents, graph analytics, and graph learning models ^[extracted]

## Product Vision

Upside addresses the problem of enterprises that are "data hoarders" but not "data practitioners" — they store everything in Salesforce (described as "a SQL database in a trench coat") but don't know what to do with it ^[extracted]. The platform:

- Uses LLMs to extract important details from messy, unstructured email records ^[extracted]
- Creates a structured map of the world and all customer interactions ^[extracted]
- Builds a "data command center" for sales and marketing teams to reach customers more effectively ^[extracted]

## Analogy

Just as search engines and web crawlers learned to make sense of the unstructured web, Upside is doing the same for enterprise data ^[extracted].
