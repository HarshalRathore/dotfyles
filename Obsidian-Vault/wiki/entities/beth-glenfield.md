# Beth Glenfield

Beth Glenfield is a technical recruitment professional from Ireland who speaks at DevDay on the topic of AI-era hiring practices. ^[extracted]

## Background

Glenfield is based in Ireland and has noted that her accent may vary between Irish and American depending on context. ^[extracted]

## DevDay

She is a speaker at DevDay, where she presented "How to Hire AI Engineers when EVERYONE is cheating with AI" at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025. In this talk she argued that AI is fundamentally breaking technical recruitment and that DevDay has developed an alternative hiring process based on workplace simulations with AI agents. ^[extracted]
