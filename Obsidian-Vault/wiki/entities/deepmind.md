# DeepMind

DeepMind is an AI research lab owned by Alphabet (Google). It was referenced at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Key Positions

**Yann LeCun**, DeepMind's founder and CEO, argued that compound AI agent errors will take more than 10 years to fix through scaling alone, and that a fundamentally new AI architecture is needed to push the paradigm forward ^[extracted]. This position directly supports the "scaling is dead" thesis articulated by Federalist AI at the same event.
