## Overview

**Notable** is a venture capital firm focused on early-stage investments. Chelcie Taylor, a speaker at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, is an investor at Notable. ^[extracted]

## Content and Thought Leadership

Notable publishes blog content on fundraising and startup strategy, which founders reference in cold outreach emails. ^[extracted] Referencing a Notable blog post was cited as an effective cold-email tactic during the AIEF2025 talk. ^[extracted]

## Investment Focus

Notable invests at the pre-seed and seed stages, backing companies based on founder quality and vision rather than requiring revenue or a finished product at the time of investment. ^[inferred]
