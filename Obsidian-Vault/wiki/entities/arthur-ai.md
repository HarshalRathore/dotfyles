# Arthur AI

A company in the AI/ML monitoring, evaluation, and security space, founded around 2018. Arthur operated across the full evolution of AI — traditional ML, deep learning, gen AI, and agentic AI. ^[extracted]

## Key Facts

- Co-founder and chief scientist: [[entities/john-dickerson|John Dickerson]] (6 years, through acquisition/transition to [[entities/mozilla-ai|Mozilla AI]]) ^[extracted]
- Series A led by Index Ventures. ^[extracted]
- Product categories: AI observability, evaluation, security, and guardrails. ^[extracted]
- Competed in the same space as Galileo, Braintrust, Weights & Biases, Aporia, Arise AI, Fiddler, Protect AI, and the observability/monitoring offerings from Snowflake, Databricks, Datadog, SageMaker, and Vertex. ^[inferred]
- By 2025, like other evaluation/observability companies, shifted focus toward agentic and multi-agent system monitoring — the thesis being "you should monitor the whole system, not just the one model." ^[extracted]
- Created guardrail products (hallucination detection, prompt injection detection) that sold into CISO offices — CISOs tend to write smaller checks more quickly than CIOs. ^[extracted]
