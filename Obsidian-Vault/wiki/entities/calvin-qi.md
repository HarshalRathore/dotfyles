# Calvin Qi

Calvin Qi (introduces himself as "Chong") is CEO and co-founder of [[entities/lance-db|LanceCB]], building data tools for machine learning and data science for about 20 years. He is one of the co-authors of the [[entities/pandas|Pandas]] library and now works on LanceCB for data that doesn't fit neatly into Pandas data frames. ^[extracted]

At [[entities/harvey-ai|Harvey]], Calvin leads one of the teams working on RAG, tackling tough RAG problems across massive data sets of complex legal documents and complex use cases. ^[extracted]

## Background

- ~20 years building data tools for ML and data science ^[extracted]
- Co-author of [[entities/pandas|Pandas]] library ^[extracted]
- CEO and co-founder of [[entities/lance-db|LanceCB]] ^[extracted]
- Leads RAG team at [[entities/harvey-ai|Harvey]] ^[extracted]

## At Harvey

Calvin's team at Harvey handles RAG over three scales of data: ^[extracted]

1. **On-demand uploads** — small scale, 1 to 50 documents, like typical AI assistant uploads
2. **Vaults** — larger project-scale collections, e.g. all contracts, litigation documents, and emails for a big deal
3. **Data corpuses** — largest scale, knowledge bases spanning legislation, case laws, laws, taxes, and regulations for entire countries
