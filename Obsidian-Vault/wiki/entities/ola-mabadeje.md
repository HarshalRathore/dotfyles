# Ola Mabadeje

Ola Mabadeje is a product lead at [[entities/cisco|Cisco]] who has been working on AI for the last three years. He is based in Cisco's [[entities/outshift|OutShift]] incubation group, which focuses on emerging technologies and their application to traditional business units. ^[extracted]

## Background

By training, Ola is an electrical engineer who doubled into network engineering. Over the last three years, he has focused on AI applications, with additional group focus on quantum technology and quantum networking. ^[extracted]

## Approach

Ola follows a customer-first product methodology: start with customer problems, work backwards to solution design. His team goes through an incubation cycle — customer interviews, prototyping, A/B testing, MVP delivery — and successful products graduate into Cisco's main business units. ^[extracted]

## Notable Work

Presented at AIEF2025 on a multi-agent AI system combined with network knowledge graphs to solve change management failures in network operations. The system uses ArangoDB, OpenConfig schema, and a layered graph architecture as a digital twin of production networks. ^[extracted]
