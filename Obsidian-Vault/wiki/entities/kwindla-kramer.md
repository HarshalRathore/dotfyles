# Kwindla Kramer

Kwindla Kramer is a co-founder of Daily and creator of PipeCat, an open source voice AI framework.^[extracted] He is also known by the name Quinn.^[extracted]

## Background

Kwindla focuses on the infrastructure layer for real-time communication — the same layer that WebRTC operates on. His work at Daily builds the audio/video infrastructure that powers real-time applications.^[extracted] Daily was founded in 2016 and provides global infrastructure for real-time audio, video, and AI.^[extracted]

## PipeCat

Kwindla co-created PipeCat, an open source, vendor-neutral voice AI framework that enables builders to create voice AI applications.^[extracted] PipeCat supports multi-party connections, native telephony, and 60+ model integrations.^[extracted]

## PipeCat Cloud

Kwindla presented PipeCat Cloud at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, describing it as a thin wrapper around Docker and Kubernetes optimized for voice AI, built on top of Daily's global real-time infrastructure.^[extracted] He was initially resistant to building anything commercial around PipeCat but launched PipeCat Cloud in response to frequent community questions about Kubernetes deployment and scaling.^[extracted]
