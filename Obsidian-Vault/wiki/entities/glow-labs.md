# Glow Labs

Glow Labs is an incentive protocol that revolutionizes solar panel construction by applying Bitcoin-like mining incentives to renewable energy infrastructure. It was presented at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Key Facts

- **Funding**: $30 million round from Framework and Union Square Ventures (USV) ^[extracted]
- **Revenue record**: World record for on-chain DPIN revenue — over $10 million in a single day ^[extracted]
- **Prior work**: Founder David Vorick built Scalia, a cryptocurrency taken from $10K to $3B+ market cap ^[extracted]
- **Markets**: Building incentive projects in India, Mexico, Lebanon, and across the developing world ^[extracted]
- **Vision**: "Why not tens of millions of solar panels?" — applying Bitcoin's incentive model to solar construction ^[extracted]

## Problem Statement

In the developing world, rising temperatures and growing populations have strained the grid. People cannot run air conditioners during heat of the day, causing deaths from heat stroke ^[extracted]. Glow aims to take government subsidies and turn them into 10x more solar capacity through incentive mechanisms ^[extracted].

## Approach

Glow builds solar "not with shovels, but with incentives." The protocol uses token rewards to motivate solar panel construction in markets where traditional infrastructure investment is insufficient ^[extracted].
