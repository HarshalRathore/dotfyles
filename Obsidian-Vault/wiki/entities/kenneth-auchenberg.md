## Background

Kenneth Auchenberg is an engineer and product builder with experience across Microsoft, Stripe, and venture capital. He was part of the early [[entities/vs-code|VS Code]] team at Microsoft, helping build the product that became the market leader in 2019. He then spent time at [[entities/stripe|Stripe]] running the developer platform, building a significant portion of everything Stripe did for developers. Currently he is a partner at [[entities/alicorp|Alicorp]], an early-stage venture fund based in New York.

## Product Philosophy

Kenneth's overarching product principle is that building great products — especially in the age of AI — is not about the number of big-bang launches but about the number of iterations you can crank out: problems you get into and feedback you get back. His core framework centers on enabling rapid iterative feedback loops with real users, using a continuously viable product as the foundation for evolution rather than a finished product delivered only at the end.

He advocates writing the launch blog post or PRD before building anything, designing the best product before considering constraints like legal and compliance, prototyping everything regardless of role, and getting high-quality high-bandwidth feedback through direct observation and personal relationships with users.
