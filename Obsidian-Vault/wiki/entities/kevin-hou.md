# Kevin Hou

Kevin Hou is the lead product at [[entities/windsurf|Windsurf]]. He delivered the keynote "Windsurf everywhere, doing everything, all at once" at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.

## Role and Focus

As lead product at Windsurf, Hou oversees the product vision for what he describes as the shared timeline between human and AI — the core differentiator of the Windsurf editor. He articulates Windsurf's product philosophy around making AI ubiquitous in software engineering workflows, from context ingestion across external tools to autonomous action across the full development lifecycle.

## Key Contributions

Hou introduced [[concepts/software-engineering-model|SweeOne]], Windsurf's custom-trained model for software engineering workflows, and articulated the [[concepts/data-flywheel|data flywheel]] pattern connecting product usage to model improvement. He framed the industry's trajectory from autocomplete (Copilot-era) through agentic coding (Windsurf-era) toward always-on ambient AI that works in the background. ^[extracted]
