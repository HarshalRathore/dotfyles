# CREA

CREA is an AI creative suite founded by Diego Rodriguez, who serves as co-founder and CTO. The company was presented as part of the HF0 Residency showcase at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.

## Key Facts

- **Team size**: 8 people ^[extracted]
- **Users**: 25 million ^[extracted]
- **Category**: AI creative suite — tools for generating and managing creative content at scale ^[extracted]
- **Funding**: "Raised a bunch of money" (specific amount not disclosed) ^[extract7ed]

## Product Philosophy

Diego Rodriguez frames CREA's approach around three stories: unpredictability (traffic vs. cars), language barriers (Tower of Babel), and speed of adoption (a Korea Fox ad broadcast to millions in two days from signup). The product enables studios to generate millions of images per day, addressing the challenge of finding and managing personalized content at scale ^[extracted].
