# Will Brown

**Will Brown** is a speaker at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, affiliated with [[entities/prime-intellect|Prime Intellect]]. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk: Training Agentic Reasoners

At AIEF2025, Brown presented on **training agentic reasoners** via reinforcement learning. His core thesis: the conversations between reasoning models and agents are often treated as separate topics, but they are fundamentally the same thing. ^[extracted]

Key points from his talk:

- **RL works now** — DeepSeek's open RL models surprised many by demonstrating that RL applied at scale works with surprisingly few tweaks
- **O3 is naturally agentic** — OpenAI's O3 model, with its tool use and agentic task settings, represents the natural evolution of RL-trained models
- **Agents are RL** — Building an agent (harness, environment, tools, iteration) maps directly onto canonical RL concepts (policies, actions, states, rewards, transition probabilities)
- **The moat thesis** — Teams that can RL-tune open models for their specific tasks will have a huge advantage over those relying on generic API wrappers
