# Matt Ball

Matt Ball is a Solutions Architect at [[entities/augment-code|Augment Code]], an AI-assisted coding platform focused on enterprise codebases. He was previously at Postman for several years before moving into the AI developer tool space. ^[extracted]

He co-presented "To the moon! Navigating deep context in legacy code with Augment Agent" alongside [[entities/forrest-brazeal|Forrest Brazeal]] at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025. Ball provided the technical depth on Augment's architecture, particularly its proprietary context engine and enterprise security capabilities (customer-managed encryption keys, ISO 42001 certification). ^[extracted]
