# Patrick Collison

Patrick Collison is the co-founder and CEO of [[entities/stripe|Stripe]]. ^[extracted] He met [[entities/greg-brockman|Greg Brockman]] in a late-night meeting during a storm, and they immediately connected over code — Brockman later described it as "one of those moments where you're like, this is the kind of person that I've wanted to work with and been looking for." ^[extracted]

Collison is known for extreme customer obsession at Stripe, including the legendary practice of personally installing Stripe at customer offices — an episode so rare and cool that it became an [[concepts/urban-legend|urban legend]] within the company. ^[extracted]
