# Qwen

**Qwen** is Alibaba's family of open-weight large language models. The Qwen 3 series has emerged as a leading open-weight model family in both reasoning and non-reasoning categories.

## Qwen 3 Series

Per George Cameron at AIEF2025, the Qwen 3 series ranks: ^[extracted]

- **Second in reasoning open-weight** — behind DeepSeek R1
- **Leading in non-reasoning open-weight** — competitive with the best proprietary models

## Open-Weight Positioning

Qwen is part of the broader Chinese open-weight movement that has closed the intelligence gap between open and proprietary models to its narrowest point ever. ^[extracted]
