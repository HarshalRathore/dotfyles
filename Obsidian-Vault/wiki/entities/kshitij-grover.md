# Kshitij Grover

Kshitij Grover is a co-founder of [[entities/orb|Orb]], a company that provides usage-based billing infrastructure. He presented on revenue engineering and AI product pricing at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.

## Background

- Co-founder of [[entities/orb|Orb]], a usage-based billing platform that helps companies design, iterate on, and implement pricing models. ^[extracted]
- His company works with customers across many layers of the AI stack, giving him broad visibility into how AI companies are approaching monetization. ^[extracted]
- Delivered a talk at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 titled "Revenue Engineering: How to Price (and Reprice) Your AI Product." ^[extracted]

## Key Ideas

- **Pricing as friction** — Pricing is a form of friction that can signal value or prevent adoption depending on how it's applied. ^[extracted]
- **Three principles for AI pricing** — Audience understanding, margin structure (not literal margins), and flexibility to experiment over time. ^[extracted]
- **Pricing is an engineering discipline** — Pricing decisions should be data-driven, simulated before rollout, and iterated frequently — sometimes multiple times per month. ^[inferred]
