# Jost Tobias Springberg

**Jost Tobias Springberg** (referred to as "Toby" in the talk) is co-founder of [[entities/physical-intelligence|Physical Intelligence]], an AI robotics company building Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models.

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Toby delivered the technical deep-dive portion of the talk, explaining VLA architecture, contrasting VLM and VLA training pipelines, and describing PI's data engine pipeline in detail.

## Key Contributions

- Co-founded Physical Intelligence with Quan Vuong
- Explained VLA model architecture and how it extends VLMs for robotics
- Articulated the three unique engineering challenges for VLA training (data sourcing, architecture adaptation, on-device deployment)
- Detailed PI's data collection pipeline: leader arms, teleoperation, cloud annotation, model training
- Presented the data scale comparison: Open X-Embodiment (3,800 hours) vs PI's 10,000+ hours in 6 months

## Notable Claims

- "What the analogous data source for the web actually is — [is] a trillion dollar question for the industry."
- "More than 50 percent of the work is getting the data pipeline right."
- VLA models need to produce "high frequency controls" — a key architectural difference from VLMs.
