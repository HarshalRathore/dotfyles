# Corey Cooper

Corey Cooper leads Developer Relations at [[entities/circle|Circle]]. At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, he presented a talk and demo on automating escrow with USDC and AI, showcasing how multimodal AI agents can parse agreements, verify deliverables, and trigger conditional stablecoin settlement via smart contracts. ^[extracted]

## Key Insights from AIEF2025

- USDC is "an order of magnitude better at escrow than traditional payment rails." ^[extracted]
- Stablecoin infrastructure is "designed for agents" — near-instant settlement, irreversibility, always-on availability, and programmability. ^[extracted]
- Human-in-the-loop is the current safest pattern for AI-driven payments; full autonomy is 5-10 years away. ^[extracted]
- [[concepts/circle-cctp|Cross-chain escrow]] enables depositing from one blockchain and paying out on another. ^[extracted]
- Circle's open-source escrow agent app is available for developers to run locally. ^[extracted]
