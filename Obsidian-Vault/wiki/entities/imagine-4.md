# Imagine 4

Imagine 4 is Google DeepMind's static image generation model. It preserves realism across a wide range of subjects — from humans to animals to objects — and maintains detail across diverse artistic styles.

## Capabilities

- **Realism preservation** — Accurate rendering of humans, animals (including whales, puppies), and objects
- **Style diversity** — Supports multiple artistic styles while preserving detail
- **Typography** — Can render text within images, demonstrated with Alamo Square and Mission district stamps
- **Artist collaboration** — Built in close collaboration with creative industry artists, including Ross Lovegrove
