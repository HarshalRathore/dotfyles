# Brian Balfour

Brian Balfour is the founder and CEO of [[entities/reforge|Reforge]], a company that helps product teams decode best practices through expert-led courses and AI agents. He has over 25 years of experience in tech startups and has been operating in highly competitive product categories.

## Background
- Helped HubSpot launch their CRM almost a decade ago — entering a category many considered too competitive ^[extracted]
- Founder and CEO of [[entities/reforge|Reforge]] for approximately 10 years as of 2025 ^[extracted]
- Built a community of over 400 experts and helped over 100,000 professionals through Reforge ^[extracted]

## AI Product Strategy

At the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Balfour presented the [[concepts/ai-lego-blocks-framework|AI Lego Blocks Framework]], a product strategy for building differentiated AI products in crowded markets. Key contributions: ^[extracted]

- Articulated the two common traps in AI product building: reinventing the AI wheel (building custom models) vs. copying basic AI features (chatbots)
- Positioned the middle path: assembling off-the-shelf AI capabilities with proprietary data and functionality
- Emphasized that competitive advantage comes from what is uniquely yours — your data, your functionality, your understanding of unmet customer needs — not the AI itself
- Showcased the [[entities/granola|Granola]] case study as an example of the framework in practice
