# Ensemble Health Partners

Ensemble Health Partners is a U.S.-based healthcare revenue cycle management (RCM) organization that works with hundreds of hospitals and health systems. The company employs approximately 14,000 people and positions itself as a leader on the quality side within the RCM industry. ^[extracted]

## Business Model

Ensemble provides end-to-end RCM as a full-service provider, meaning they support every stage of the patient financial journey — from eligibility checks and registration through documentation, medical coding, denial management, and final payment collection. This end-to-end positioning gives them a unique advantage: they can see longitudinal data across the entire process, connect the dots from beginning to end, and identify opportunities to prevent errors before they occur. ^[extracted]

## AI Strategy

Under the leadership of Head of AI Nathan Wan, Ensemble is applying AI to reduce claim denials and healthcare administrative friction. Their approach leverages their position as an end-to-end RCM provider — having visibility into the full lifecycle of claims, prior authorizations, and denials enables them to build models that predict and prevent errors at the source rather than managing appeals after the fact. ^[inferred]

A key focus area is prior authorization, where Wan identifies significant opportunity for AI to correct errors before they happen by leveraging historical data from the beginning and end of the authorization process. ^[extracted]

## Industry Context

Ensemble operates in a market where 40% of U.S. hospitals operate at a negative margin, and almost half are losing money — not from clinical costs, but from broken and manual revenue cycle processes involving delays, denials, rework, and lost revenue. ^[extracted]
