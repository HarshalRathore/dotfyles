# David Vorick

David Vorick is the founder of Glow Labs, an incentive protocol that applies Bitcoin-like mining incentives to renewable energy infrastructure. He spoke at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Background

David Vorick previously built Scalia, a cryptocurrency that he took from $10,000 to over $3 billion in market capitalization ^[extracted]. This track record in incentive design informed his approach to solar infrastructure.

## At Glow Labs

Glow Labs applies Bitcoin's incentive model to solar panel construction in developing markets. The protocol uses token rewards to motivate solar panel construction where traditional infrastructure investment is insufficient ^[extracted]. Glow raised a $30 million round from Framework and Union Square Ventures (USV) ^[extracted]. The company recorded a world record for on-chain DPIN revenue — over $10 million in a single day ^[extracted].

## Vision

"Why not tens of millions of solar panels?" — applying incentive mechanisms to solar construction in India, Mexico, Lebanon, and across the developing world ^[extracted].
