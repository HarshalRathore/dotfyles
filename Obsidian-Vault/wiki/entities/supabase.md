# Supabase

[[entities/ivan-burazin|Ivan Burazin]] cited [[entities/supabase|Supabase]] as a company doing API-first design well for AI agents. Supabase is a backend-as-a-service platform where all key agent-accessible functionality is exposed via API.

## Why Cited

In Burazin's taxonomy of AX best practices, API-first design is "the most critical" principle. Supabase is cited alongside [[entities/neon|Neon]] and [[entities/etlify|Etlify]] as companies where "all the key things that an agent needs to use their services can be accessed via an API." ^[extracted]
