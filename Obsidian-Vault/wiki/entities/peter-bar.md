# Peter Bar

Peter Bar is a product leader at [[entities/intercom-fin|Intercom Fin]], where he led the build of Finvoice — an enterprise voice AI agent for phone support that was shipped in approximately 100 days.^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

Peter presented "Shipping an Enterprise Voice AI Agent in 100 Days" at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025.^[extracted] The talk covered the product decisions, technical architecture, and lessons learned from building Finvoice from concept to production deployment on enterprise phone lines.^[extracted]

## Key Insights from Peter Bar

- Voice is the next big frontier in AI for customer service — over 80% of support teams still use phone support, and over one-third of all customer service interactions happen over the phone.^[extracted]
- The biggest challenge in building voice agents is not the model but the product: picking the right use case, designing for phone conversation realities, building trust with support teams, and integrating with existing workflows.^[extracted]
- Outcome-based pricing will converge as the market standard because it aligns provider and customer incentives better than usage-based pricing.^[extracted]
