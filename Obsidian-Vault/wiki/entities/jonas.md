# Jonas

Jonas is a co-founder of Upside, a forensic revenue attribution and intelligence platform. He spoke at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 as part of the HF0 Residency showcase.

## Background

Jonas is an engineer who previously worked at [[entities/branch|Branch]], a startup focused on mobile deep linking. He built a search engine used by over 100 million people every day at Branch ^[extracted]. He left Branch last year along with one of its founders to start Upside ^[extracted].

## At Upside

Upside uses LLMs to transform messy enterprise data into structured maps of customer interactions. Jonas described enterprises as "data hoarders" who store everything in Salesforce (a "SQL database in a trench coat") but don't know what to do with it ^[extracted]. The platform uses LLMs to extract important details from unstructured email records and build a "data command center" for sales and marketing teams ^[extracted].
