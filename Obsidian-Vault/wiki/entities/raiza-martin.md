## Raiza Martin

Raiza Martin is a product leader who previously worked at [[Google]] on [[NotebookLM]], where she served as the product manager for the first version (codenamed "Tailwind"), announced at Google I/O 2023. She later moved to [[Huxe]], an AI-powered writing tool.

She dropped out of college early in her career, then returned to attend full-time while simultaneously building NotebookLM at Google — a dual commitment she describes as giving her deep personal clarity about the product she wanted to build. She has stated that if she could have a tool where you "put a bunch of shit in it — docs, slides — and just chat with it and it does something valuable," she would build it from the ground up.

Her talk at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 articulates a framework for understanding product building in the AI era, centered on the idea that "everything is ugly" because most products were built in the pre-AI era and need to be rebuilt. She emphasizes personal clarity (vision, purpose, taste) as the foundation of forceful product building.
