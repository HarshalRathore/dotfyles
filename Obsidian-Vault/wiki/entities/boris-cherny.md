# Boris Cherny

Boris Cherny is a Member of Technical Staff at [[entities/anthropic|Anthropic]] and the creator of [[entities/claude-code|Claude Code]]. He is a well-known figure in the JavaScript ecosystem, previously known for creating ts-node and contributing extensively to Node.js tooling.

## Claude Code

Boris leads the development of Claude Code, Anthropic's agentic coding product. His design philosophy for the product emphasizes being unopinionated and starting from the terminal — giving users low-level access to the model while remaining productive. He has articulated a vision where Claude Code serves as a minimal, general-purpose interface that adapts to wherever the user works: terminals, IDEs, GitHub, or over SSH. ^[extracted]

## Talk: Evolution of Agentic Coding

At AI Engineer World's Fair 2025, Boris presented on the evolution of agentic coding, tracing the history of programming from punch cards through text editors to modern AI coding agents. He framed Claude Code's terminal-first approach as a deliberate product choice: because the model is improving exponentially, Anthropic doesn't want to lock in a specific UX that might become obsolete. ^[extracted]
