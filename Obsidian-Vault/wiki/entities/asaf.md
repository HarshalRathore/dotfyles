# Asaf (Monday.com AI)

**Asaf** is the head of AI at [[entities/monday-com|Monday.com]] and the creator of **GPT Researcher**, an open-source package for AI-powered research tasks. He is cited by [[entities/harrison-chase|Harrison Chase]] as a key influence on his thinking about enterprise agent reliability.

## Influence on Enterprise Agent Design

[[entities/harrison-chase|Harrison Chase]] says many ideas in his AIEF2025 talk ("3 ingredients for building reliable enterprise agents") were borrowed from conversations with Asaf. Chase encourages the audience to check out Asaf's upcoming blog post on the topic, which frames the ideas differently. ^[extracted]

## GPT Researcher

GPT Researcher is an open-source package for AI-powered research. It represents the kind of high-value, long-running agent that [[entities/harrison-chase|Harrison Chase]] advocates for — an agent that does substantial work (research) rather than providing quick Q&A responses. ^[inferred]
