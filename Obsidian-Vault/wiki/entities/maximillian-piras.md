# Maximillian Piras

Designer at [[entities/yutori|Yutori]] who presented "The Bitter Layout or: How I Learned to Love the Model Picker" at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025. ^[extracted]

## Key Concepts

Piras introduced several framing concepts for understanding the state of AI UX:

- [[concepts/bitter-layout]] — The converged AI UX pattern (chat + model picker) that persists because it absorbs next-model capabilities
- [[concepts/bitter-design-lesson]] — The corollary to Sutton's Bitter Lesson: if models aren't commoditized, the interface is
- [[concepts/schrodingers-chat]] — The paradox of chat being simultaneously the future and not the future
- [[concepts/model-picker-as-mode]] — The argument that model pickers are mode selectors
- [[concepts/gardening-as-design-paradigm]] — The shift from construction to gardening in AI UX

Piras draws inspiration from [[entities/brett-victor|Brett Victor]]'s "Future of Programming" talk, [[entities/larry-tesler|Larry Tesler]]'s modeless principle, and [[entities/rich-sutton|Rich Sutton]]'s Bitter Lesson. ^[extracted]
