# Adam Behrens

Adam Behrens is a philosopher-turned-engineer and founder of [[entities/new-generation|New Generation]], a company building infrastructure for agentic commerce. He has been obsessed with two questions his whole life: "What is a thing?" and "Why does it exist?" ^[extracted]

## Background

Before founding New Generation, Behrens worked at [[entities/bridgewater|Bridgewater]] on trading systems and market systems, giving him direct experience with how financial markets manage differences between buyers and sellers. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk

At AIEF2025, he delivered "Machines of Buying and Selling Grace," mapping the evolution of e-commerce from static websites to an agentic future where AI agents on both sides of transactions interact through programmatic interfaces. His core thesis: e-commerce digitized merchandise and distribution; AI digitizes the participants and their interactions. ^[extracted]
