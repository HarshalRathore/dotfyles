# Toki Sherbakov

Toki Sherbakov leads the US Solution Architecture team at [[entities/openai|OpenAI]]. He co-presented "Building Effective Voice Agents" at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 alongside [[entities/anoop-kotha|Anoop Kotha]].

## Talk Contributions

In the talk, Sherbakov:
- Described the transition from text-only to multimodal AI, focusing on the audio modality
- Demonstrated the quality jump in speech-to-speech models from ~6 months prior (slow, robotic, brittle) to current models (fast, expressive, accurate)
- Presented two emerging [[concepts/voice-agent-architecture|voice agent architectures]]: chained and speech-to-speech
- Defined the trade-off framework across latency, cost, accuracy, UX, and integrations

## Key Quotes

> "Audio is really getting to that point where it's becoming much more usable at scale."

> "I really feel like now is the time to build. You have this first mover's advantage."
