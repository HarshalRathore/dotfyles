# Kevin Madura

Kevin Madura is a consultant at [[entities/alix-partners|Alix Partners]], a global management consulting firm, where he helps companies, courts, and regulators understand new technologies like AI and LLMs. He co-presented at AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 with Mo Bhasin on how AI is reshaping knowledge work and professional services.

Madura emphasizes that Alix Partners goes beyond delivering PowerPoints — they "roll up their sleeves and solve problems," including coding and getting into the weeds of technical problems. At AIEF2025, he framed the three-phase structure of typical consulting engagements (data ingestion → analysis/hypothesis generation → recommendation/deliverable) and argued that AI is significantly compressing the first phase, transforming it from ~50% of engagement time down to 10-20%.

He introduced the concept of the "AI first firm" (coined by Dwarkesh Patel) as a future model where senior knowledge and experience is replicated through AI, creating "50 copies of the CEO," while leveraging AI to scale the work delivered below that senior tier.
