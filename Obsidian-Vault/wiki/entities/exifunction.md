# Exifunction

Exifunction was the original company that became [[entities/windsurf|Windsurf]]. ^[extracted]

## Background

Exifunction had a background in GPU optimization, which gave the team deep expertise in performance-critical computation. This background directly informed Windsurf's approach to the context retrieval problem — finding the most relevant context quickly as codebases grow. ^[extracted]

Sam Fertig noted during his AIEF2025 talk that Windsurf "excels at" context retrieval "because of our background in GPU optimization" and that they "started as a company called Exifunction." ^[extracted] The company's technical DNA in optimization carries into their philosophy of optimizing **what** goes into the context window rather than just making it bigger. ^[inferred]
