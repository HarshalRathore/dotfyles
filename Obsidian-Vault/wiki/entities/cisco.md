# Cisco

Cisco is a networking and technology company that has established an AI incubation program through its [[entities/outshift|OutShift]] group. The company is applying multi-agent AI and knowledge graph technology to solve network operations challenges, particularly change management failures in production environments. ^[extracted]

## OutShift Incubation Group

Cisco's [[entities/outshift|OutShift]] group serves as an internal incubator for emerging technologies. Its charter is to evaluate emerging technologies and determine how they can accelerate the roadmaps of Cisco's traditional business units. OutShift focuses on AI and quantum technology, including quantum networking. ^[extracted]

## AI in Network Operations

At AIEF2025, [[entities/ola-mabadeje|Ola Mabadeje]] from Cisco presented a production system that combines multi-agent AI, natural language interfaces, and network knowledge graphs built on [[entities/arango-db|ArangoDB]]. The system was developed in response to customer pain points around high failure rates during network change deployments, representing a shift from rule-based automation to AI-agent-assisted change management. ^[extracted]
