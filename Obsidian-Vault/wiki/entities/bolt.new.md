# Bolt.new

**Bolt.new** is an AI-powered web development product built by **StackBlitz** on the same core technology the company had been developing for seven years prior to launch.

## Launch and Growth

Bolt.new launched in October of the previous year as what the team expected would be their "last effort" before potentially shutting down the company. At launch:
- ARR was $0.7M (accumulated over seven years)
- Team size: fewer than 20 people
- Product was an MVP stripped down to bare essentials — no back seat, no side seat, just metal

The launch exceeded all expectations. Within 60 days, ARR grew from $0.7M to $20M — a nearly 29x increase. The growth curve was described as "clean" with no jagged edges despite the insanity of early demand. The company subsequently over-doubled that figure.

By month two, Bolt.new had approximately 30,000-40,000 active customers, creating an extreme support load that the tiny team had to manage without any dedicated support or success personnel.

## Product Philosophy

The Bolt.new product embodies the StackBlitz philosophy of shipping fast with minimal viable features — the team put online a product that was "just metal" with no extras, yet it scaled remarkably well under the team's lean structure.
