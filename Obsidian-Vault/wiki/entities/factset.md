# FactSet

## AI Initiatives

FactSet's engineering team, including [[entities/yogendra-miraje|Yogendra Miraje]], has been building planning-based agentic workflows that leverage their existing enterprise microservices as tool backends. Their approach emphasizes structured planning through a Blueprint Generator pattern, enabling agents to automate complex financial research workflows like earnings call preparation. ^[extracted]
