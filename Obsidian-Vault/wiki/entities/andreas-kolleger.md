# Andreas Kollegger

Andreas Kollegger is a speaker at [[entities/neo4j|Neo4j]] who presented at AIEF2025 alongside [[entities/stephen-chin|Stephen Chin]] on the "Top Ten Challenges to Reach AGI" talk. ^[extracted]

He also presented alongside Alison Cosette on graph analytics for RAG curation at AIEF2025, demonstrating the Connect → Cluster → Curate framework for improving retrieval quality through graph data science algorithms. ^[extracted]

## AIEF2025 Talk: Top Ten Challenges to Reach AGI

Kolleger and Chin used sci-fi movie memes as a playful framework to discuss serious AGI challenges. Kollegger started with Memento (prompt engineering as short-term memory loss) and Skynet (autonomous systems with unforeseen consequences), while Chin presented the remaining memes including The Matrix (agent simulation), Monsters Inc. (emotions and creator responsibility), Star Wars (language understanding), Star Trek (hive mind assimilation), and Deep Thought (not knowing the right questions). ^[extracted]
