OMB Memorandum M-25-22 is an April 2025 directive from the Office of Management and Budget, issued alongside [[concepts/omb-memorandum-m25-21|M-25-21]]. Together, these memoranda form the current foundation for [[concepts/ai-governance|U.S. AI governance]] policy.

While the specific provisions of M-25-22 are closely related to M-25-21 (both mandate 180-day strategy development periods and direct agencies to accelerate AI adoption), M-25-22 addresses complementary aspects of AI governance for federal agencies. The memoranda collectively:

- Require agencies to develop AI implementation strategies within 180 days
- Direct agencies to classify AI use cases by risk level
- Mandate integration of AI into mission-critical workflows beyond productivity tools
- Acknowledge the trust requirements that come with processing sensitive government data

As of the talk date (April 2025), agencies are in the rule-making period to develop their strategies. AI-specific governance frameworks are still being finalized.
