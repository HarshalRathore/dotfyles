# AI Education Summit

The AI Education Summit is a new initiative announced at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2025 by [[entities/benjamin-dunphy|Benjamin Dunphy]]. It aims to build a global community dedicated to AI education, empowering children, parents, and educators with knowledge, skills, and ethical frameworks to thrive in an AI-driven world.

## Motivation

The summit addresses a significant gap between the rapid advancement of AI and the preparedness of three key stakeholder groups: children, parents, and educators. Benjamin Dunphy frames this as both an educational challenge and a human-computer interaction (HCI) evolution question — how do we move beyond screen-and-keyboard interfaces toward more natural forms of learning with AI? ^[extracted]

## Core Thesis

Dunphy draws a parallel between the historical evolution of computers (from large, cold corporate machines to personal companions) and the future of AI education. His argument is that AI's growing capabilities should free us from the constraints of traditional classroom and lecture-style learning, potentially enabling even mediocre teachers to become world-class instructors through AI augmentation. ^[extracted]

## First Event

The inaugural AI Education Summit is a free online event featuring Stefania Druga (former Google research scientist) as a keynote speaker. Her previous talk at AIEF2024 demonstrated a student learning to code by programming "the very thing that is teaching them to code" — a recursive self-improvement loop in educational AI. ^[extracted]
