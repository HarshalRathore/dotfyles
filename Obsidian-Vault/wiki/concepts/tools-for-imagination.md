# Tools for Imagination

**Tools for imagination** is a product vision — articulated by [[entities/gamma|Gamma]] — that goes beyond content creation to help people stretch, shape, and share their ideas in ways that are dramatically easier than current tools allow. ^[extracted]

## The Vision

The goal is not just to make presentations easier to build, but to help people push innovation forward by lowering the barrier between having an idea and sharing it with others. ^[extracted]

## Relationship to Content-First Design

Tools for imagination extends [[concepts/content-first-design|content-first design]] — if content-first design removes the friction of visual formatting, tools for imagination remove the friction of idea development and communication itself.

## AI's Role

AI is uniquely positioned to enable tools for imagination because it can:

- Help users develop and refine ideas through conversation
- Generate visual representations of abstract concepts
- Adapt output format to the audience and context
- Iterate rapidly on user feedback
