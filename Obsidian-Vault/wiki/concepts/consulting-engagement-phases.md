# Consulting Engagement Phases

Kevin Madura at AIEF2025 framed typical consulting engagements as three sequential phases, each with distinct AI impact:

**Phase 1 — Data Ingestion & Normalization:** The upfront work of understanding what you have — ingesting PDFs, databases, spreadsheets, and other data sources. This phase typically consumes ~50% of engagement time. AI compresses this to 10-20%, enabling 100% corpus coverage instead of 20% sampling. The key insight: AI removes human throughput limits on data processing.

**Phase 2 — Analysis & Hypothesis Generation:** The "playbook" phase where analysts apply methodology, generate hypotheses, and derive insights from the normalized data. AI augments this phase by providing the analyst with complete data coverage and automated pattern detection, but the analytical reasoning remains human-centric.

**Phase 3 — Recommendation & Deliverable:** The output phase — what clients actually care about. The recommendation, deliverable, or solution is the reason the client hired the firm. AI's impact here is indirect but significant: better analysis (from phases 1 and 2) produces better recommendations, and freed time allows more thorough analysis and client engagement.

The critical observation is that AI doesn't just speed up phase 1 — it transforms the quality of all downstream phases by enabling complete data coverage rather than prioritized sampling. This is the difference between reviewing the top 20% of 5,000 contracts versus all 5,000 contracts.^[inferred]
