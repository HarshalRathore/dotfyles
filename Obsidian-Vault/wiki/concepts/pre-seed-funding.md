## Pre-Stage VC Investing

Pre-seed funds are the earliest institutional VC stage. They are well-versed in investing in companies that are pre-revenue and sometimes pre-product. ^[extracted]

## What Pre-Seed Investors Evaluate

Pre-seed investors back:
- The founder's judgment and abilities
- The team the founder can recruit
- The founder's unique insight into the market

Not the product, which will look dramatically different in 7–10 years. ^[extracted]

## Pre-Seed vs. Seed

Both pre-seed and seed funds operate in the same general territory: investing based on founder quality rather than metrics. The distinction between them is often about how much capital is deployed and how much traction is expected, but both are appropriate targets for pre-revenue founders. ^[inferred]

## Strategic Advice

If you are pre-revenue and pre-product, target pre-seed and seed funds. These are the funds that understand your stage and are structured to invest at it. ^[extracted]
