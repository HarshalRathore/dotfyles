# E-Commerce AI Transformation

The transformation of e-commerce through generative media represents one of the most concrete and near-term applications of AI in retail. While e-commerce is already growing at ~1% annually as a share of U.S. retail, generative media is expected to significantly accelerate this growth by adding interactivity and personalization to the online shopping experience.^[extracted]

## Current State

Multiple companies are actively redefining online shopping through AI. Virtual try-on is the clearest example and is already widely adopted across retailers and startups. The prediction is that every e-commerce platform will eventually leverage generative media capabilities.^[extracted]

## Dynamics

The e-commerce transformation follows a different pattern than advertising: e-commerce growth is happening "with or without AI," but generative media will play a "big part" in accelerating it. The visual nature of online shopping makes it naturally suited to AI interactivity, but the transformation is more incremental than the disruptive potential seen in advertising.^[extracted]
