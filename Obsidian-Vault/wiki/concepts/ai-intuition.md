# AI Intuition

AI intuition is the practical understanding of what AI coding agents are good at, what they struggle with, and how to effectively steer them — developed through hands-on experimentation rather than theoretical study. ^[extracted]

## Building AI Intuition

Harald Kirshner (Microsoft) presented at AIEF2025 that building AI intuition is one of the primary purposes of [[concepts/vibe-coding|YOLO vibe coding]]:

- **Experiment broadly** — Use a "very open canvas" to explore what AI can do
- **Discover strengths and limits** — Learn through trial what AI is good at and what it cannot do yet
- **Baseline capability** — Establish a personal baseline of AI capabilities before applying them in structured contexts
- **Informs all AI usage** — The intuition gained from YOLO vibe coding "informs all of other AI usage"

## The Role of YOLO Vibe Coding

[[concepts/yolo-structured-spectrum-vibe-coding|YOLO vibe coding]] is the primary mechanism for building AI intuition because it removes barriers (auto-approve, no code review, free iteration) and lets developers focus purely on outcomes. This creates a low-friction environment for rapid experimentation. ^[extracted]

## Practical Implications

- AI intuition is a skill, not just knowledge — it requires hands-on practice
- The "best" model for coding may differ from the "best" model for design (see [[concepts/ai-design-sense|AI Design Sense]])
- Understanding model design sense through parallel experimentation (different models, different design systems) sharpens intuition
 - AI intuition transfers across domains — learning Rust through AI helps understand AI's capabilities regardless of the target language

 ## Specificity as a Steering Technique

 [[entities/robert-brennan|Robert Brennan]] (OpenHands) notes that being specific when communicating with agents is not just about getting the right result — it also makes the agent faster. Telling the agent exactly which files, functions, frameworks, and strategies to use reduces exploration time and produces a more focused execution path. This is a practical technique for steering agents that develops with experience. ^[extracted]
