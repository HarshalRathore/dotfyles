# S-Curve Technology

**S-curve technology** is the observation that **all technology exponentials eventually flatten into S-curves** — but humanity has always responded by developing new basic technologies to start the next exponential. ^[extracted]

Presented at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2026. ^[extracted]

## The Pattern

Throughout history, technology has followed S-curves: ^[extracted]

1. **Rapid growth** — exponential improvement
2. **Flattening** — the exponential flattens as the technology matures
3. **New technology** — a new basic technology is developed, starting a new exponential

## Historical Examples

| Era | Technology | Response to Flattening |
|-----|-----------|----------------------|
| Early humanity | Hunting, farming | Developed new basic technologies |
| Early enlightenment | Scientific method | Industrial revolution |
| Industrial era | Manufacturing, energy | Information technology |
| Information era | Computing, internet | AI |

## Mark Andreessen's Techno-Optimism

This pattern aligns with **Mark Andreessen's techno-optimism** — the belief that technology will continue to solve problems, even as individual technologies reach their limits. ^[inferred]

## Connection to AI

AI is the latest exponential in this sequence. The question is not whether AI will flatten (it will), but what new basic technology will emerge in response. ^[inferred]
