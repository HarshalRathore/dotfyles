# Sovereign AI

**Sovereign AI** is the thesis that local models enable full pipeline control — from data privacy to cost reduction to infrastructure independence. It is presented as a strategic imperative for organizations that want control over their AI infrastructure. ^[extracted]

Presented at the AI Engineer World's Fair 2026. ^[extracted]

## The Coinbase Example

The CEO of **Coinbase** shared how their company managed to reduce AI spend significantly by moving to local models. This was shared in a tweet just two days before the talk. ^[extracted]

## The Three Eras of Software

Sovereign AI fits into a broader framework of three eras of software: ^[extracted]

1. **Machine-limited** — software constrained by available computing power
2. **Human-brain-limited** — software constrained by human cognitive capacity
3. **AI frontier** — software constrained by what AI can do

In the AI frontier era, sovereignty over the AI pipeline becomes as important as sovereignty over the compute pipeline. ^[inferred]

## Local Models as the Foundation

Sovereign AI is enabled by **local models** — models that run on consumer hardware without cloud dependency. By early 2025, models as small as 24B parameters achieved GPT-4-class capabilities, making local inference practical for real work. ^[extracted]
