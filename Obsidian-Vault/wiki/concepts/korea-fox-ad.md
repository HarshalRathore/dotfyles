# Korea Fox Ad

The Korea Fox Ad is Diego Rodriguez's third framing metaphor from his AIEF2025 talk, illustrating the extraordinary speed of AI-driven creative workflows. ^[extracted]

## The Story

A studio used Rodriguez's platform (CREA) to go from sign-up to conversion to payment to broadcast in two days. This velocity — traditionally requiring weeks or months of creative production — was compressed into a single day using AI tools. ^[extracted]

## Significance

The example demonstrates two things:

1. **Adoption velocity** — AI creative tools can be adopted and deployed at unprecedented speed. The friction between idea and execution is collapsing.
2. **Creative empowerment** — The tool enables studios to express themselves more quickly, which is Rodriguez's stated mission for CREA. ^[extracted]

## The Three Stories

Rodriguez opens his talk with three stories:
1. **Cars vs Traffic** — predictable vs emergent behavior
2. **Tower of Babel** — language barriers and AI as bridge
3. **Korea Fox Ad** — speed of AI adoption

The Korea Fox Ad is the most concrete and commercial of the three — it shows what is already possible rather than what might be possible. ^[inferred]
