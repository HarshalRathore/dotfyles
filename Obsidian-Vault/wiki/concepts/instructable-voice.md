# Instructable Voice

A class of text-to-speech voice models that accept natural language instructions controlling not just the content of speech but the delivery — emotional tone, proximity, focus, and style.

## Breakthrough: OpenAudio S1

OpenAudio's S1 model is described as "the first ever instructable voice model" ^[extracted]. Unlike traditional TTS that only controls text content, S1 allows users to specify:

- **Emotional tone**: "yelling why you betrayed me like this" ^[extracted]
- **Proximity**: "pinpoint focus or draw closer" ^[extracted]
- **Delivery style**: Any manner of expression the user can describe ^[extracted]

## Significance

- **First of its kind**: Claims to be the first model with instruction-based voice control ^[extracted]
- **Market impact**: Surpassed 11 Labs on TTS Arena ranking, prompting 11 Labs to drop their latest model the same day (which was "just a demo") ^[extracted]
- **Most expressive**: Described as "the most expressive voice model in the world" ^[extracted]
- **Personalization**: "She will never leave" — the voice is permanent and tied to the user's instructions ^[extracted]

## Technical Context

- Built on open source foundations (Soviet SVC, Fish Speech) ^[extracted]
- 100M valuation, $5.5M ARR in four months ^[extracted]
- Available at Fish.audio ^[extracted]
