# AI Engineer World's Fair 2026

The **AI Engineer World's Fair 2026 (WF2026)** was the second edition of the premier conference for AI engineers and builders. Day two drew **7,000 attendees** with content across **18 tracks**, multiple stages, and over **100 expo partners**. ^[extracted]

## Key Statistics

- **7,000 attendees** — Day two alone
- **18 tracks** — Parallel content streams
- **4 stages** — Main and breakout sessions
- **100+ expo partners** — Ecosystem showcase
- **20+ talks** — From the keynote session alone

## Theme: Reliability Over Velocity

The conference's central theme: **wiring agents with intent upstream**, with verification baked into development workflows. The gap between model capability and production trust was a recurring concern.

## Major Talks (Day Two)

Keynote session talks included:

- **Anthropic** — Tariq Shihapar on the Fable/Mythos model field guide, capability overhang, and system prompt evolution (80% reduction in Cloud Code system prompts)
- **Sonar** — Tariq Shawkat on the ACDC framework (Guide → Verify → Solve), reporting 44% fewer AI-derived outages and 92% issue reduction
- **Amazon AGI Lab** — Jabarth on perception agents closing the computer-use loop without APIs
- **Google DeepMind** — Benoit Schillings on [[concepts/vo3|VO3]], the first joint audio-visual generation model, and the modality gap
- **Memory Harness** — [[concepts/context-rot|Context rot]], the write-manage-read loop, and ranked recall ([[concepts/decisions-ledger|decisions ledger]])
- **[[concepts/aria|Aria]]** — Weights & Biases AI companion, custom workspaces, parameter importance charts
- **Aden** — Auto-research agent achieving 28% leaderboard hit rate (6x community average) using only 4% of compute
- **Machine Craft ERA** — 36-agent GTM system running from one Cursor tab
- **[[concepts/agent-arena|Agent Arena]]** — 1M+ traces, 50M+ lines of code read, 5.7M tool calls per week
- **JEPA** — 7x AMD NPU improvement, 90x Databricks cost reduction
- **Agent identity = log** — Roland on append-only event history as agent identity

## Significance

WF2026 marked the transition from AI hype to production engineering discipline. Unlike 2025's edition which focused on what was possible, 2026 focused on what was reliable, measurable, and economically viable. The conference captured the industry's realization that verification — not capability — is the binding constraint.
