# Anti-PowerPoint

**Anti-PowerPoint** is a product positioning and design philosophy — articulated by [[entities/gamma|Gamma]] — that inverts the traditional presentation creation model. Instead of starting with a blank canvas and designing around content, users start with content and let the tool handle visual presentation. ^[extracted]

## The Problem

The traditional approach (exemplified by PowerPoint) forces users to confront the "blank slide" problem — staring at an empty template and spending disproportionate time on formatting boxes, aligning layouts, and resizing elements. ^[extracted] This is a **design-first** approach where the visual structure dominates the creative process.

## The Inversion

Gamma positions itself as the anti-PowerPoint by making content creation dead simple and letting AI handle the design mechanics. ^[extracted] Users focus on their ideas; the tool handles visual presentation automatically. This is the practical implementation of [[concepts/content-first-design|content-first design]].

## Relationship to AI

AI-powered tools are uniquely positioned to implement anti-PowerPoint because they can:

- Automatically generate layouts based on content type
- Suggest visual hierarchies without user input
- Adapt designs to different audiences and contexts
- Maintain consistent professional quality without design expertise
