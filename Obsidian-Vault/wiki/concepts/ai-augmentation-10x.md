# AI Augmentation 10X

The principle that AI tools make a 10X engineer into a 100X engineer, not that tools fill gaps in weaker hires.^[extracted]

## The Philosophy

When thinking about hiring and tool use, the framing matters:

- **Correct framing:** Tools allow a 10Xer to become a 100Xer.^[extracted] High-skill people with AI tools produce exponential output.
- **Incorrect framing:** Using tools to fill gaps and augment the shortcomings of someone who doesn't meet the hiring standard.^[extracted]

This means AI augmentation is a multiplier on existing talent, not a substitute for it.^[inferred]

## Practical Application

Olive uses a slew of products for day-to-day task automation across:

- Script writing
- Campaign analysis
- Operations
- Code generation
- Communications^[extracted]

The result: "by paying for a bunch of services, we have augmented and enabled everyone to have their own chief of staff within the company."^[extracted]

## Relationship to Tiny Teams

AI augmentation is the primary enabler of the tiny teams model — it is what allows a team of four to generate $6M ARR.^[extracted] Without AI tooling, the Olive playbook would not be feasible.^[inferred]
