# Automation in ML Pipelines

Automation in ML pipelines refers to the extensive tooling and automation required to manage complex model development workflows. In [[concepts/360brew|360Brew]], building automation into the pipeline was identified as a key lesson for making large model development tractable. ^[extracted]

## Why It Matters

The 360Brew brewing pipeline involves many complex stages — upcycling, continuous pre-training, fine-tuning, instruction fine-tuning, alignment, and distillation. Without automation, managing these stages would be intractable at scale. Automation makes the pipeline smooth, manageable, and repeatable. ^[extracted]
