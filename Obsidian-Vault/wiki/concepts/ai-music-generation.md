## AI Music Generation for Books

AI-generated music designed to accompany book reading, where the music matches the emotional tone and setting of each scene. The system uses mood detection and theme prompts to guide generation.

### How It Works

The process starts with an overall music theme for the book. For example, for Sherlock Holmes: "Victoria London, somber, classical." The AI then generates music that fits this theme and adjusts it per scene based on [[concepts/books-reimagined|mood detection]] from scene analysis.

### Why It Matters

Music "influences emotions hugely" in reading. When background music "flows with the book," it creates an experience similar to watching a movie. The emotional resonance of music matching the narrative is something humans have always valued in storytelling — AI just makes it feasible at scale.

### Quality Control

AI-generated music requires human curation. The AI cannot determine if the generated music is "good" — that judgment requires human taste. This is an example of [[concepts/ai-hide-from-user|hiding AI from users]]: the AI generates the draft, a human curates the final product.
