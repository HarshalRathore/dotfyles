# Virtual Try-On

Virtual try-on is the use of generative AI to allow online shoppers to visualize how products (clothing, accessories, cosmetics) would look on them before purchasing. It is described as one of the clearest product-market fits in the generative media space as of 2025.^[extracted]

## Product-Market Fit Status

Virtual try-on has been commercially active for approximately one to two years as of the talk. Multiple retailers and e-commerce websites have adopted the technology, and numerous startups have been built specifically around it. The prediction is that every retailer and e-commerce website will eventually become a generative media user, with virtual try-on as the entry point.^[extracted]

## Why It Works

The fit between virtual try-on and e-commerce is structural:

- **Visual nature of shopping**: Online shopping is inherently visual, making it naturally suited to AI-generated visual enhancements.
- **Clear value proposition**: Reducing purchase uncertainty directly impacts conversion rates and return rates.
- **Scalable personalization**: Each user gets a personalized experience without proportional cost increase.^[extracted]

## Market Position

While e-commerce itself is growing at a modest ~1% annually as a share of U.S. retail, generative media is expected to play a significant role in accelerating that growth by adding interactivity to the online shopping experience. Virtual try-on represents the earliest and clearest example of this dynamic.^[extracted]
