## The Stage Spectrum

Not all VC funds are the same. There are different types of VC funds with different investment criteria:

- **Pre-seed funds**: Invest in companies before revenue or product. Back the founder, team, and vision.
- **Seed funds**: Similar to pre-seed but sometimes with slightly more traction expected. Still comfortable with pre-revenue investments.
- **Multi-stage funds**: Can invest across stages but may have different criteria at each stage.
- **Later-stage funds**: Look for ARR growth or ARR full stop. Require demonstrated traction. ^[extracted]

## Knowing Your Target

If you know the types of funds you are looking for, you can target the right ones. ^[extracted] Pre-seed and seed funds are well-versed in investing in companies pre-revenue and sometimes pre-product. ^[extracted] Later-stage funds are not the right target at early stages. ^[inferred]

## Strategic Implication

Founders should identify which fund stages align with their current stage and focus their outreach there. Approaching later-stage funds before you have ARR is a waste of time for both parties. ^[inferred]
