# AI Adoption Acceleration

**AI adoption acceleration** describes the phenomenon where AI adoption rates have surged far beyond prior predictions, compressing timelines that analysts expected to span decades into a matter of years.

## Adoption Statistics

Rita Kozlov (Cloudflare) at AIEF2025 highlighted the acceleration:

- **One year ago**: 44% of developers used AI daily. Gartner predicted 50% of knowledge workers would use AI by 2030.
- **Today**: Over 75% of knowledge workers use AI to augment their work — **surpassing the 2030 estimate**.
- **Developers**: More than 76% use AI in their development process, and the number continues to grow.

## The Three Phases of AI

Rita frames the evolution of AI in three accelerating phases:

1. **Training** — Building and training models (the initial focus)
2. **Inference** — Using trained models to generate outputs (the current dominant phase)
3. **Automation** — Agents that execute end-to-end tasks (the emerging phase)

The shift from training to inference is already well underway. OpenAI's O1 model and DeepSeek's optimized training both demonstrate more energy being spent on inference than training. The next shift — from inference to automation — is where the most significant impact is expected.

## Internet Infrastructure Context

Rita opened her talk by noting that about 20% of Internet traffic flows through Cloudflare. If you've ordered an Uber recently or ordered food, you've used Cloudflare. This infrastructure layer provides the foundation for agent-scale workloads: CDN, DNS, DDoS protection, functions, storage, compute, and AI inference at global scale.
