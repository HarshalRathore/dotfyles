# AIEF2025 - Building Metrics that actually work

David Karam from Pi Labs (fmr Google Search) presented a full workshop at AIEF2025 on building evaluation metrics that actually work for AI systems.

## Key Themes

- **Evals consume 80% of feature development time** in AI projects
- **Evals are custom and subjective** — no copy-paste solutions, everything built from scratch
- **Two components: synthetic data and metrics** — distinct concerns in evaluation
- **Automated evals are hard** — LLMs are difficult at evaluating their own outputs
- **Methodology over tooling** — the evaluation process matters more than the platform
